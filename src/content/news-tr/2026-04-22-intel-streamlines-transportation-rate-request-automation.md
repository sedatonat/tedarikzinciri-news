<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Intel Corp.</strong>&rsquo;ın <strong>Global Logistics Org</strong> (<strong>GLO</strong>) ve <strong>Corporate Strategic Procurement</strong> (<strong>CSP</strong>) birimleri; teknoloji devinin taşımacılık taşıyıcı sözleşmelerini ve rate&rsquo;lerini birlikte yönetmekte. Carrier rate request sürecinden engellenmekte olunduklarını bulmakta &mdash; yeni bir rate request için gereken bilgileri toplamaktan harici teklif aracına veri yüklemekte kadar muazzam insan katılımı gerektirmekte. Benzer şekilde; nihai; sözleşmeli rate&rsquo;ler &mdash; tüm taşımacılık modları arasında &mdash; <strong>Intel</strong>&rsquo;in iç sistemlerine toplamak; işlemek ve yüklemek hantal olmakta. Bu sponsorlu makalede; <strong>Intel</strong>&rsquo;in nasıl <strong>AI</strong>/otomasyon ile bu süreci radikal olarak akıcılaştırmakta olunduğu anlatılmakta. Tedarik zinciri perspektifinden bakıldığında <strong>Intel Corporation</strong>; <strong>Santa Clara California</strong> kökenli; 1968&rsquo;de <strong>Gordon Moore</strong>; <strong>Robert Noyce</strong> ve <strong>Andy Grove</strong> tarafından kurulmakta &mdash; küresel olarak en büyük yarı iletken üreticilerinden biri olmakta. <strong>Lip-Bu Tan</strong>; Mart 2025&rsquo;ten beri <strong>Intel</strong> CEO&rsquo;su olmakta &mdash; <strong>Pat Gelsinger</strong>&rsquo;in Aralık 2024&rsquo;te ayrılmakta olunmasından sonra. <strong>Intel</strong>; küresel olarak <strong>Hillsboro Oregon</strong>; <strong>Chandler Arizona</strong>; <strong>Rio Rancho New Mexico</strong>; <strong>Columbus Ohio</strong> (<strong>New Albany</strong>); <strong>Leixlip Ireland</strong>; <strong>Magdeburg Germany</strong>; <strong>Wroclaw Poland</strong>; <strong>Penang Malaysia</strong>; <strong>Kulim Malaysia</strong>; <strong>Kiryat Gat Israel</strong>; <strong>Chengdu China</strong>; ana fab ve assembly tesisleri olmakta.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Tedarik zinciri perspektifinden bakıldığında küresel yarı iletken tedarik zinciri; <strong>fab</strong> (<strong>front-end manufacturing</strong>; <strong>wafer fabrication</strong>); <strong>OSAT</strong> (<strong>Outsourced Semiconductor Assembly and Test</strong>; <strong>back-end</strong>); <strong>EDA</strong> (<strong>Electronic Design Automation</strong>; <strong>Synopsys</strong>; <strong>Cadence</strong>; <strong>Siemens EDA</strong>); <strong>IP</strong> (<strong>Arm</strong>; <strong>Imagination</strong>; <strong>RISC-V Foundation</strong>); <strong>foundry</strong> (<strong>TSMC</strong>; <strong>Samsung Foundry</strong>; <strong>Intel Foundry Services</strong>; <strong>GlobalFoundries</strong>; <strong>SMIC</strong>; <strong>UMC</strong>; <strong>Powerchip/Tower</strong>); <strong>fabless</strong> (<strong>AMD</strong>; <strong>Nvidia</strong>; <strong>Qualcomm</strong>; <strong>Broadcom</strong>; <strong>MediaTek</strong>; <strong>Marvell</strong>; <strong>Apple Silicon</strong>); <strong>IDM</strong> (<strong>Integrated Device Manufacturer</strong>; <strong>Intel</strong>; <strong>Samsung</strong>; <strong>SK Hynix</strong>; <strong>Micron</strong>; <strong>Texas Instruments</strong>; <strong>STMicroelectronics</strong>; <strong>Infineon</strong>; <strong>NXP</strong>; <strong>Renesas</strong>; <strong>Microchip</strong>; <strong>Analog Devices</strong>); ana iş modelleri olmakta. <strong>EUV</strong> (<strong>Extreme Ultraviolet Lithography</strong>; <strong>ASML</strong> tekel); <strong>DUV</strong> (<strong>Deep Ultraviolet</strong>); <strong>High-NA EUV</strong>; <strong>multi-patterning</strong>; <strong>FinFET</strong>; <strong>GAAFET</strong> (<strong>Gate-All-Around</strong>); <strong>RibbonFET</strong> (<strong>Intel</strong> markası); <strong>nanosheet transistor</strong>; <strong>3D NAND</strong>; ana ileri proses teknolojileri olmakta.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Tedarik zinciri perspektifinden bakıldığında taşımacılık procurement süreçleri; <strong>RFI</strong> (<strong>Request for Information</strong>); <strong>RFQ</strong> (<strong>Request for Quotation</strong>); <strong>RFP</strong> (<strong>Request for Proposal</strong>); <strong>e-RFx</strong>; <strong>reverse auction</strong>; <strong>combinatorial optimization</strong>; ana ihale formatları olmakta. <strong>SCAC</strong> (<strong>Standard Carrier Alpha Code</strong>); <strong>USDOT</strong> number; <strong>MC number</strong> (<strong>Motor Carrier</strong>); <strong>NMFTA</strong> (<strong>National Motor Freight Traffic Association</strong>); <strong>NMFC</strong> (<strong>National Motor Freight Classification</strong>); <strong>FAK</strong> (<strong>Freight All Kinds</strong>); ana <strong>U.S. trucking</strong> tanımlayıcıları olmakta. <strong>FAK</strong>; <strong>FCL</strong>/<strong>LCL</strong>; <strong>full truckload</strong>/<strong>less-than-truckload</strong>; <strong>parcel</strong>; <strong>air freight</strong>; <strong>ocean freight</strong>; <strong>rail intermodal</strong>; ana taşımacılık modları olmakta. <strong>tender</strong>; <strong>spot</strong>; <strong>contract</strong>; <strong>dedicated</strong>; <strong>fuel surcharge</strong>; <strong>accessorial</strong>; <strong>detention</strong>; <strong>demurrage</strong>; <strong>chassis fee</strong>; <strong>peak season surcharge</strong>; <strong>congestion surcharge</strong>; <strong>BAF</strong>; <strong>CAF</strong>; <strong>ana fiyatlandırma bileşenleri</strong> olmakta. <strong>Coupa</strong>; <strong>SAP Ariba</strong>; <strong>Ivalua</strong>; <strong>JAGGAER</strong>; <strong>GEP</strong>; <strong>Zycus</strong>; <strong>Workday Strategic Sourcing</strong> (<strong>Scout RFP</strong>); <strong>Keelvar</strong>; <strong>Globality</strong>; <strong>Fairmarkit</strong>; <strong>Procurement Sciences</strong>; ana <strong>e-sourcing</strong>/<strong>spend management</strong> platformları olmakta.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Tedarik zinciri perspektifinden bakıldığında <strong>AI</strong>/otomasyon dahili süreçler; <strong>RPA</strong> (<strong>Robotic Process Automation</strong>; <strong>UiPath</strong>; <strong>Automation Anywhere</strong>; <strong>Microsoft Power Automate</strong>; <strong>Blue Prism</strong>); <strong>IDP</strong> (<strong>Intelligent Document Processing</strong>; <strong>ABBYY</strong>; <strong>Hyperscience</strong>; <strong>Rossum</strong>; <strong>Eigen Technologies</strong>); <strong>workflow orchestration</strong>; <strong>low-code/no-code</strong> platformlar (<strong>Microsoft Power Apps</strong>; <strong>Mendix</strong>; <strong>OutSystems</strong>; <strong>Appian</strong>; <strong>Pega</strong>; <strong>ServiceNow App Engine</strong>); ana otomasyon kategorileri olmakta. <strong>generative AI</strong> uygulamaları; sözleşme oluşturma/inceleme (<strong>Spellbook</strong>; <strong>Harvey AI</strong>; <strong>Ironclad AI</strong>); fiyat analizi/forecasting (<strong>Coupa Sourcing Optimization</strong>; <strong>Keelvar AutoBid</strong>); tedarikçi keşfi (<strong>Scoutbee</strong>; <strong>Tealbook</strong>; <strong>Veridion</strong>); risk skoru (<strong>Resilinc</strong>; <strong>Riskmethods</strong>; <strong>Interos</strong>; <strong>Everstream Analytics</strong>; <strong>Achilles</strong>); ana güncel uygulama alanları olmakta. <strong>spend cube</strong>; <strong>category management</strong>; <strong>strategic sourcing</strong>; <strong>tail spend</strong>; <strong>maverick spend</strong>; <strong>contract lifecycle management</strong> (<strong>CLM</strong>); modern procurement disiplinlerinin temel kavramları olmakta. Sonuç olarak <strong>Intel</strong>&rsquo;in rate request otomasyon başarısı; küresel teknoloji devlerinin <strong>AI</strong>-tabanlı procurement transformasyonunun pragmatik gerçekleştirme örneği olmakta.</p>
