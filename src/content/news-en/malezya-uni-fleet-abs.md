<p>Malaysia-based <strong>UNI-FLEET SDN BHD</strong>, a specialist tanker operator focused on ammonia transport in Asia, has decided to implement <strong>ABS Wavesight™</strong>'s <strong>Nautical Systems (NS) Maintenance Manager</strong> and <strong>Purchasing Manager</strong> modules to enhance its maintenance and inventory processes.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>ABS Wavesight™</strong>, a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) company affiliated with the <strong>American Bureau of Shipping (ABS)</strong>, has welcomed the partnership. <strong>Staci Satterwhite</strong>, CEO of the company, stated: "We are excited to work with UNI-FLEET. The integration of NS Maintenance Manager and NS Purchasing Manager will equip UNI-FLEET with the tools necessary to automate maintenance processes, efficiently manage inventory, and achieve operational excellence."</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>NS Maintenance Manager</strong> provides a robust integrated database that consolidates critical maintenance and procurement information, streamlining scheduling, monitoring, and forecasting processes. This approach aims to reduce costs and increase operational uptime.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>NS Purchasing Manager</strong> seeks to transform purchasing and inventory control activities such as competitive bidding, contracting, and inventory tracking. By integrating these activities with maintenance operations, the module enables unified process management.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>Mohd Iylia Tan</strong>, General Manager of UNI-FLEET SDN BHD, stated: "The implementation of ABS Wavesight's Nautical Systems solutions marks an important step in advancing our maintenance and purchasing operations. We believe this technology will streamline our processes and enhance our operational efficiency."</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>ABS Wavesight™</strong> is a global leader in decarbonizing maritime operations through digitalization. Through its <strong>My Digital Fleet™</strong> and <strong>Nautical Systems™</strong> platforms, the company provides innovative fleet management software to maritime customers to enhance the reliability and performance of their shipping operations. ABS Wavesight's portfolio comprises best-in-class proprietary technology and third-party integrations that provide unique insights into every aspect of a fleet's operations.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>ABS Wavesight™</strong> is engaging in various partnerships to accelerate digital transformation in the maritime industry. For example, its partnership with <strong>Marlink</strong> aims to accelerate digital transformation for maritime customers.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Additionally, <strong>ABS Wavesight™</strong> has formed an alliance with <strong>ActZero</strong> to provide cybersecurity solutions. This collaboration aims to deliver cybersecurity solutions to global fleets.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>ABS Wavesight™</strong> has announced new updates to its fleet management software <strong>Nautical Systems</strong>. These updates deliver deep efficiency gains in core fleet operations, facilitating easier management of maintenance tasks and providing more effective procurement processes.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Furthermore, <strong>ABS Wavesight™</strong>'s <strong>eLogs™</strong> software has been officially approved by the <strong>U.S. Coast Guard (USCG)</strong> for U.S. flag vessels. This approval demonstrates that the eLogs software meets all USCG requirements.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>ABS Wavesight™</strong> continues to lead the maritime industry in digitalization and operational efficiency.
