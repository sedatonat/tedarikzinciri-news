<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DHL Supply Chain</strong> is launching a <strong>multi-customer national reverse logistics network</strong> across North America, making returns processes more efficient and sustainable. This move represents the logistics giant's most comprehensive restructuring in the <strong>reverse logistics</strong> space following its acquisition of <strong>Inmar Supply Chain Solutions</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Strengthened New Structure with Inmar Acquisition</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">DHL Supply Chain acquired <strong>Inmar Supply Chain Solutions</strong>, a division of <strong>Inmar Intelligence</strong>, in <strong>January 2025</strong>. This acquisition brought the company <strong>14 returns centers</strong> and approximately <strong>800 employees</strong>. As a result, DHL elevated itself to the position of <strong>largest reverse logistics provider in North America</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The newly announced <strong>"DHL ReTurn Network"</strong> is built on the foundation of this acquisition. The company expanded its operations centered in <strong>Westerville, Ohio</strong>, combining Inmar's process expertise with <strong>DHL's operational excellence and advanced technological infrastructure</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Kraig Foreman</strong>, President of E-Commerce at DHL Supply Chain North America, stated: "The launch of DHL ReTurn Network marks a major milestone for DHL. After integrating Inmar into our operations, we systematically integrated our operations and aimed to reduce complexity in returns management."</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Next-Generation Reverse Logistics Infrastructure</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DHL ReTurn Network</strong> is designed to manage <strong>returns processes for multiple businesses</strong>, not just a single customer. The network can process various product types under one roof through <strong>11 dedicated facilities</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">These facilities work in integration with <strong>returns initiators</strong>, <strong>drop-off hosts</strong>, and <strong>direct mail returns</strong>. This enables businesses to establish a complete <strong>reverse logistics operation</strong> <strong>without additional infrastructure investment</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Thomas Borders</strong>, Vice President of Reverse Logistics at DHL Supply Chain North America, summarizes the system's advantages as follows:</p>
<p><br>
"Our national-scale shared facility network reduces <strong>storage, labor, and transportation costs</strong>. We also optimize the processing and remarketing of returned products, providing customers with <strong>flexibility and operational visibility</strong>."</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Remarketing Power with Arrive Recommerce Technology</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Along with the announcement of the new network, DHL disclosed that it has acquired <strong>technology rights from brand-focused recommerce provider Arrive Recommerce</strong>. This move expands DHL's <strong>remarketing</strong> solutions, enabling customers to reintegrate returned products into the value chain.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In doing so, DHL provides customers not only with returns management but also <strong>resale and sustainable value recovery</strong> services. This approach helps brands develop a logistics infrastructure aligned with <strong>circular economy</strong> goals.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Global Approach with Europe Innovation Center</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">DHL also opened its new <strong>Europe Innovation Center</strong> in <strong>Troisdorf, Germany</strong> this week. The <strong>58,000 square-meter</strong> new facility, located close to <strong>headquarters in Bonn</strong>, takes a central place in the company's innovation strategy.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The center is designed to test real-world applications of <strong>artificial intelligence (AI)</strong>, <strong>robotics</strong>, <strong>Internet of Things (IoT)</strong>, and <strong>sustainability-focused technologies</strong>. DHL noted that the center will serve as a <strong>joint innovation development platform with customers, startups, academic institutions, and industry partners</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Conclusion: Integrated Transformation in Reverse Logistics</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">DHL's <strong>Inmar integration</strong> and <strong>ReTurn Network launch</strong> transform returns processes from being a "side operation" to a strategic value-creation area in supply chain management.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This move responds to rising returns volumes in <strong>e-commerce and retail sectors</strong> while striking a strong balance between <strong>sustainability</strong>, <strong>cost optimization</strong>, and <strong>technological integration</strong>. DHL is thus positioning itself not merely as a logistics provider but as a <strong>leader in circular supply chain solutions</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Highlights:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DHL Supply Chain</strong> acquired <strong>Inmar Supply Chain Solutions</strong>, adding 14 returns centers and 800 employees to its operations.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The new <strong>DHL ReTurn Network</strong> serves as a <strong>multi-customer reverse logistics network</strong> across North America.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The network integrates <strong>returns initiators, drop-off points, and direct mail returns</strong> across <strong>11 dedicated facilities</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The system reduces <strong>storage, labor, and transportation costs</strong>, offering businesses <strong>flexibility</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">With the acquisition of <strong>Arrive Recommerce</strong> technology, <strong>remarketing solutions</strong> have been expanded.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>new innovation center in Troisdorf, Germany</strong> supports AI, IoT, and sustainability-focused logistics technologies.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">DHL is strengthening its vision of <strong>integrated, sustainable, and technology-driven</strong> leadership in reverse logistics.</p></li>
</ul>
