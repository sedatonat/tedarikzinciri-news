<p><a href="https://www.und.org.tr/"><u>UND</u></a> has unveiled a new publication titled "The Carrier's Handbook," a legal guide tailored to the transportation sector. Developed over a decade of accumulated expertise by lawyers specializing in the field, this comprehensive work addresses legal issues encountered in the transportation industry. The book serves as an important reference resource for companies and employees in the sector. The launch event, held in Istanbul on March 25, was attended by the UND Management Board Chairman, the authors, and representatives of sponsoring firms. The publication offers practical information on legal matters relating to transportation, including contracts, guidelines, and procedural documents.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Additionally, it includes information notes on areas such as CMR & TTK, the Customs and Smuggling Law, and the Labor Law. The authors have chosen to explain these topics in a practical and straightforward manner rather than through purely academic discourse.</p>
<h5>Key Points</h5>
<ul>
  <li><p>The book provides a comprehensive resource on legal matters in the transportation sector.</p></li>
  <li><p>The UND Management Board Chairman and the book's authors have prepared content focused on the sector's needs.</p></li>
  <li><p>The launch featured book sponsors and leading figures in the transportation industry.</p></li>
  <li><p>The book covers important topics including CMR & TTK, the Customs and Smuggling Law, and the Labor Law.</p></li>
  <li><p>It is filled with practical applications and real-world examples.</p></li>
</ul>
