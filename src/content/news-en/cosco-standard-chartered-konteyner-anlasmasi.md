<p>COSCO SHIPPING Development and Standard Chartered Bank (China) Limited have signed a framework agreement to modernize the container industry supply chain. The collaboration will strengthen communication between the two organizations by increasing continuous information sharing at national, regional, and global levels. It will also deepen cooperation on transition financing, liquidity and risk management, and contribute to promoting green and sustainable development projects. This initiative aims to support high-quality improvements in environmental, social and governance (ESG) standards across the industry.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>News regarding COSCO SHIPPING's 20,000 TEU vessel calling at La Spezia is also drawing attention as part of these developments. The effort is being described as the world's first financial project dedicated to the transformation of the entire container industry supply chain. The project aims to integrate "transformation financing" solutions by evaluating the daily operations of business segments such as container manufacturing and leasing, and to support and accelerate the green and low-carbon transition process for each node in the industrial chain.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Points:</h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>COSCO SHIPPING Development and Standard Chartered Bank (China) are collaborating to modernize the container industry supply chain.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The agreement aims to increase continuous information sharing between the two organizations at national, regional, and global levels.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The collaboration will be strengthened in areas such as transition financing, liquidity, and risk management.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Focus will be placed on promoting green and sustainable development projects.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The project aims to identify pathways for the green and low-carbon transformation of the container industry.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.porttechnology.org/news/cosco-shipping-to-develop-container-industry-chain/"><u>https://www.porttechnology.org/news/cosco-shipping-to-develop-container-industry-chain/</u></a></p>
