<p style="text-align: justify">A critical milestone has been achieved in the development of the International North-South Transport Corridor (<strong>INSTC</strong>). The first regular train service from Russia has successfully reached <strong>Aprin dry port</strong> near Iran's capital city of Tehran. This service is considered one of the most tangible indicators that the route is entering its operational phase.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">According to officials, the new regular service is reducing transit times on the Russia-Iran connection to <strong>under two weeks</strong>, thereby enhancing the corridor's competitive advantage. This speed enables more effective integration between Russia's Caspian transit multimodal routes and Iran's north-south logistics network.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A Strategic Milestone for INSTC</strong></h4>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The INSTC is designed as a multimodal transport corridor connecting Russia → Caspian region → Iran → Gulf countries → India. The objective is to achieve the following in trade between Europe and Central Asia to South Asia:</p>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">reduce distances,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">lower costs,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">reduce dependence on the Suez Canal.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Within this framework, Aprin Dry Port holds significant importance for the corridor due to its role as Iran's <strong>central intermodal transfer hub</strong>. Its proximity to Tehran facilitates connections between the railway entry points in the north, such as Astara and Enzeli, and southern ports like Bandar Abbas.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Aprin Dry Port: Iran's Logistics Backbone</strong></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Aprin is a modern <strong>dry port and logistics center</strong> managed by PTB Group, handling a substantial portion of Iran's inland customs clearance, storage, and transfer operations. This results in:</p>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">reduced congestion at border gateways,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">faster multimodal transfer processes,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">transit cargo being routed without entering city centers.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The arrival of regular train services from Russia at Aprin strengthens the facility's position as <strong>INSTC's backbone terminal</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Transit Time Under Two Weeks Enhances Competitiveness</strong></h4>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The most significant advantage provided by the new regular train line between Russia and Iran is its <strong>fast and predictable transit time</strong>.<br>\nThe under-two-week timeframe enhances the corridor's commercial appeal for transportation to Indian and Gulf markets. This development signals potential for increased volumes from Russia.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Important Political and Economic Implications</strong></h4>\n<p style="text-align: justify">In line with Russia's objective of strengthening its southern trade routes:</p>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">activating the INSTC,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">launching regular railway services on the Iran line,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">accelerating access to Gulf and Indian markets<br>\nare of strategic importance.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Similarly, Iran has reached an important turning point supporting its vision of becoming a regional logistics center. The commencement of regular cargo flows to Aprin will significantly enhance Iran's north-south logistics capacity.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h4>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>first regular train service</strong> from Russia to Iran's Aprin Dry Port has successfully commenced.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Transit time is <strong>under two weeks</strong>, enhancing the corridor's competitive strength.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Aprin is consolidating its position as INSTC's <strong>central intermodal transfer hub</strong>.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The corridor is developing as an alternative to Suez on the Russia–Iran–India route.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The development provides strategic logistics advantages for both Russia and Iran.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p>----------</p>\n\n<p>--------------------</p>\n<p><strong>Author: </strong><a href="http://sedatonat.com/"><u>SedatOnat.com</u></a></p>\n<p>--------------------</p>\n<p><strong>!!!
