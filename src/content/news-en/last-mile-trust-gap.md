<p>Last-mile deliveries are decisive in customer satisfaction, yet they represent the stage most vulnerable to trust issues. Lack of visibility in the delivery process and misinformation damage both brand reputation and customer loyalty. AI, IoT, and digital identity technologies play a fundamental role in closing this trust gap.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Smart locker systems and secure drop-off solutions physically safeguard deliveries. Facial recognition and digital proof of delivery (e-POD) applications reduce misdelivery rates by as much as 80%. Customers receive real-time notifications and verification codes, ensuring transparency.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Due to data privacy concerns, balance must be struck between transparency and security. Blockchain-based record systems ensure that delivery history is maintained immutably. This strengthens both consumer confidence and legal protection.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>In conclusion, last-mile trust is not merely a logistics matter, but a brand value issue.
