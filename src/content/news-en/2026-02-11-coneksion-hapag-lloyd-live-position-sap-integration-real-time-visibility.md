<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Coneksion</strong> has entered into a cooperation with Hapag-Lloyd to make Hapag-Lloyd's <strong>Live Position</strong> service available to shippers using SAP's freight management applications and IT systems. Hapag-Lloyd has made significant investments in real-time container visibility by equipping its container fleet (<strong>2 million boxes</strong>) with <strong>IoT devices</strong> that transmit exact GPS location data throughout the whole transport journey.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This capability is delivered through the Live Position service, enabling customers to track containers in real time from door to door. Live Position is available via a dedicated Tracking application in the Hapag-Lloyd Online Business Suite and through an <strong>API</strong>. However, customers who want to consume this data within their own operational systems must build and maintain their own integrations.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The cooperation with Coneksion eliminates that integration burden by streaming Live Position data directly into SAP <strong>S/4HANA Transportation Management</strong> and other SAP modules. For exporters and importers, that means more predictable transit times, faster exception management and more efficient <strong>supply chain orchestration</strong>. The solution complements DCSA digital shipping data standards with a richer location dimension.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong><br>
1. Coneksion and Hapag-Lloyd will make Live Position tracking available within SAP freight management applications.<br>
2. Hapag-Lloyd has equipped its 2-million-container fleet with IoT GPS devices.<br>
3. Live Position is delivered via the Hapag-Lloyd Online Business Suite and a dedicated API.<br>
4. The integration eliminates the custom development customers previously needed to ingest the data.<br>
5. The solution provides real-time container visibility within S/4HANA TM and other SAP modules.</p>
