<p>Emissions targets and sustainability concerns are pushing the shipping sector to explore new alternatives.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><a href="https://www.wartsila.com/"><u>Wärtsilä </u></a>is fully aware of this shift and is preparing to bring to market the <strong>world's first four-stroke ammonia-fuelled</strong> engine.&nbsp;</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>The company has already received Letters of Intent for 25 engines, with contracts expected to be signed in early 2024.&nbsp;</p>
