<p>Eyüp Logistics has introduced "Depootel," a new concept offering flexible storage solutions to meet customer warehousing needs. The service provides storage capacity ranging from 1 square meter to 100,000 square meters and from 1 pallet to 100,000 pallets. Depootel offers daily, weekly, and annual storage options tailored to customer demands, along with services including loading, unloading, packaging, handling, and inventory counting. Eyüp Logistics Board Chairman Eyüp Bartık notes that the concept operates on a hotel model and is completely flexible according to customer requirements. Storage facilities are protected around the clock by security personnel and cameras, with real-time information sharing provided by professional teams.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>Depootel offers flexible storage space ranging from 1 square meter to 100,000 square meters.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Storage services can be provided on a daily, weekly, or annual basis.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Loading, unloading, packaging, handling, and inventory counting services are available.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Storage facilities are monitored 24 hours by security personnel and cameras.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Flexible and customized storage solutions are offered according to customer needs.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Depootel addresses both corporate and individual storage requirements.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n
