<p>Ocean Network Express (ONE) has announced two new Northern Europe/Mediterranean routes set to commence in January 2025: Aegean Express (AEX) and Levant Express (LEX). ONE stated that the AEX and LEX services will provide North-South connectivity, industry-leading transit times, and seamless access to feeder networks in Northern Europe and the Mediterranean through major transhipment hubs in Rotterdam, Istanbul, Piraeus, and Damietta.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Aegean Express operates on the following port rotation: London Gateway, Antwerp, Rotterdam, Piraeus, Istanbul, Gebze, Gemlik, Aliaga, Piraeus, London Gateway. AEX will be served weekly by a four-vessel fleet.</p>\n<p>Levant Express (LEX) operates on the following port rotation: London Gateway, Rotterdam, Hamburg, Antwerp, Alexandria, Damietta, Mersin, London Gateway. LEX will operate on a weekly frequency with a four-vessel fleet.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>With these new services, ONE aims to strengthen its connectivity between Northern Europe and the Mediterranean, offering customers faster and more efficient shipping options. Through major transhipment hubs in Rotterdam, Istanbul, Piraeus, and Damietta, the company plans to provide uninterrupted access to feeder networks in Northern Europe and the Mediterranean.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>\n<ul>\n  <li><p>Ocean Network Express (ONE) will launch Aegean Express (AEX) and Levant Express (LEX) routes in January 2025.</p></li>\n  <li><p>AEX and LEX services will offer North-South connectivity and fast transit times.</p></li>\n  <li><p>AEX rotation: London Gateway, Antwerp, Rotterdam, Piraeus, Istanbul, Gebze, Gemlik, Aliaga, Piraeus, London Gateway.</p></li>\n  <li><p>LEX rotation: London Gateway, Rotterdam, Hamburg, Antwerp, Alexandria, Damietta, Mersin, London Gateway.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Both services will operate on weekly frequency with four-vessel fleets.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Rotterdam, Istanbul, Piraeus, and Damietta will be utilized as major transhipment hubs.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link: </strong><a href="https://www.porttechnology.org/news/one-unveils-northern-europe-mediterranean-routes/"><u>https://www.porttechnology.org/news/one-unveils-northern-europe-mediterranean-routes/</u></a></p>\n<p>-------------------</p>\n<p><strong>!!!
