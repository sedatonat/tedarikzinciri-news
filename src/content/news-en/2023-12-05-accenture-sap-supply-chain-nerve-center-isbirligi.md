<p><a href="https://www.accenture.com/us-en"><u>Accenture</u></a> and <a href="https://www.sap.com"><u>SAP</u></a> are collaborating to develop a platform called the <a href="https://www.sap.com/documents/2023/11/f271be4a-9b7e-0010-bca6-c68f7e60039b.html"><u>"Supply Chain Nerve Center,"</u></a> designed to help organizations reshape their supply chains while reducing risk, enhancing visibility, and supporting sustainability objectives.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Aimed at the consumer products, high-technology, industrial manufacturing, and automotive sectors, this <a href="https://www.sap.com/documents/2023/11/f271be4a-9b7e-0010-bca6-c68f7e60039b.html"><u>"Supply Chain Nerve Center"</u></a> will deliver n-tier transparency by leveraging cloud, data, artificial intelligence (AI), and analytics. This will enable organizations to identify risks beyond first and second-tier suppliers, providing deeper visibility across their supplier and subcontractor network.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Additionally, the platform will transform data into action by assessing whether suppliers align with the organization's environmental, social, and governance (ESG) objectives, thereby improving decision-making processes.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>The <a href="https://www.sap.com/documents/2023/11/f271be4a-9b7e-0010-bca6-c68f7e60039b.html"><u>"Supply Chain Nerve Center"</u></a> also incorporates a supply chain resilience stress test developed by <a href="https://www.accenture.com/us-en"><u>Accenture</u></a> and the <a href="https://www.mit.edu/"><u>Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT).</u></a> This test helps identify potential failure points, assess associated financial risks, and determine appropriate risk mitigation strategies and measures.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Additionally, digital twin simulation software developed by <a href="https://cosmotech.com/"><u>Cosmo Tech</u></a> enables organizations to identify vulnerabilities in their supply chains, simulate how they perform under heavy load, and develop robust risk mitigation plans throughout the planning process.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>The partners also plan to develop new capabilities to further enhance the features of the <a href="https://www.sap.com/products/scm/integrated-business-planning.html"><u>SAP Integrated Business Planning for Supply Chain (SAP IBP)</u></a> solution, which will help organizations quickly and cost-effectively respond to changes in supply, demand, and inventory.</p>
