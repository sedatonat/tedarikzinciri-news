<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>INFORM</strong> has introduced <strong>YMSlite</strong>, a <strong>cloud-based</strong> yard management solution aimed at small to mid-sized logistics facilities. The new product will serve a broad user base including warehouses, distribution centres, carrier yards, container yards, 3PL sites, and manufacturing operations with yard activity. INFORM's existing yard management offering included an enterprise-level system used at several large distribution centres across Europe; YMSlite broadens that portfolio.</p><p style="text-align: justify">Alongside the enterprise system supporting large logistics operations, YMSlite is positioned as a <strong>lighter solution</strong> for sites looking to digitise yard processes <strong>without implementing a full enterprise platform</strong>. At many logistics facilities, yard coordination still depends on manual tools and informal communication between gate staff, drivers, and warehouse teams.</p><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Gary Van Tassel</strong>, Director of Sales, North America for Terminal & Distribution Center Logistics at INFORM, noted that this leads to delays in gate wait times and data gaps at smaller sites. YMSlite brings together features such as <strong>licence plate recognition, a driver mobile app, yard mapping, and real-time notifications</strong>. The cloud-based architecture reduces implementation time and IT infrastructure investment. INFORM announced that YMSlite will launch in European and North American markets in the coming months.</p>
