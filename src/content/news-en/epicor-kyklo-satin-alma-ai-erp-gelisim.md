<p>Epicor announced the acquisition of Kyklo, a nine-year-old startup based in Buffalo, New York. Kyklo provides product information management (PIM) and lead generation solutions aimed at helping manufacturers and distributors increase sales. The acquisition is intended to accelerate Epicor's development of artificial intelligence-powered, cognitive enterprise resource planning (ERP) software. Epicor CEO Steve Murphy noted that the acquisition supports the company's goal of becoming a core partner equipped with digital capabilities.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>With Kyklo, Epicor customers will be able to more easily create, manage, and distribute product information—such as content, pricing, and inventory—across multiple channels. This enables them to generate qualified leads and revenue. Additionally, Kyklo supports collaboration with suppliers and the use of product discovery tools that strengthen e-commerce strategies.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Takeaways</h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>Epicor acquired Kyklo, which offers product information management and lead generation solutions.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The acquisition targets the development of Epicor's AI-powered, cognitive ERP software.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The acquisition will give customers the ability to manage and distribute product information across multiple channels.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Kyklo supports tools that will strengthen supplier collaboration and e-commerce strategies.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10404-epicor-acquires-enterprise-software-startup-kyklo"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10404-epicor-acquires-enterprise-software-startup-kyklo</u></a></p>
