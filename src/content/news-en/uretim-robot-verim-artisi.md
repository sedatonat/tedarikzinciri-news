<p style="text-align: justify">Warehouse robotics investments are accelerating worldwide. While <strong>51,000+ AMRs</strong> were deployed in 2023, that number is expected to reach <strong>180,000 by 2030</strong>. AMRs deliver significant benefits in labor-constrained operations; however, simply "adding more robots" often means <strong>diminishing returns</strong>, <strong>high capital expenditure (capex)</strong>, and <strong>complex workflows</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Zebra is introducing a solution that unites robotic and human workforce through a single orchestration layer using modern <strong>cloud + edge</strong> architecture, making it possible to achieve <strong>the same throughput with just one-third fewer robots</strong>. Here are five essential strategies for extracting maximum efficiency from every AMR:</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>1. Asset Utilization: Reduce Dwell Time, Unlock Capacity</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Many warehouses operate larger AMR fleets than necessary. The reasons:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Robots waiting in queue during <strong>induction</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>AMRs parked in corridors</strong> waiting for an operator,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Idle robots due to order flow fluctuations during <strong>takeoff</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Robot orchestration optimizes AMR movements and tasks <strong>in real time</strong>.</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Edge devices provide instant coordination.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The cloud layer continuously analyzes performance data.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Task assignments are automatically updated.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Result: <strong>dwell time decreases</strong>, <strong>facility throughput increases</strong>, and the true potential of AMRs is realized.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>2. Higher Cubic Capacity: 30% Fewer Robots with Right Cart Design</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Most AMRs make more trips than necessary because of limited volume on integrated transport platforms.<br>
Zebra's approach:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>High-capacity rolling carts</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Detachable and low-cost carrier carts</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Cart buffering</strong> (staging areas with full loads ready).</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This enables:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">A single robot to handle 3 times more volume,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">AMR count to be <strong>reduced by 30%</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Reduced robot idle time and continuous workflow.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>3. Optimize Pick Tours: Smart Orchestration, Not "Swarm"</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">In swarm models, robots fill the pick area; workers waste time searching for the next AMR.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Zebra's orchestration engine:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Increases pick density,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Optimizes robot-picker pairing,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Minimizes pick routes,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Directs AMRs in <strong>leapfrog</strong> sequence, staying one step ahead of the picker.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This creates continuous workflow:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Pickers never wait,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">AMRs remain productive,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">More items collected in less time.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>4. Directed Workflows: End-to-End Flow with Wearable Technology</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Many automation solutions focus solely on robots. Zebra integrates robot-human interaction.<br>
Wearable devices:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Direct correct product-order matching during induction,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Provide real-time task and routing information to pickers,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Free both hands via ring scanner,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Display the correct staging area at takeoff.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Because each step is directed, error rates drop, worker satisfaction rises, and physical strain decreases.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>5. Real-Time Visibility: Real-Time Management with 1,000 Data Points</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Wearables don't just direct workers—they provide management with <strong>instant visibility</strong>:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Picker performance</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Customer-order cycle time analysis</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Identification of slotting requirements</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Identification of profitable and unprofitable business models</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Zebra's advanced telemetry system processes <strong>500–1,000 data points per order</strong>, identifying anomalies, bottlenecks, and opportunities.<br>
Result: 3PLs and warehouse managers can optimize resources on a minute-by-minute basis.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Zebra Technologies: Integrated Orchestration with AMR + Edge + Cloud</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Zebra Symmetry Fulfillment:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">AMRs + edge devices + cloud analytics</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Synchronization of human-robot workflows on a single platform</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Reducing dwell time to increase throughput</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Optimizing robot investments</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Integration with existing WMS/ERP infrastructure</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Zebra also provides:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Barcode &amp; RFID solutions</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Mobile computers and rugged tablets</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Wearables</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Real-time location systems</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">AI-powered workflow analytics</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">supporting 80% of Fortune 500 supply chain operations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Orchestration</strong>, rather than increasing AMR count, delivers higher ROI.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Cart buffering and high-volume carts <strong>reduce robot requirements by 30%</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Leapfrog robot routing shortens pick paths and increases throughput.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Wearables direct the entire end-to-end flow; reducing errors and fatigue.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Advanced telemetry gives management <strong>real-time decision-making</strong> power.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Zebra Symmetry Fulfillment manages robot-human interaction on a single platform.</p></li>
</ul>
