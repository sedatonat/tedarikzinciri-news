<p>Vertical Cold Storage has expanded its operations in the Dallas-Fort Worth region by opening its second temperature-controlled distribution center. The new facility, located in Burleson, Texas, spans 400,000 square feet and offers a range of services including blast freezing, order picking, kitting, and labeling. The facility provides an ideal location for perishable products such as frozen and chilled foods, with its size and versatile refrigeration capacities. The company's CEO West Hutchison noted that the new facility delivers comprehensive service coverage across both sides of the DFW market.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5>Key Points:</h5>
<ol>
  <li><p><strong>New Facility:</strong> A 400,000 square-foot temperature-controlled distribution center has opened in the Dallas-Fort Worth region.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>Services:</strong> Blast freezing, order picking, kitting, and labeling services are offered.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>Location:</strong> The facility is situated in Burleson, Texas, immediately adjacent to the I-35 highway.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>Capabilities:</strong> The facility features rooms convertible to -20°F and 53 loading docks.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>Company Strategy:</strong> The company opened a second facility as part of its expansion strategy.</p></li>
</ol>
