<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM Group has announced it has signed a term sheet to acquire a <strong>20% stake</strong> in <strong>EUROGATE Container Terminal Hamburg (CTH)</strong> as part of its strategy to expand port assets in Europe. The transaction is expected to be completed in the first half of 2026, following regulatory approvals.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM already regularly uses Hamburg CTH today, particularly through its <strong>FAL (French Asia Line)</strong> service. The FAL service consists of routes between Asia and Northern Europe operated by 23,000 TEU LNG-fueled megamax vessels. Consequently, this investment represents both a logical fit with existing operations and a strategic step toward long-term strengthening of European operations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Strengthening the terminal portfolio in Europe</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM operates in <strong>64 terminals</strong> worldwide. This investment in Hamburg aligns with the company's expansion plans aimed at making its European distribution network more resilient and <strong>reducing carbon footprint</strong> in continental logistics chains.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">As Hamburg Port is one of Northern Europe's most important container hubs, this stake acquisition will further solidify CMA CGM's strategic position.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>EUROGATE Western Extension: Terminal to reach 6 million TEU</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">This partnership directly supports EUROGATE's <strong>Western Extension</strong> expansion project in Hamburg. Under the project scope:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The terminal will expand by approximately <strong>38 hectares</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Approximately <strong>1,050 metres</strong> of new quay wall will be constructed,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Capacity will increase from <strong>4 million TEU to 6 million TEU</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Additional draft and operational space will be provided for larger new-generation vessels.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This expansion will significantly enhance the terminal's efficiency and <strong>intermodal connections</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Direct connection to Germany's largest intermodal terminal</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">CTH has a direct rail connection to <strong>EUROKOMBI</strong>, Germany's largest intermodal facility. The investment:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Enables faster access to inland regions,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Increases capacity on rail connections,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Delivers lower carbon emissions in multimodal transport,<br>
supporting CMA CGM's <strong>low-emission logistics chain</strong> vision.</p></li>
  <li><p><br></p></li>
</ul>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>CMA CGM: "We are confident in Hamburg's long-term competitiveness"</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM CEO <strong>Rodolphe Saadé</strong> noted that the agreement represents a significant milestone for Hamburg Port and demonstrates the company's long-term commitment:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"This new partnership we are establishing with EUROGATE will strengthen Hamburg's critical role in European trade. We will work to increase terminal capacity, improve rail connections, and support more sustainable operations. We are confident in Germany's long-term competitiveness."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Impact on European supply chain</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">This investment aligns with CMA CGM's broader strategy in Europe:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Making the network more resilient,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Greater integration in intermodal transport,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Reducing congestion risks on Northern European routes,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Enhancing Hamburg's competitive strength against major carriers such as <strong>MSC, Maersk, and Hapag-Lloyd</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">The company continues to expand its terminal portfolio across the continent to meet growing European hinterland demand.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM signed a term sheet to acquire a <strong>20% stake in Hamburg CTH</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Transaction will be completed in the first half of 2026.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Terminal capacity will increase from <strong>4M to 6M TEU</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">38-hectare expansion and 1,050 metres of new quay planned.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">EUROKOMBI connection strengthens intermodal capability.</p></li>

</ul>
