<p>Florida-based railroad operator CSX Corp has joined the <a href="https://www.railpulse.com/technology/"><u>RailPulse</u></a> coalition, which aims to accelerate the adoption of GPS and other telematics technologies across the North American railroad industry to enhance safety, efficiency, and visibility. The coalition has set objectives to support industry-wide telematics infrastructure and enable data-driven transformation, with the goal of making railroads more competitive with other freight transportation modes. With CSX's participation, the coalition has added the fourth-largest railroad operator in its class and its eleventh member overall.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Points:</h5>\n<ul>\n  <li><p>CSX joined the RailPulse coalition to enhance railroad safety and efficiency in North America.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The coalition aims to make railroads more competitive with other transportation modes.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Objectives include establishing industry-wide telematics infrastructure and enabling data-driven transformation.</p></li>\n  <li><p>CSX became the coalition's fourth-largest railroad company and its eleventh member overall.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10560-csx-joins-railpulse-digitization-initative"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10560-csx-joins-railpulse-digitization-initative</u></a></p>
