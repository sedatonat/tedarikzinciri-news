<p>Kion North America will manufacture Fox Robotics' autonomous forklifts, the FoxBot ATL (autonomous trailer loader/unloader), under a new agreement with the company. The partnership will be carried out at facilities in Summerville, South Carolina. Since 2019, Fox Robotics has been marketing the FoxBot, the world's first electric, stand-up autonomous forklift in the autonomous mobile robot (AMR) category. The FoxBot features technology that automates loading and unloading in warehouse loading areas.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>This manufacturing agreement will allow Fox Robotics to fulfill a significant order backlog and expand its supply chain capabilities. Investments by major retailers such as Walmart in Fox's autonomous forklifts and taking a stake in the company strengthen Fox Robotics' market position. Walmart plans to deploy 19 FoxBot units across four modern distribution centers to unload pallets from trucks.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The partnership between Fox Robotics and Kion North America underscores the importance of innovation in the logistics and transportation industry. Jonathan Dawley, CEO of Kion North America, noted that Fox Robotics is an industry pioneer in automated trailer loading and unloading—the final piece of warehouse automation. Fox's AI/ML algorithms, technology stack, and robot fleet are raising industry standards.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Takeaways</h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>Kion North America will manufacture Fox Robotics' autonomous forklifts at its South Carolina facilities.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Fox Robotics has been marketing the FoxBot autonomous forklift since 2019.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Walmart has taken a stake in Fox Robotics and purchased 19 FoxBot units.</p></li>\n  <li><p>FoxBot is an autonomous mobile robot designed for use in warehouse loading areas.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Fox Robotics is a leader in the industry with AI and machine learning algorithms.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link: </strong><a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10232-kion-north-america-to-manufacture-fox-robotics-autonomous-forklifts"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10232-kion-north-america-to-manufacture-fox-robotics-autonomous-forklifts</u></a></p>
