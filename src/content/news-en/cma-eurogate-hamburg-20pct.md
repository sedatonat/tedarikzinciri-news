<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM confirmed it has acquired a <strong>20% stake</strong> in <strong>EUROGATE Container Terminal Hamburg (CTH)</strong> as part of its strategy to expand intermodal capacity in Europe. CTH, one of Hamburg's largest container facilities, has annual handling capacity of <strong>4 million TEU</strong>. The <strong>Western Expansion</strong> project, to be implemented over the coming years, aims to increase the terminal's capacity to <strong>6 million TEU</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM's investment represents a continuation of the company's <strong>vertically integrated logistics structure</strong>, which has accelerated over the past five years. In 2019, the company expanded its land transportation and warehousing network by acquiring a majority stake in CEVA Logistics. This was followed by the acquisition of <strong>GEFCO</strong>, the automotive logistics giant, in 2022, and the purchase of <strong>Freightliner UK</strong>, one of the United Kingdom's largest rail freight operators, in 2025.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This sequence demonstrates that CMA CGM has evolved from being merely a shipping line into an <strong>end-to-end integrated logistics giant</strong>. The CTH investment in Hamburg represents a strategic expansion on the port operations side of this vision.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>The Importance of Terminal Investment</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Hamburg Port is one of Northern Europe's most strategic hubs for major carriers such as MSC, Maersk, Hapag-Lloyd, COSCO, and ONE. Under the CTH Western Expansion project:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>38 hectares of additional land</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>1,050 metres of new quay</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Higher automation,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Increased draft for larger vessels<br>
are planned.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM's minority stake participation supports both terminal modernisation and provides the company with greater access to its intra-Europe intermodal infrastructure.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>The Rise of Vertical Integration</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">This stake acquisition is significant not only for CMA CGM but also for the global container shipping industry. MSC, Maersk, and COSCO have, over the past 10 years, expanded beyond container shipping into:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">port operations,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">rail companies,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">warehousing and fulfillment,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">e-commerce logistics,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">air cargo<br>
with aggressive expansions.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM stands out as one of the strong players following this strategy. Through the acquisition of Freightliner UK, it has gained a significant foothold in the UK's <strong>rail backbone</strong>; with CEVA, it has built global <strong>warehousing and land transportation</strong> capabilities; and with CTH, it has established an important position in <strong>port operations</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This trend will be one of the main discussion topics at the <strong>European Silk Road Summit</strong> to be held in Milan on 19–20 November. The summit will address why major lines are increasingly becoming "fully integrated logistics groups" and how supply chain verticals are being reshaped.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Strengthening Europe's Network with CTH</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM is expanding its capacity on the Asia–Northern Europe corridor with LNG-powered <strong>23,000 TEU FAL vessels</strong>. The advantages the CTH investment in Hamburg will provide:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Stronger terminal access for FAL service,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Direct access to rail connections in Hamburg's hinterland (particularly <strong>EUROKOMBI</strong>),</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Time-to-market advantage in the intra-Europe intermodal network,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Operational flexibility at Northern European gateways.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">All these elements are making CMA CGM's presence in Europe more resilient, independent, and low-carbon.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM is acquiring a <strong>20% stake</strong> in Hamburg CTH.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Terminal capacity will increase from 4 million to 6 million TEU.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The investment is part of CMA CGM's <strong>vertically integrated logistics</strong> strategy.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Aligned with the acquisitions of CEVA, GEFCO, and Freightliner UK.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Hamburg Port is one of Europe's most strategic gateways.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">CTH's Western Expansion project will deliver modernisation, capacity for larger vessels, and intermodal integration.</p></li>
</ul>
