<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Between July 16 and August 31, 2025,</strong> the <strong>U.S. supply chain technology sector</strong> witnessed notable merger, acquisition, and investment deals.</p>
<p><br>
This period demonstrated sustained strong capital flows toward both <strong>logistics software and AI-enabled supply chain solutions.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Below are the <strong>10 most significant investments and capital transactions</strong> listed by category:</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>1. Private Equity (PE) – Growth / Expansion</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>ACI Logistix</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Business Focus:</strong> <strong>Parcel logistics and distribution platform</strong> for e-commerce and physical retail.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Investor:</strong> <strong>Federation Asset Management.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Objective:</strong> <strong>Expand parcel delivery network</strong> across the U.S. and <strong>invest in automation technologies.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>2. Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A)</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>E2open</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Focus:</strong> Cloud-based, end-to-end supply chain management SaaS platform.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Acquirer:</strong> <strong>WiseTech Global.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Value:</strong> <strong>$2.1 billion.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Impact:</strong> Expected to <strong>expand WiseTech's global logistics software ecosystem</strong> and integrate E2open's <strong>visibility and integration capabilities.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FreightVerify</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Focus:</strong> Real-time transportation visibility and business intelligence platform.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Acquirer:</strong> <strong>Overhaul.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Objective:</strong> <strong>Expand Overhaul's shipment compliance and risk management modules.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>3. Later Stage Venture Capital (Later Stage VC)</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>BinSentry</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Focus:</strong> Supply chain visibility and operations monitoring solutions for agriculture.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Amount:</strong> <strong>$70 million (Series C).</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Investor:</strong> <strong>Lead Edge Capital (lead investor).</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Objective:</strong> Expand agricultural automation and IoT-based inventory tracking solutions into international markets.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>ketteQ</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Focus:</strong> Sales and operations planning (S&OP)-focused supply chain planning platform.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Amount:</strong> <strong>$20 million (Series B).</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Investor:</strong> <strong>Vocap Partners (lead investor).</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Objective:</strong> <strong>Expand platform's AI-powered demand planning and scenario modeling</strong> capabilities.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Overhaul</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Focus:</strong> Real-time supply chain visibility, compliance, and risk management.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Amount:</strong> <strong>$105 million (Series C).</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Investor:</strong> <strong>Springcoast Capital Partners (lead investor).</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Note:</strong> During the same period, Overhaul acquired FreightVerify to create an <strong>integrated transportation risk management ecosystem.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>4. Early Stage Venture Capital (Early Stage VC)</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>BulkSource</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Focus:</strong> ERP solution that digitalizes manufacturing and logistics processes for <strong>bulk material suppliers.</strong></p></li>

  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Objective:</strong> <strong>Improve supply chain efficiency for small and medium-sized manufacturers.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Lyric</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Focus:</strong> <strong>AI-powered supply chain management platform</strong> that automates operational decisions.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Amount:</strong> <strong>$43.5 million (Series B).</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Investor:</strong> <strong>Insight Partners (lead investor).</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Objective:</strong> <strong>Expand platform's AI-first operations management</strong> capabilities and go global.</p></li>
</ul>

<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Overall Assessment: Strong Capital Flows, Focus on "Visibility and AI"</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">During the summer months of 2025, investment trends in supply chain technologies centered on three core themes:</p>
<ol>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Growing demand for real-time visibility</strong> solutions,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>AI-powered decision systems</strong> and <strong>operational automation,</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Sustainability and circular economy applications.</strong></p></li>
</ol>
<p style="text-align: justify">The total investment volume demonstrates continued digital transformation in the sector and shows that investors are <strong>focusing on long-term infrastructure platforms.</strong><br>
Major acquisitions (particularly <strong>WiseTech–E2open</strong>) also highlight <strong>strengthening consolidation trends in the supply chain software market.</strong></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Highlights:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Total transaction value:</strong> Over $2.3 billion (M&A + VC + PE).</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Largest acquisition:</strong> <strong>WiseTech Global's acquisition of E2open for $2.1 billion.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Highest VC investment:</strong> <strong>Overhaul – $105 million (Series C).</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Primary investment themes:</strong> Visibility, artificial intelligence, sustainable supply chain, and digital integration.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Friendlier and Olivery</strong> stood out as notable 2025 seed-stage investments in circular economy and SME logistics.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">These investments signal that <strong>global supply chain technologies are entering a maturation and consolidation phase.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
