<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Delta Cargo</strong> has introduced <strong>DeliverDirect</strong>, a new solution developed to address the e-commerce sector's demand for fast and reliable delivery. Strengthened with <strong>SmartKargo</strong> infrastructure, this system represents the <strong>first comprehensive "dock-to-door" air transportation service in the United States</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">As e-commerce grows, customer expectations are shifting. Today, consumers care not only about product quality but also about <strong>delivery speed and reliability</strong>. <strong>DeliverDirect</strong> addresses these changing expectations by leveraging <strong>Delta Air Lines' network of more than 2,500 daily domestic flights</strong>, offering a <strong>hybrid model that combines the speed of air transportation with the cost advantages of ground logistics</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Delivering at E-Commerce Speed</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DeliverDirect</strong> combines Delta Air Lines' extensive <strong>domestic flight network</strong> with <strong>SmartKargo's digital logistics platform</strong>, providing <strong>high-speed and traceable delivery</strong> capabilities for small package transportation.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The system utilizes the space available in the <strong>cargo compartments (belly space)</strong> of Delta's passenger aircraft. This allows existing flight capacity to be integrated into the logistics chain without requiring new warehouse investments.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>SmartKargo Business Development Vice President Derreck Travers</strong> stated, "With DeliverDirect, we are redefining small package delivery by tapping into the potential within Delta's flight network."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Under this model, shippers can choose from <strong>various speed options ranging from next-day delivery to five days</strong>. Additionally, features such as <strong>real-time tracking</strong>, <strong>automatic notifications</strong>, and <strong>photographic proof of delivery</strong> provide a transparent user experience.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Addressing Industry Challenges</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Rising <strong>labor costs</strong>, <strong>volatile cargo volumes</strong>, <strong>rate pressures</strong>, and <strong>high warehouse expenses</strong> present significant challenges for logistics firms globally. <strong>DeliverDirect</strong> responds to these challenges by adopting an <strong>"asset-light"</strong> business model.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This model is built on an <strong>airport-to-door delivery chain</strong> without requiring investment in fixed warehouse infrastructure. As a result, operations can <strong>easily scale as demand increases</strong> and become more resilient to economic fluctuations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Delta Air Lines Cargo Products President Jeffrey Elder</strong> commented, "Small package transportation has been on our radar for some time. With DeliverDirect, we are offering our customers a more transparent, reliable, and competitive alternative."</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A Digital and Customer-Centric Approach</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DeliverDirect</strong> is designed with a user-friendly digital platform. Shippers can access advanced features such as <strong>live package tracking</strong> (even during flights), <strong>automatic alerts</strong>, and <strong>delivery performance reports</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>SmartKargo's technological backbone</strong> makes every package's journey <strong>traceable from pickup to delivery</strong>. This enables brands to achieve <strong>complete transparency</strong> in their interactions with logistics providers.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, the system avoids <strong>complex pricing models</strong> and offers businesses a <strong>predictable and simplified cost structure</strong>. Without accessorial fees or hidden charges, users have clarity and control over pricing.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A Next-Generation Delivery Model</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DeliverDirect</strong> introduces a new delivery paradigm focused on speed, cost control, and flexibility. Beyond serving as an alternative to traditional ground transportation, the system <strong>creates a new segment by integrating air transportation with small package logistics</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This innovative solution is particularly appealing to brands operating in sectors such as <strong>apparel, eyewear, and manufacturing</strong>. <strong>This warehouse-network-free, real-time traceable, digital-based model</strong> is redefining the delivery standards of future e-commerce.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">DeliverDirect thus positions itself not merely as <strong>a delivery service</strong>, but also as <strong>a transformation movement bringing air logistics to the grassroots</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DeliverDirect</strong> was developed in partnership between <strong>Delta Cargo and SmartKargo</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">It is positioned as <strong>the first comprehensive dock-to-door air transportation solution in the United States</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Delta's more than 2,500 daily domestic flights</strong> have been integrated into the delivery network.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Real-time tracking, photographic proof of delivery, and digital notifications</strong> are provided.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>asset-light model eliminates warehouse requirements</strong>, providing cost and speed advantages.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The system stands out with <strong>simplified pricing</strong> and a <strong>customer-centric digital interface</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">DeliverDirect aims to become <strong>the future standard in small package delivery</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
