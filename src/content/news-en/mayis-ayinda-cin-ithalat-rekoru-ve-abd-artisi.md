<p>In May, U.S. imports showed significant growth, particularly in shipments arriving from China.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>According to the "June Global Shipping Report" prepared by Descartes Systems Group, U.S. container import volumes increased 6.2% compared with April and 11.9% year-over-year. This growth marked the second-highest monthly volume since May 2023. However, the report noted that risks of disruption to global supply chains remained elevated due to current conditions at the Panama and Suez Canals, upcoming labor negotiations at U.S. South Atlantic and Gulf ports, and conflicts in the Middle East.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Despite these concerns, the report indicated that import volumes at East and Gulf Coast ports had not yet been materially affected by these factors, with port transit delays showing signs of improvement.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Chris Jones from Descartes stated that U.S. container import volumes in the first five months of 2024 increased 15.5% compared with the same period last year, with May imports from China rising 17.6% and playing a key role in driving overall growth.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5><strong>Key Points</strong></h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>U.S. total import volumes in May increased 6.2% compared with April and 11.9% year-over-year.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Imports from China reached their second-highest monthly volume since May 2023.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Global supply chain disruption risks remain elevated, with risk factors including conditions at the Panama and Suez Canals, labor negotiations at U.S. South Atlantic and Gulf ports, and Middle East conflicts.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Although import volumes at East and Gulf Coast ports remain unaffected, port transit delays are showing improvement.</p></li>\n  <li><p>In the first five months of 2024, U.S. container import volumes increased 15.5% compared with the same period last year.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Imports from China, growing 17.6% in May, served as a primary driver of overall growth.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10384-may-imports-from-china-were-highest-in-16-months"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10384-may-imports-from-china-were-highest-in-16-months</u></a></p>
