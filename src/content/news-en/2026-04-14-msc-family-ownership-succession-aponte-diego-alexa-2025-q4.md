<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>MSC Mediterranean Shipping Company (MSC)</strong> has confirmed a <strong>transfer of ownership</strong> completed during the final quarter of 2025. MSC Group Chairman and founder <strong>Captain Gianluigi Aponte</strong> transferred ownership to his children <strong>Diego Aponte</strong> (Group President) and <strong>Alexa Aponte</strong> (Group Chief Financial Officer). Both names had served in senior leadership roles within the Geneva-based group for many years.</p><p style="text-align: justify">Italian nationals raised in Switzerland and currently based there, Diego and Alexa Aponte have taken on ownership responsibility to secure the group's <strong>long-term stability</strong> and sustained growth. According to MSC management, the move ensures the company's strategic direction will be maintained under the stewardship of the <strong>next generation of the Aponte family</strong>.</p><p style="text-align: justify">Founder Captain Gianluigi Aponte said: "Passing ownership to my children is not only a reflection of their dedication and achievements, but also a continuation of our maritime legacy." MSC is one of the world's largest container carriers and overtook Maersk in the 2020s to claim <strong>leadership of the container market</strong>. The next generation of Apontes is expected to expand the group's port terminal investments, energy transition, and digitalisation strategies more aggressively.</p>

<p><br></p>
<p><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong><br>
1. MSC confirmed the ownership transfer within the Aponte family in Q4 2025.<br>
2. Captain Gianluigi Aponte passed ownership to his children Diego and Alexa Aponte.<br>
3. Diego Aponte serves as Group President; Alexa Aponte as Group Chief Financial Officer.<br>
4. The transfer aims to ensure long-term stability and sustained growth.<br>
5. MSC remains the global container market leader.</p>
