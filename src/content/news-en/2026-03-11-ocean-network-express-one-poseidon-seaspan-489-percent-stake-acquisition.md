<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Ocean Network Express (ONE)</strong> has signed agreements to acquire an additional stake in <strong>Poseidon Corp.</strong> from existing shareholders. Poseidon is the parent company of <strong>Seaspan Corporation</strong>, the world's largest <strong>non-operating owner of containerships</strong>. Once the transaction closes, ONE's ownership in Poseidon will increase to <strong>48.9 per cent</strong>.</p><p style="text-align: justify">ONE CEO <strong>Jeremy Nixon</strong> said: "Since our initial investment in 2023, Seaspan has achieved remarkable growth as the world's leading tonnage platform. We are pleased to acquire this additional stake in Poseidon, which fully aligns with our <strong>ONE 2030 strategic growth plan</strong>." Seaspan operates with a business model of leasing containerships to operating lines under long-term charter contracts and owns a fleet of hundreds of ships.</p><p style="text-align: justify">ONE's increase in ownership aims to strengthen the <strong>vertical integration</strong> of the container line. By gaining direct access against fluctuations in the charter market, ONE can improve cost predictability and fleet planning flexibility. Recently, ONE also enhanced its West Coast South America (AX4) service, expanding network coverage across Latin America and Asia. These two moves are interpreted as strategic steps reshaping ONE's global liner structure.</p>
