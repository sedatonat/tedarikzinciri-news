<p style="text-align: justify">Following the devastating wildfires in Los Angeles, <strong>American Logistics Aid Network (ALAN)</strong> is providing critical logistics support to aid survivors. Thanks to generous donations from communities, local organizations are currently well-stocked with supplies; however, they are facing logistics challenges in sorting and distributing these materials. <strong>ALAN</strong> is providing critical guidance to help them overcome these obstacles and will host a logistics briefing for nonprofits on Monday at 3:30 p.m. Eastern time. ​</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>ALAN</strong>'s Executive Director <strong>Kathy Fulton</strong> stated, "Many of these organizations have little to no experience running temporary storage and transportation operations like this, especially under such challenging conditions; therefore, <strong>ALAN</strong> is particularly focused on providing these organizations with the guidance they need at this critical time." ​</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>ALAN</strong> is also collecting critical updates about road closures, power outages, and other infrastructure disruptions to help relief groups make informed decisions. To support long-term recovery efforts, they are encouraging businesses to donate warehouse space, trucks, or equipment. Financial contributions are also welcome, as these funds support <strong>ALAN</strong>'s ability to coordinate donated logistics services.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>ALAN</strong> is providing logistics support to those affected by the wildfires in Los Angeles.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Local organizations are facing challenges in sorting and distributing supplies.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>ALAN</strong> is collecting updates on infrastructure disruptions such as road closures and power outages.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Businesses can support the effort by donating warehouse space, trucks, or equipment.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Financial contributions support <strong>ALAN</strong>'s ability to coordinate logistics services.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>---</p>\n<p><strong>News Link: </strong><a href="https://www.supplychain247.com/article/los-angeles-wildfires-ALAN-assistance"><u>https://www.supplychain247.com/article/los-angeles-wildfires-ALAN-assistance</u></a></p>\n<p>-------------------</p>\n<p><strong>!!!
