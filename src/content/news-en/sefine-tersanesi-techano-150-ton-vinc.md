<p style="text-align: justify"><a href="https://sefine.com.tr/"><u><strong>Sefine Shipyard</strong></u></a> has signed a contract with Norway-based <a href="https://techano.no/"><u><strong>Techano Oceanlift</strong></u></a> to supply a 150-ton-capacity offshore crane for a newly constructed Construction Support Vessel (CSV). This collaboration demonstrates <strong>Sefine Shipyard</strong>'s commitment to producing modern vessels equipped with advanced maritime equipment.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Techano Oceanlift</strong> is recognized as a firm that provides innovative and reliable lifting solutions in the maritime sector. The company's product range includes offshore cranes, lifting equipment, and specialized maritime solutions. The 150-ton-capacity crane to be used in this project has been designed to deliver high performance and reliability in offshore operations. The crane is capable of performing precise and safe lifting operations even in challenging marine conditions.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Construction Support Vessels (CSV) play a critical role in offshore energy projects, particularly in the oil, gas, and renewable energy sectors. These vessels are used for maintenance, repair, installation, and other support services. The new CSV to be constructed by <strong>Sefine Shipyard</strong> is expected to make a significant impact in the industry with its modern equipment and high carrying capacity. The 150-ton-capacity crane will enhance the vessel's operational flexibility, enabling it to perform a wider range of tasks.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This collaboration strengthens <strong>Sefine Shipyard</strong>'s competitive position on the international stage and reinforces the shipyard's reputation for high-quality vessel construction. Similarly, the preference for <strong>Techano Oceanlift</strong>'s products in international projects reinforces the firm's position in the global market.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify">Technological advances and innovative solutions in the maritime sector enhance operational efficiency and safety. The partnership between <strong>Sefine Shipyard</strong> and <strong>Techano Oceanlift</strong> reflects this trend in the industry. The integration of advanced crane systems enables vessels to undertake more complex and demanding tasks, thereby improving the overall effectiveness of maritime operations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The successful completion of this project could serve as a precedent for similar collaborations in the future. The experience gained by <strong>Sefine Shipyard</strong> and <strong>Techano Oceanlift</strong> in this project can help both companies improve their products and services. Furthermore, such projects have positive impacts on local and international supply chains, contributing to economic growth.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Sustainability and environmental responsibility are gaining increasing importance in the maritime sector. Energy efficiency, emission reduction, and the use of environmentally friendly technologies are at the forefront of modern ship construction projects.
