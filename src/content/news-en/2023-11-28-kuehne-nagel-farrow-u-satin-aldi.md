<p>Swiss freight forwarding company <a href="https://home.kuehne-nagel.com/"><u>Kuehne+Nagel</u></a> has acquired <a href="https://farrow.com/"><u>Farrow</u></a><u> </u>, a customs brokerage firm founded in 1911 and headquartered in Canada. Through this acquisition, Kuehne+Nagel aims to expand its presence in North America and strengthen its capabilities across Canada and Mexico at the U.S. border.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Based in Windsor, Ontario, Farrow operates across 41 locations in Canada and the United States with 830 employees. With more than 110 years of experience in international trade, customs solutions, and integrated logistics, Farrow is a recognized name in the industry.&nbsp;</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Farrow's services include:</p>
<p><strong>1-</strong> Farrow Logistics (North American trade warehousing, distribution, and processing),&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>2-</strong> Farrow Global Logistics (International freight forwarding and global supply chain management),&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>3-</strong> Farrow Express (Specialization in expedited deliveries),</p>
<p><strong>4-</strong> Farrow Consulting (Import/export and trade management consulting)</p>
<p>.</p>
