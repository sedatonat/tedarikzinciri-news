<p>GXO has deployed 500 autonomous mobile robots (AMRs) across a 130,000 square-foot section of a distribution center in France serving a major global sports merchandise retailer. Managing a significant portion of the European retailer's logistics operations, GXO is bringing automation technologies online to meet customer demands.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The new system is designed to accelerate order processing, enable rapid response to seasonal volume fluctuations, and deliver efficiency savings. The AMRs, supplied by technology provider Geek+, navigate between high-density storage racks, moving 70,000 cartons to new locations across 18-foot-tall shelving and supporting order picking activities. The robots have improved productivity, accuracy, and safety.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Additionally, thanks to their lightweight design, these robots are easily transportable and offer flexibility to quickly adapt to seasonal changes.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Takeaways</h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>GXO deployed 500 AMRs at a distribution center in France.</p></li>\n  <li><p>These robots serve a major global sports merchandise retailer.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The automation solutions are designed to meet customer demands.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Robots manage 70,000 cartons and retrieve items from 18-foot-tall shelving.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The AMRs have delivered improvements in productivity, accuracy, and safety.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Robots are easily transportable and can be rapidly deployed to address seasonal needs.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10267-gxo-deploys-500-robots-at-french-dc"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10267-gxo-deploys-500-robots-at-french-dc</u></a></p>
