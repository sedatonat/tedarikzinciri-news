<p style="text-align: justify">Ukrainian Railways (<strong>Ukrzaliznytsia – UZ</strong>) is taking one of the most critical long-awaited steps in modernizing the country's railway system by purchasing <strong>55 dual-voltage electric locomotives</strong> from French manufacturer <strong>Alstom</strong>. The agreement is viewed as a strategic investment to make Ukraine's aging rail infrastructure more resilient and efficient both under wartime conditions and in meeting EU integration targets.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The purchase became possible through financing support provided by the <strong>World Bank</strong> and the <strong>European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD)</strong>. Both international financial institutions view Ukraine's transport infrastructure, particularly its railways, as a cornerstone of the country's economic and logistical resilience.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>An aging fleet is no longer sustainable</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">UZ's current locomotive fleet has an <strong>average age of 46 years</strong>. This signals equipment that is unprecedentedly old and worn compared to European standards.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Heavy loads, high line density, and increased operational demands under wartime conditions have rendered this aging fleet inadequate. Most existing locomotives:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">date from the Soviet era,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">have low energy efficiency,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">require frequent maintenance,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">face spare parts supply challenges.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new locomotives supplied by Alstom will significantly enhance UZ's operational reliability and cross-border transport capability.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Dual-voltage technology connects Ukraine to European rail lines</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Alstom's locomotives can operate on both <strong>25 kV AC</strong> and <strong>3 kV DC</strong> lines. This feature provides a major advantage in connections with neighboring countries — particularly <strong>Poland, Slovakia, and Romania</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Thanks to this technology:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">the need to change locomotives at border crossings will be reduced,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">transit times will be shortened,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">integrated European logistics corridors can be operated more efficiently.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This represents important infrastructure preparation for Ukraine's future <strong>TEN-T integration</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Critical modernization under wartime conditions</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Since 2022, Ukrainian railways have become the country's most reliable and highest-capacity logistics backbone. The majority of weapons, humanitarian aid, grain, fuel, and heavy industrial products are transported by rail.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new locomotives therefore represent not merely a modernization step, but also:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>security in military and humanitarian logistics</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>sustainability of export corridors</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>acceleration of economic recovery</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>full technical compliance with the EU</strong><br>
carry strategic importance.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Alstom's growing partnership with Ukraine</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Alstom is preparing to establish a deeper long-term partnership with UZ in the areas of maintenance, training, spare parts, and software management. The company's Europe-wide experience with high-capacity freight locomotives is viewed as critical for meeting Ukraine's increasing freight volumes.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Ukrainian Railways is purchasing <strong>55 dual-voltage electric locomotives</strong> from Alstom.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The project became possible thanks to <strong>World Bank and EBRD financing</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">UZ's fleet has an average age of <strong>46 years</strong> — modernization has become essential.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The new locomotives will provide significant advantages on cross-border lines.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The investment is strategic for Ukraine's logistical resilience and EU integration.</p></li>
</ul>
