<p>Walmart opened a new "next-generation" distribution center spanning 1.5 million square meters in Pennsylvania. The facility is designed to support increased order capacity and faster shipping speeds for both its own products and third-party logistics services. This is Walmart's fourth next-generation distribution center, with a fifth facility scheduled to open in California in 2026.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Walmart did not provide details about the high-tech facility but noted generally that next-generation sites combine people, robots, and machine learning to achieve twice the storage capacity of standard distribution centers and fulfill twice the daily customer orders.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Located in Greencastle, Pennsylvania, the facility will help Walmart expand its next-day or two-day delivery service. The Bentonville, Arkansas-based company will also use the new space to support Walmart Fulfillment Services, its third-party logistics offering that provides products from its marketplace e-commerce platform.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Takeaways</h5>\n<ul>\n  <li><p>Walmart opened a 1.5 million square meter distribution center in Pennsylvania.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The facility is designed for both Walmart's own products and third-party logistics services.</p></li>\n  <li><p>This is Walmart's fourth next-generation distribution center.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The new facility has twice the storage capacity and order processing capacity compared to standard DCs.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The facility will help Walmart expand its fast-delivery services.</p></li>\n  <li><p>A fifth next-generation facility is scheduled to open in California in 2026.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10279-walmart-opens-fourth-next-generation-fulfillment-center"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10279-walmart-opens-fourth-next-generation-fulfillment-center</u></a></p>
