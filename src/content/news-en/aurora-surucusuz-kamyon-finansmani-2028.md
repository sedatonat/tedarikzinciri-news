<p>Aurora Innovation, a company developing autonomous truck technology, is targeting the large-scale deployment of its driverless trucks by 2028. The company has secured $483 million in new funding to support this objective. Aurora plans to launch commercially by year-end. The company has three key pillars to realize these goals: transportation agreements with existing customers, production-ready platforms developed with Volvo and Peterbilt, and a technology manufacturing agreement with Continental in 2027.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Points:</h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>Aurora Innovation plans to deploy driverless trucks at scale by 2028.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The company has secured $483 million in new funding.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Commercial launch will take place by year-end.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The company has an existing customer base and production-ready platforms.</p></li>\n  <li><p>A manufacturing agreement with Continental was signed for 2027.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10677-aurora-raises-another-483-million-for-driverless-trucks"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10677-aurora-raises-another-483-million-for-driverless-trucks</u></a></p>
