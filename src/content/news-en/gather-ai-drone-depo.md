<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Gather AI</strong>, once known for <strong>drone-based inventory scans</strong>, is now redefining <strong>warehouse intelligence and visibility</strong> through <strong>software-driven artificial intelligence vision solutions</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
 The company, which won the <strong>Startup Award</strong> at the <strong>CSCMP EDGE Conference</strong>, is positioning itself not as a hardware manufacturer but as a platform delivering <strong>real-time warehouse management</strong> through <strong>AI-powered digital twin technologies</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>From Hardware to Intelligence: The Software-First Transformation</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Rob Rozicki, Vice President of Marketing at Gather AI</strong>, explains the company's transformation from "drone manufacturer to data intelligence provider" in the following terms:</p>
<p><br>
"We are no longer just a company of drones, but a company of the software that makes those drones intelligent. We are integrating AI vision into forklifts, cameras, wearables, and fixed sensors."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This approach enables Gather AI to make <strong>AI vision technology hardware-agnostic</strong>, transforming it into a <strong>scalable</strong> architecture.</p>
<p><br>
This allows the company to create <strong>real-time digital twin models by collecting data from every corner of the warehouse</strong>, not just from flying sensors.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Gather AI, born from autonomous flight research at <strong>Carnegie Mellon University</strong>, is now making <strong>"warehouse digital intelligence"</strong> its core focus.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>AI Vision and Digital Twins: Real-Time Visibility</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Gather AI's software analyzes visual data collected from <strong>forklifts, fixed cameras, and edge AI systems</strong> to create <strong>real-time warehouse maps</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
This system integrates with traditional <strong>WMS (Warehouse Management System)</strong> and <strong>ERP</strong> infrastructures, providing managers and operators with a <strong>single source of truth</strong>, from floor staff to upper management.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Rozicki summarizes this vision as follows:<br>
"AI vision is no longer just about counting shelves; it's about making sense of the physical world digitally. Our goal is to create 100 percent accurate digital twins."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This technology directly improves <strong>operational accuracy and speed</strong> by eliminating incorrect inventory records, lost products, and manual counting errors.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Consulting-Focused Approach and Long Sales Cycles</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Gather AI's sales processes can typically take <strong>up to nine months</strong>. This is due to the company's strategy of "providing consulting rather than selling products."</p>
<p><br>
Rozicki identifies two types of customer profiles:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Those advanced in digital transformation and with <strong>specific use case scenarios</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Those who see the potential but don't know "where to start."</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Gather AI works one-on-one with customers to identify <strong>pain points</strong>, developing <strong>realistic use cases</strong> based on examples from similar companies.<br>
This enables the company to establish <strong>high-trust, long-term business partnerships</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>AI Copilot: Natural Language Warehouse Management</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Gather AI's next major step is the <strong>AI Copilot</strong>, a natural language-enabled interface.<br>
This system allows managers and warehouse workers to access complex datasets simply by speaking.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Rozicki describes this technology as follows:<br>
"Copilot allows users to query warehouse data in natural language. Now you can simply ask 'How much inventory do I have?' and get an instant response from the system."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>AI Copilot</strong> combines Gather AI's <strong>AI vision</strong> data with existing <strong>WMS and ERP systems</strong> to support <strong>proactive decision-making</strong> processes.<br>
The company plans to launch the <strong>enterprise version in 2026</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Trust, ROI, and Scalability</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to Rozicki, the most important thing customers expect from AI solutions is <strong>accuracy</strong> and <strong>ROI</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
The majority of Gather AI's customers achieve ROI <strong>within 6 months or less.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The company's goal is not just to generate data, but also to become a <strong>trusted advisor</strong> on customers' <strong>AI maturity journeys</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Rozicki emphasizes their distance from hype culture by stating, "AI is everywhere now, but not every solution is implemented correctly. We bring 'AI discipline,' not 'AI hype.'"</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Evolution From Drone Company to Data Company</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Gather AI is now positioning itself as <strong>a data intelligence provider</strong>, not <strong>a drone company</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
Rozicki explains this transformation as follows:<br>
"For us, drones are just a tool. Our real business is making sense of data. We've integrated with forklifts, cameras, wearables. Now the possibilities are endless."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This vision is transforming Gather AI into a <strong>next-generation technology company that bridges physical processes in warehouses with digital insights</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Gather AI</strong> is transitioning from a <strong>drone-based system to a software-first</strong> model.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>AI vision</strong> technology is now being integrated into <strong>forklifts, cameras, wearables</strong>, and other sensors.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The company's goal is to create <strong>real-time digital twins</strong> and provide <strong>100 percent accurate warehouse visibility.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>AI Copilot</strong> enables warehouse data querying through natural language interface and <strong>proactive decision-making</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Customers typically achieve ROI <strong>within 6 months</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Gather AI follows a <strong>disciplined implementation strategy, distancing itself from AI hype culture</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The company, now <strong>a data intelligence provider rather than a drone manufacturer</strong>, is shaping supply chain innovation.</p></li>
</ul>
