<p style="text-align: justify">At the end of December, a freight train carrying vehicles that had been parked for three weeks north of Milan became the target of thieves. The train, stationed in the dedicated freight area at <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Desio_railway_station"><u><strong>Desio</strong></u></a> station, extended over a busy national highway due to its length. This situation placed the train in a highly visible public location and attracted the attention of unscrupulous individuals.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to Italian media, thieves who noticed the abandoned train began looting the vehicles. Bumpers, windows, and internal electronic components were stolen; in some cases, the vehicle canopies were damaged. The train was heading to an automotive center in <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Piadena"><u><strong>Piadena</strong></u></a>, but many vehicles arrived in a damaged condition.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This incident once again highlighted the importance of security measures in railway transportation. The protection of freight trains left parked for extended periods plays a critical role in both safeguarding transported goods and maintaining the reputation of the rail freight sector.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Railway transportation is preferred as a more environmentally friendly and economical alternative to road transport. However, such security breaches can negatively impact sector reliability. Therefore, rail companies and relevant authorities must implement additional measures to enhance the security of parked trains.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Increased freight volumes and congestion during holiday periods, in particular, can create conditions for such security vulnerabilities to emerge. For this reason, reviewing security protocols during holiday seasons and ensuring staff awareness of these issues is crucial.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Technological solutions can also be effective in preventing such incidents. For example, GPS tracking of trains, the use of security cameras, and the integration of alarm systems can deter theft attempts and reduce response times.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This incident serves as a warning not only in Italy but for all rail freight companies worldwide. Enhanced security measures, staff training, and technological investments will play a key role in preventing similar incidents in the future.</p>
