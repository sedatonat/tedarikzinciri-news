<p>Turkey's logistics sector faces a critical warehouse capacity crunch. With warehouse occupancy rates surpassing 90%, rents have climbed 96% over the past year and leasing demand has spiked 138%. Disruptions in international trade routes and contraction in the domestic market have extended product holding times, signaling an emerging crisis in storage facilities. This situation could trigger cost increases and shipment delays. Storage space availability remains insufficient in Istanbul and surrounding areas to meet demand. These developments could strain the sector further in the coming period.</p>
