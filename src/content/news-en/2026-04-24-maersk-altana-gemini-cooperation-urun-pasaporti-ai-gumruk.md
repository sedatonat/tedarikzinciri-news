<p style="text-align: justify">Maersk and data-intelligence firm Altana have agreed to embed an AI-powered digital trade layer into the Gemini Cooperation. The deal extends the alliance beyond physical shipping into product-level compliance and customs clearance.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify">The system introduces "Product Passports" that allow cargo to move with pre-cleared identity markers recognised by customs authorities across 12 major ports handling about 70% of global trade. Gemini, covering seven East-West trade routes, will become the first large-scale deployment of a federated trade data network linking carriers, cargo owners and customs agencies through a shared compliance architecture.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify">Altana's passport manager will connect importers and logistics providers directly with authorities, enabling risk assessments and clearance decisions using product-level data before arrival. Gemini Cooperation, operated by Maersk and Hapag-Lloyd since February 2025, runs 29 mainliner and 29 shuttle services.</p>
