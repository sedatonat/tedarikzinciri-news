<p style="text-align: justify">Walmart is expanding its investments in inventory tracking technologies to make its supply chain more visible and intelligent. The retail giant is partnering with <strong>Wiliot</strong> to deploy <strong>ambient IoT sensors</strong> throughout its supply chain, broadening automation and real-time data usage across inventory management, cold chain operations, and beyond.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Walmart's objective is clear: deliver <strong>faster, more accurate, and more efficient inventory management</strong> to further strengthen its AI-powered supply chain.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Sensors Active in 500 Stores — National Scale by 2026</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Wiliot sensors are currently deployed in <strong>500 Walmart stores</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The plan:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>4,600 stores by end of 2026</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>40+ distribution centers</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Indirectly equipping millions of pallets and cases with sensors.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This technology is a cornerstone of Walmart's goal to achieve <strong>near-complete IoT visibility</strong> across its U.S. supply chain network.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>How Ambient IoT Sensors Work</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Wiliot's small, low-cost sensors that harvest energy from their environment are called <strong>Pixels</strong>.<br>
These sensors:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Monitor product levels on pallets,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Track location changes,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Monitor temperature and environmental conditions (particularly critical for cold chain),</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Automatically track movement from distribution center to store.<br></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Wiliot President <strong>Julien Bellanger</strong> emphasizes that these sensors are particularly critical in cold chain operations:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"It's vital to ensure that products reaching stores are quickly placed in refrigeration."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Sensors <strong>eliminate manual scanning</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Positive delivery verification</strong> becomes fully automated,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Enables shared visibility between supplier and Walmart.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>With AI: Major Reduction in Inventory Errors</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Walmart feeds data from sensors directly into <strong>AI-powered inventory management systems</strong>.<br>
This enables:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">"What products do we have?"</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">"Where are they located?"</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">"Are stock levels actually accurate?"</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">to be answered <strong>in real time for the first time</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Walmart SVP <strong>Greg Cathey</strong> summarizes the partnership:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Through ambient IoT and AI integration, we're making faster and smarter inventory decisions.<br>
We're solving retail's toughest problem: knowing when, where, and what we have."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Case-Level Tagging: The Next Step</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Walmart currently uses sensors at the pallet level; however, Wiliot's goal is <strong>case-level tagging</strong>.<br>
When this transition occurs:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Inventory accuracy will increase further,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Shrinkage and loss rates will decrease,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Replenishment processes will become more automated,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Manual workload for store employees will be reduced.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Bellanger:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Our goal is to reach case-level tagging. This will completely transform Walmart's inventory visibility."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Operational Efficiency Gains</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Wiliot sensors also reduce workload for Walmart associates:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">No manual scanning</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">No paper processes</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">No unnecessary checks</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Cold chain breaches are instantly caught with automated alerts</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This enables teams to focus on higher-value activities:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Product placement</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Replenishment</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Customer service<br></p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Strategic Takeaway: Walmart Accelerates in the "AI-Powered Supply Chain" Race</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Walmart is expanding its AI investments beyond software alone, embedding them in <strong>physical supply chain infrastructure through sensors</strong>.<br>
With this technology:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Out-of-stock rates decline,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Overstock situations decrease,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Replenishment cycles accelerate,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Cold chain errors are minimized.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This enables Walmart to strengthen both its cost advantage and customer experience.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Walmart is scaling <strong>ambient IoT sensors</strong> nationally through its Wiliot partnership.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">By 2026, <strong>4,600 stores and 40 distribution centers</strong> will be covered.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Sensors eliminate manual scanning and enhance cold chain compliance.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Data is directly integrated into Walmart's <strong>AI systems</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Goal: Full visibility through <strong>case-level tagging</strong> instead of pallet-level.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">For Walmart, this technology creates strategic advantage along the <strong>inventory accuracy + speed + cost</strong> axis.</p></li>
</ul>
