<p style="text-align: justify">Hapag-Lloyd is conducting a comprehensive review of the <strong>Gemini Cooperation</strong> network developed jointly with Maersk to improve operational performance. CEO <strong>Rolf Habben Jansen</strong> said during the third-quarter results presentation that the purpose of the review is to analyze "what is working well and where improvements are needed."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Gemini Cooperation is among the projects reshaping the two carriers' networks using a <strong>hub-and-spoke</strong> model and set for major transformation in 2025. The model aims to deploy large, high-frequency vessels on main trunk routes while connecting feeder ports to smaller feeder operations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Habben Jansen emphasized that some strengths of this structure have emerged, but certain port calls also need to be revised:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"We will complete the review and then inform the market, but don't expect revolutionary changes."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">This suggests the review is more in the nature of <strong>operational fine-tuning</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Port Closures and Additions on the Gemini Agenda</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to the CEO's remarks, the review covers issues such as "where additional port calls might be needed" and "whether some existing stops can be removed."</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Gemini routes are particularly utilized on:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Asia–Europe,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Trans-Pacific</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">and Trans-Atlantic<br>
services.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Consequently, these adjustments will directly impact <strong>transit times</strong>, <strong>transhipment congestion</strong>, and <strong>feeder reliability</strong> across the global shipping network.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>No Return to Red Sea and Suez</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Habben Jansen reiterated once again that despite the Houthi ceasefire, the company <strong>has no plans to return to Red Sea and Suez Canal routes</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Given the persistent security risks in the region:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Transit times remain unstable,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Insurance costs are elevated,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Vessel and crew safety is at risk.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This is why Hapag-Lloyd, along with other major carriers such as Maersk, continues to favor the <strong>Cape of Good Hope</strong> route.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The company's criteria for returning:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Marine insurance costs normalize,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">High security guarantees,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Sustained reduction in regional risks.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Currently, these conditions have not been met.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Gemini Cooperation Focused on Sustainability</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Gemini Cooperation serves both carriers as a strong platform for:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>increasing on-vessel utilization rates</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>fine-tuning capacity through harmonized routes</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>improving fuel efficiency</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>achieving higher on-time delivery rates</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Given 2025's volatile market conditions (rate fluctuations, Cape of Good Hope routing dynamics, volatile demand in Asian markets), the Gemini network has become a <strong>stability-creating structure</strong> for both Hapag-Lloyd and Maersk.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Hapag-Lloyd is planning <strong>small optimizations</strong> to the Gemini network it operates with Maersk.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Some port calls will be added, others will be removed.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">CEO: "Don't expect revolution."</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The company has <strong>no plans to return</strong> to the Red Sea and Suez.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Gemini's objective: boost speed, efficiency, connection reliability, and on-time performance.</p></li>
</ul>
