<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Kuehne+Nagel</strong> has announced the opening of <strong>five new fulfilment centers</strong> in India, keeping pace with the country's rapid economic growth.<br>
The new facilities will be located in <strong>Gurgaon, Kolkata, Nagpur, Mumbai and Rajpura</strong>, adding a combined <strong>100,000 square meters of additional space</strong> and bringing the company's <strong>total warehousing capacity in India to approximately 500,000 m²</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This expansion initiative will create <strong>more than 1,500 new jobs</strong> across the country and <strong>contribute to local economic growth.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>India: At the Heart of a Strategic Growth Market</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>India</strong> is on track to become <strong>the world's third-largest economy by 2030</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
Strong growth in sectors such as <strong>high technology, automotive, consumer goods and healthcare</strong> is rapidly increasing demand for <strong>scalable and high-performance logistics infrastructure</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In response to this trend, Kuehne+Nagel is building <strong>a multi-layered logistics network providing regional access</strong> across different parts of the country.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>New Centers: Equipped with Intelligent Automation</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The newly opened fulfilment centers are equipped with <strong>advanced automation technologies</strong>.</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Telescopic conveyor systems</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>High-performance sorting systems</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">These systems increase the warehouses' <strong>peak order processing capacity by 75 percent.</strong><br>
This translates into <strong>faster, error-free and more flexible delivery processes</strong>, particularly in <strong>e-commerce, healthcare products and consumer goods</strong> sectors.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Geographic Distribution: Adapting to India's Industrial Decentralization</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Kuehne+Nagel's new investment locations reflect India's economic diversity:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Mumbai</strong> and <strong>Kolkata</strong>, as <strong>Tier-1</strong> cities, are strong demand centers,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Gurgaon</strong> and <strong>Nagpur</strong>, as <strong>Tier-2</strong> industrial hubs, are growing rapidly,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Rajpura</strong>, despite being a <strong>Tier-3</strong> city, is emerging as <strong>a rising hub in manufacturing and distribution activities</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This strategic positioning enables the company to establish an extensive supply chain network across <strong>both northern and western India</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Kuehne+Nagel Scaling Up Investment in Asia-Pacific</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Damian Raczynski, Senior Vice President of Contract Logistics at Kuehne+Nagel Asia-Pacific</strong>, commented on the investment:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"India is one of the key growth markets for Kuehne+Nagel.<br>
We invest where our customers are — this expansion strengthens our ability to deliver speed, reliability and flexibility, particularly in the consumer goods and healthcare sectors."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">The company is continuing its growth not only in India but also on a regional scale.<br>
In early 2025, it expanded its Asia network by opening <strong>a new 2,800 m² Container Freight Station (CFS) near Laem Chabang Port in Thailand</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Conclusion: Integrated Logistics Strength for Sustainable Growth</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Kuehne+Nagel's investment in India stands out as part of the country's <strong>logistics infrastructure transformation</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
The new fulfilment centers align with the company's sustainability goals through <strong>energy-efficient design, automation-driven processes</strong> and <strong>data-driven operations management</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This move marks an important milestone in Kuehne+Nagel's <strong>strategic expansion plans for the Asia-Pacific region</strong> — enabling it to deliver <strong>more agile, flexible and sustainable logistics solutions</strong> for both local and global customers.</p>
<p><br></p>
