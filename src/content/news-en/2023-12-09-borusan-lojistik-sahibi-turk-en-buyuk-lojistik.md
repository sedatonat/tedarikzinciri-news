<p><a href="https://www.borusanlojistik.com/tr"><u>Borusan Logistics</u></a> General Manager <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/serdarercal/"><u>Serdar Erçal</u></a> shared important information regarding 2023. He stated that the company aims to close 2023 with a revenue of 684 million dollars and emphasized that there is no other firm of this scale in terms of Turkish ownership. He also expressed that Borusan Logistics has a major objective in the technology field and that they aim to become the sector's largest technology company.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Borusan Logistics provides a wide range of services in the logistics field. These services include warehousing, distribution, customs clearance, transportation and more. He particularly highlighted their prominence in automotive logistics and their collaboration with new automotive brands entering Turkey.</p>
<p>Serdar Erçal provided information about the company's technology-focused <a href="https://www.etasimacilik.com/"><u>eTA platform</u></a> and Digital Wallet application. He explained that through these platforms, they have simplified operations for customers, truck drivers and logistics companies, and they manage a more efficient transportation network.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>In 2023, Borusan Logistics reported positive performance in domestic operations. However, he noted that they faced challenges in international operations due to the decline in global freight rate indices.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Making projections for 2024, Serdar Erçal stated that digitalization and sustainability would be at the center of business processes. He also noted that with increased investments in the automotive sector and new players entering the market, vehicle logistics operations have expanded, and they have created facilities suitable for electric vehicles. He emphasized that Borusan Logistics is focused on intermodal transportation and the eTA Platform.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Finally, he provided information about the "Truck Drivers Matter" project they developed for truck and lorry drivers.</p>
