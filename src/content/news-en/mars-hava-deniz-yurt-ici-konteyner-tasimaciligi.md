<p>Founded in 1997, Mars Air and Sea Cargo has begun offering domestic container transportation services. CEO Selmin Kahraman emphasized the importance of pre- and final-mile transportation services in air and sea freight for the company. With the new service aimed at streamlining customer operations, the company conducts 400 trips monthly and provides various services including full containers, general cargo, insured containers, and warehouse-to-rail transfer connections.
