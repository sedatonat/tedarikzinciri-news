<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DHL Express</strong> has launched a <strong>$90 million expansion investment</strong> to strengthen its presence in the <strong>New York metropolitan region</strong>, one of the United States' most intensive logistics hubs. The company announced it will relocate its <strong>Manhattan operations to a new, high-capacity facility in Jersey City, New Jersey</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new facility will become operational by <strong>the end of November 2025</strong> and will replace DHL's current <strong>Manhattan service center</strong>. The <strong>92,505 square-meter</strong> (approximately <strong>larger than one acre</strong>) new hub is positioned <strong>just 8 kilometers from Lower Manhattan</strong>. According to DHL's announcement, the facility will provide <strong>faster delivery, advanced automation, and sustainable vehicle infrastructure</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Capacity doubles</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new <strong>Jersey City</strong> hub will have <strong>double the sorting capacity</strong> of the current Manhattan facility. Thanks to advanced automation systems, <strong>package processing speed will increase from 3,000 to 6,000 per hour</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The facility will also feature:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Electric vehicle charging stations</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Dedicated courier access areas</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>New route planning and dispatch software</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">These improvements will enable drivers to get on the road faster, thereby <strong>increasing distribution efficiency.</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DHL Express USA CEO Greg Hewitt</strong> stated in his announcement:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"The New York region sits at the heart of our U.S. network and is one of the most critical gateways for international trade. This investment builds infrastructure that will support our customers' growing needs for many years to come."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Brooklyn and regional network investments</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The capacity expansion in the New York region is not limited to Jersey City alone. DHL has also expanded its <strong>Brooklyn facility to 70,202 square meters</strong>, nearly <strong>tripling the previous space</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
The <strong>automatic sorting capacity of this facility has also increased from 1,500 to 3,000 packages per hour</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to DHL officials, following these two major investments, the company will complete its network optimization project across <strong>Brooklyn, the Bronx, Queens, Long Island City, and Hauppauge</strong> regions. These steps will support objectives such as:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>load balancing</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>reducing pressure on high-demand facilities</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>shortening delivery times</strong><br>
and more.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>New flight route and integration</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">DHL has also announced the addition of a <strong>new direct flight route between Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky Airport</strong>, DHL's U.S. main air hub, and <strong>Newark Liberty International Airport</strong>. This connection will provide <strong>a closer air transportation network to the Jersey City hub</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Other DHL centers in the New York region will continue to maintain their connections through <strong>JFK Airport</strong>. The new route will <strong>shorten transit times</strong> and <strong>increase delivery reliability</strong> across the region.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Hewitt emphasized that this step will strengthen DHL's competitive advantage in <strong>complex and dense urban logistics environments</strong>:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"With this investment, we're not just increasing our capacity; we're redefining how we work smarter in one of the world's most complex logistics networks. Our new facilities and flight connections will enable us to manage growing shipment volumes faster, more reliably, and sustainably."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Sustainability focus</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new Jersey City hub aims to reduce its carbon footprint through <strong>electric vehicle infrastructure</strong>, <strong>smart energy systems</strong>, and <strong>automation-driven process management</strong>. With this investment, DHL indicates it is entering an important phase in the U.S. leg of its <strong>"zero-emission logistics" strategy</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to experts, DHL's move is part of a broader <strong>trend of competitors like UPS and FedEx restructuring their operations in the New York region</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DHL Express</strong> will relocate its Manhattan operations to Jersey City <strong>by November 2025.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Total investment: <strong>$90 million.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">New hub: <strong>92,505 m²</strong>, <strong>6,000 packages/hour capacity</strong> – a doubling increase.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Electric vehicle charging stations</strong> and <strong>new dispatch technologies</strong> will be deployed.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Brooklyn facility</strong> has been expanded to <strong>3 times its previous size with doubled capacity.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>New Cincinnati–Newark flight route</strong> will provide <strong>faster delivery</strong> in the New York region.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Objective: <strong>Leadership in efficiency, sustainability, and customer service quality.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
