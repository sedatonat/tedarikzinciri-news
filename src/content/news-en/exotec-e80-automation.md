<p>The CPG and grocery supply chains face ever-increasing expectations around speed, accuracy, and shelf freshness. In response, a new partnership between <strong>Exotec</strong> and <strong>E80 Group</strong> brings innovative architecture to warehouse automation. This model combines Exotec's multi-level <strong>Skypod shuttle/robot transport system</strong> with E80's <strong>expertise in palletizing, depaletizing, and internal logistics</strong>. The objective is to redefine the balance between accuracy, speed, and shelf freshness in complex order fulfillment for grocery retail.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>The new system strikes an optimal balance between mixed-case palletizing, unit picking accuracy, and speed—particularly for CPG operations with <strong>high SKU variety</strong> and <strong>frequent order frequency</strong>. An AI-driven <strong>orchestration system</strong> integrates seamlessly with the WMS/WES layer, managing demand fluctuations in real time. This maintains continuous and flexible product flow throughout the warehouse.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><strong>Freezer-rated robots</strong> and energy-efficient drives—developed for cold chain applications—sustain operational continuity even at low temperatures. <strong>Computer vision</strong> and <strong>weight sensors</strong> minimize errors in product verification processes. This approach improves both speed and accuracy levels.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><strong>Micro-fulfillment</strong> and <strong>back-of-store</strong> solutions enable inventory storage near retail locations. This approach reduces <strong>last-mile costs</strong> while significantly lowering operational effort per order. System-wide features such as <strong>redundant lifts</strong>, <strong>fault-tolerant routing</strong>, and <strong>charger scheduling</strong> limit the impact of single failures across the entire operation, enhancing operational resilience.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>On the supplier side, <strong>API-first integration architecture</strong> and <strong>digital twin simulations</strong> shorten implementation timelines. Additionally, <strong>heatmap</strong> and <strong>travel-time analytics</strong> continuously optimize slotting decisions. Financially, improvements in labor productivity, reduced order errors, and decreased product loss contribute directly to operating margins.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Through this collaboration, Exotec and E80 Group establish a <strong>scalable, energy-efficient, and flexible fulfillment standard tailored to CPG and grocery sectors</strong>. Fast-moving inventory near customers and rapid picking capabilities reshape logistics network design.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li><p>The Skypod + E80 combination delivers speed and accuracy.</p></li>
  <li><p>AI orchestration manages demand fluctuations.</p></li>
  <li><p>Freezer-rated robots support cold chain operations.</p></li>
  <li><p>Digital twin reduces implementation time.</p></li>
  <li><p>Micro-fulfillment lowers last-mile costs.</p></li>
</ul>
