<p>Turkey's two leading courier companies, Sendeo and Kolay Gelsin, have completed a formal merger process. The combination was approved by the Competition Authority on May 29. Sendeo was owned by Koç Holding, while Kolay Gelsin was owned by Ahmet Musul. Going forward, both companies will operate under the "Kolay Gelsin" name as equal partners. The merger is viewed as a significant shift in the cargo and logistics sector, which has accelerated since the pandemic, and creates a major force that will reshape industry dynamics in Turkey.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Points</h5>\n<ul>\n  <li><p>Turkey's two major courier companies, Sendeo and Kolay Gelsin, have officially merged.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The merger was approved by the Competition Authority.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Koç Holding and Ahmet Musul, the owners of both companies, will manage the new company as equal partners.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The new company will operate under the "Kolay Gelsin" name.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The merger further strengthens the combined entity's importance in the post-pandemic cargo and logistics sector.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link: </strong><a href="https://www.lojiport.com/iki-dev-birlesti-turkiyenin-en-buyuk-kargo-sirketi-dogdu-116594h.htm"><u>https://www.lojiport.com/iki-dev-birlesti-turkiyenin-en-buyuk-kargo-sirketi-dogdu-116594h.htm</u></a></p>
