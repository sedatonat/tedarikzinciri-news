<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Hyster</strong>, a leading provider of material handling equipment, is supplying an electric empty container handler to <strong>Malta Freeport Terminals</strong>. This move enhances the terminal's operational efficiency while reducing its environmental footprint. Electric handlers deliver lower emissions and operating costs compared to their diesel counterparts, with reduced maintenance requirements as well. <strong>Hyster</strong>'s initiative demonstrates its commitment to sustainable logistics solutions. As one of the Mediterranean's leading transshipment hubs, <strong>Malta Freeport Terminals</strong> is further elevating service quality with this innovative equipment.</p>
