<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Lynxis LLC</strong>, a provider of optimization solutions for land-based logistics, has acquired <strong>TEDIVO LLC</strong>, which develops software that streamlines vessel operations at marine terminals. This strategic move aims to strengthen <strong>Lynxis</strong>'s capabilities in digitalization, helping shipping lines and terminal operators reduce vessel delays, incorrect stowage, and security risks.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Lynxis LLC</strong> CEO Larry Cuddy Jr. highlighted the synergy between the two companies, stating: "The <strong>TEDIVO</strong> platform, particularly <strong>BAPLIE Viewer Online</strong> and its ancillary products, aligns perfectly with our mission to deliver next-generation innovative solutions that quickly create value for our customers."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>BAPLIE Viewer Online</strong>, <strong>TEDIVO</strong>'s flagship product, simplifies the interpretation of <strong>BAPLIE</strong> files. This web-based platform enables maritime professionals to visualize cargo data in real time without requiring manual processes or complex installations, thereby enhancing operational accuracy and efficiency.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, <strong>TEDIVO</strong>'s <strong>Vessel Designer</strong> and <strong>Library</strong> products allow users to create and share vessel definitions compatible with various terminal systems using an open-source JSON format. The integration of these products with <strong>BAPLIE Viewer Online</strong> aims to enable seamless validation of stowage plans against vessel specifications.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>TEDIVO LLC</strong> founder Mark Traynor stated: "Our mission at <strong>TEDIVO</strong> has always been to simplify and improve maritime operations. Joining <strong>Lynxis LLC</strong> enables us to expand the reach of our solutions and continue innovating for the global maritime industry."</p>
<p><br></p>

  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>TEDIVO</strong>'s <strong>BAPLIE Viewer Online</strong> product enables real-time visualization of cargo data.</p></li>

  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Integration between <strong>Lynxis</strong> and <strong>TEDIVO</strong> aims to deliver fast and innovative solutions to customers.</p></li>
