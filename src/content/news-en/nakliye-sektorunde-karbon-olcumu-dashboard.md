<p>PSA BDP has launched a new data service called "Carbon Dashboard" as part of its logistics and transportation offerings. The service provides shippers with a tool to accurately measure carbon emissions generated during their transportation processes. This enables companies to gain a clearer picture of greenhouse gas emissions monitoring and more effectively achieve their sustainability targets. The tool helps consolidate emissions reports across various carriers and forwarders, overcoming challenges such as data inconsistencies arising from the use of different tools and methodologies.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Points:</h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p><strong>New Data Service</strong>: The "Carbon Dashboard" launched by PSA BDP introduces a new approach to measuring carbon emissions.</p></li>\n  <li><p><strong>Sustainability Targets</strong>: The tool facilitates companies in achieving their sustainability goals.</p></li>\n  <li><p><strong>Data Inconsistencies</strong>: It enables consistent processing of data from various sources.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n
