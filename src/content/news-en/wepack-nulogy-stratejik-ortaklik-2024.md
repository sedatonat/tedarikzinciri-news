<p><a href="https://www.we-pack.co.uk/"><u>WePack</u></a>, an independent contract packing specialist, has announced a partnership with software provider <a href="https://nulogy.com/"><u>Nulogy</u></a> at the <a href="https://www.packagingbirmingham.com/"><u>Packaging Innovations & Empack 2024</u></a> event, as it focuses on increasing the agility and resilience of its operations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>WePack identified a need for a digital solution to enhance agility and responsiveness, and Nulogy's <a href="https://nulogy.com/solutions/shop-floor/"><u>Shop Floor Solution</u></a> emerged as the ideal choice for the business. Nulogy was viewed as fundamental to meeting growing demand as brands increasingly rely on external partners for high-quality, innovative, and cost-effective product delivery, given that Nulogy was specifically designed for the complex procedures of contract packing operations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>With over 30 years of industry experience, WePack is a leading packing services provider working with recognized brands in the food, cosmetics, premium consumer products, and agriculture sectors. Offering comprehensive services such as bottling, sachet and pouch filling, manual assembly projects, reprocessing, and correction services, WePack is recognized for its in-house design team with a progressive approach to navigating the complexities of the contract packing industry.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h3>Key Takeaways:</h3>
<ul>
  <li><p><a href="https://www.we-pack.co.uk/"><u>WePack</u></a> and <a href="https://nulogy.com/"><u>Nulogy</u></a> are focusing on digital solutions to enhance operational agility and resilience.</p></li>
  <li><p><a href="https://nulogy.com/"><u>Nulogy</u></a>'s <a href="https://nulogy.com/solutions/shop-floor/"><u>Shop Floor Solution</u></a> addresses the specific needs of contract packing processes.</p></li>
  <li><p><a href="https://www.we-pack.co.uk/"><u>WePack</u></a> has over 30 years of experience in the industry and works with recognized brands across multiple sectors.</p></li>
  <li><p><a href="https://nulogy.com/"><u>Nulogy</u></a> launched its 'Essential Package' at the <a href="https://www.packagingbirmingham.com/"><u>Packaging Innovations & Empack 2024</u></a> event, offering a solution tailored for smaller contract packing operations.</p></li>
</ul>
