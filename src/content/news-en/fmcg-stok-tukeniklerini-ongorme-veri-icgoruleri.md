<p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast-moving_consumer_goods"><u>FMCG (Fast-Moving Consumer Goods)</u></a> sector has significant growth potential, with its current value of 10 trillion dollars expected to reach 15 trillion dollars by 2025. However, recent disruptions—including the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/COVID-19"><u>COVID-19</u></a> pandemic, the blockade of the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suez_Canal"><u>Suez Canal</u></a>, and port congestion—have further complicated the sector's complex supply chains and unpredictable demand patterns. These challenges have highlighted the fragility of the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast-moving_consumer_goods"><u>FMCG</u></a> sector, leading to unmet customer demand and jeopardizing operational resilience.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>In this context, a comprehensive project aims to identify the primary factors leading to low case fill rates (CFR) in <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast-moving_consumer_goods"><u>FMCG</u></a> companies and develop predictive models to improve future CFR performance. The project offers a transformative approach to CFR management using data-driven insights, targeting increased sales, customer loyalty, and overall resilience across the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast-moving_consumer_goods"><u>FMCG</u></a> landscape.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5>Key Takeaways</h5>
<ul>
  <li><p>The <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast-moving_consumer_goods"><u>FMCG</u></a> sector is expected to reach 15 trillion dollars by 2025.</p></li>
  <li><p>The sector has faced disruptions including the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/COVID-19"><u>COVID-19</u></a> pandemic and the blockade of the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Suez_Canal"><u>Suez Canal</u></a>.</p></li>
  <li><p>Low CFR can lead to lost sales, erosion of customer loyalty, and potential contract breaches.</p></li>
  <li><p>The project followed a three-phase methodology: business understanding, modeling, and validation.</p></li>
  <li><p>Forecast accuracy and demand variability have been identified as key drivers of CFR.</p></li>
  <li><p>Hybrid models and advanced time series machine learning models have demonstrated high accuracy and precision in CFR prediction.</p></li>
</ul>
