<p style="text-align: justify">German packaging machinery manufacturer Hugo Beck will launch its new sleeve wrapper for sustainable transport packaging under the name 'paper S' at interpack, taking place 7-13 May 2026 in Düsseldorf. The company will display the product at stand A74 in hall 11 and will share kraft paper-based alternative packaging solutions with visitors.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify">Paper S is positioned as a compact sleeve wrapper that uses kraft paper wrapping instead of conventional plastic stretch film. The machine has been designed to meet the growing demand for sustainable packaging in e-commerce, logistics hubs, and distribution operations. As kraft paper is recyclable and biodegradable, it makes it easier for operators to align with corporate ESG goals.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify">Single-use plastic reduction targets set for 2030 under the European Union's Packaging and Packaging Waste Regulation (PPWR) are rapidly expanding the market share of paper-based solutions. With paper S, Hugo Beck aims to become a key part of the machinery ecosystem supporting this transition.</p>
