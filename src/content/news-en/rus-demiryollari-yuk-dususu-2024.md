<p style="text-align: justify"><a href="https://eng.rzd.ru/"><u><strong>Russian Railways (RZD)</strong></u></a>, experienced the steepest decline in 15 years, registering a 4.1% drop in loading volumes, equivalent to a reduction of 51 million tons. The company completed a total of 1,181 million tons of loading in 2024, falling short of its initial target of 1,265 million tons.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Coal transportation in particular saw a 5.4% decline, representing approximately one-third of <a href="https://eng.rzd.ru/"><u><strong>RZD</strong></u></a>'s total freight. Non-ferrous metals declined by 9.3%, while construction materials fell 14.5%. In contrast, container transportation rose 1.1%, cokeable coal increased 2.3%, and fertilizers grew 6.2%.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><a href="https://eng.rzd.ru/"><u><strong>RZD</strong></u></a> faces multiple challenges. Locomotive maintenance difficulties persist due to spare parts shortages and sanctions, while a shortage of qualified personnel compounds the problems. The company reported a shortfall of 2,500 locomotive operators.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">These issues have also affected <a href="https://eng.rzd.ru/"><u><strong>RZD</strong></u></a>'s investment plans. The planned 12.41 trillion rubles investment program for 2025-2030 faces a 36% reduction, being cut to 7.93 trillion rubles. In particular, the investment program for developing railway infrastructure in the eastern corridor, known as the Eastern Polygon, faces a cut of 1.55 trillion rubles.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, <a href="https://eng.rzd.ru/"><u><strong>RZD</strong></u></a>'s net profit fell 90% below expectations. The company's 2024 financial results came in well below projected figures.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Personnel and locomotive shortages are negatively affecting <a href="https://eng.rzd.ru/"><u><strong>RZD</strong></u></a>'s operational capacity. Approximately 42,600 train services were cancelled or delayed in 2023 due to maintenance deficiencies. This situation severely constrains the company's freight transport capacity.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Moreover, container traffic restrictions are being imposed due to terminal congestion in the Moscow region. This situation is leading to extended delivery times and increased operational difficulties.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><a href="https://eng.rzd.ru/"><u><strong>RZD</strong></u></a> is resorting to investment cuts to manage current problems. The planned investments for 2025 are set to be reduced by 36.7%, being cut down to 834 billion rubles. These cuts will particularly impact major infrastructure projects.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In conclusion, <a href="https://eng.rzd.ru/"><u><strong>Russian Railways</strong></u></a> faces a serious crisis due to sanctions, personnel and locomotive shortages, investment cuts, and operational challenges. This situation could adversely affect Russia's eastern trade objectives and overall economic performance.</p>
