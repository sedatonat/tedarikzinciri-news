The Isa Star, flying a Panama flag, is operated and managed by Azimuth Ship Management, headquartered in the United Arab Emirates. Azimuth Ship Management is known as an experienced firm in the maritime sector with the capacity to respond swiftly to emergencies of this nature.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Detailed investigations are ongoing into why the fire started in the engine room and exactly how the flooding aboard the vessel occurred.
