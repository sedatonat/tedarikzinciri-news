<p style="text-align: justify">Increasingly complex supply chains, mounting cost pressures, and sustainability mandates are forcing companies to rethink their packaging processes. <strong>On-demand, right-sized packaging technologies</strong> stand at the heart of this pressure. Modern automation solutions from Packsize have become a powerful transformation tool for companies looking to both <strong>reduce costs</strong> and <strong>scale operations</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Below is a summary of five key benefits of the right-sized packaging approach and how Packsize supports this transformation.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>1. Higher Throughput: Eliminating Packaging Bottlenecks</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Packaging is often the weakest link in fulfillment centers, especially during peak periods.</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Manual stations can handle <strong>just a few dozen packages per hour</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">while automated systems can reach <strong>1,000+ packages per hour</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This difference <strong>reduces delays</strong> during peak seasons, <strong>accelerates delivery speed</strong>, and boosts customer satisfaction.</p>
<p><br>
Additionally, automation integration with WMS simplifies KPI tracking, strengthening operational visibility.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>2. Cutting DIM Costs: Eliminating "Empty Air" Charges</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Current carrier pricing accounts not only for package weight but also for <strong>volumetric weight (DIM weight)</strong>. As a result, incorrectly sized boxes force businesses to pay premium rates for <strong>shipping empty air</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">On-demand packaging machines minimize DIM penalties by producing <strong>perfectly sized boxes for each product</strong>.<br>
Benefits include:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Lower freight costs,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">More efficient vehicle utilization,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Fewer shipments ⇒ <strong>lower carbon emissions</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>3. Reducing Material Consumption: Less Cardboard, Less Padding</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Oversized boxes increase not only shipping costs but also cardboard and cushioning material consumption.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">With right-sized packaging:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Cardboard consumption drops significantly,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Cushioning material needs fall by <strong>80–90 percent</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Waste decreases while costs decline.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Some systems further strengthen environmental responsibility by <strong>converting unused cardboard into cushioning material</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>4. Reducing Labor Requirements: More Productivity with Less Work</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Traditional packaging stations require large teams. Repetitive tasks such as measuring, cutting, and adding cushioning are both time-consuming and costly.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Automated machines:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Can reduce packaging team needs by <strong>up to 50 percent</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Redeploy staff to more valuable work,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Provide significant flexibility in warehouses facing labor shortages.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Result: The same team can accomplish <strong>significantly more work</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>5. Freeing Warehouse Space: One Raw Material Instead of Hundreds of Box Sizes</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Companies waste warehouse space by stocking hundreds of different box sizes. Some of these inventory items become "<strong>dead stock</strong>," leading to both space and financial losses.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">With on-demand machines:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Only <strong>die-cut cardboard rolls</strong> need to be stocked,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Box variety drops from hundreds of pallets to just a few,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Warehouse space is used more efficiently.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Packsize's Role: Sustainability + Financial Returns</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Packsize delivers a comprehensive transformation by addressing both cost reduction and environmental impact reduction objectives simultaneously.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Key benefits include:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Lower DIM costs,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Reduced material consumption,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Smaller carbon footprint,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">More efficient and more profitable warehouses,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Industry-specific solutions (furniture, building materials, manufacturing, e-commerce).</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Packsize technology has been cited as a sustainability success story in ESG reports from companies such as <strong>Polaris</strong> and <strong>Dick's Sporting Goods</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In conclusion, properly sized, on-demand packaging<br>
 <strong>increases operational efficiency, controls costs, and makes sustainability a natural part of business operations.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Right-sized packaging significantly boosts throughput.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">DIM costs and freight expenses decline.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Material consumption and waste drop by 80 percent or more.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Labor requirements fall while productivity rises.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Warehouse space is used more efficiently; dead stock is eliminated.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Packsize delivers a cross-industry solution providing both <strong>environmental and financial ROI</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
