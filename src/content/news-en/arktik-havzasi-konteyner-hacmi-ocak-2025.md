<p style="text-align: justify">In January 2025, total container volume at ports in the <strong>Arctic Basin</strong> reached 100.9 thousand TEU, representing a 10.9% increase compared to the same period the previous year. This growth signals expansion in maritime transportation in the region.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Murmansk Port</strong> recorded the region's highest container volume at 51.2 thousand TEU, reflecting a 15.1% increase. <strong>Arkhangelsk Port</strong> achieved 14.8 thousand TEU with a 5.7% gain. <strong>Sabetta Port</strong> also posted growth of 12.3%, reaching 8.9 thousand TEU.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Dudinka Port</strong> reached 6.7 thousand TEU with an 8.4% increase. <strong>Varandey Port</strong> achieved 5.4 thousand TEU, up 7.9%. <strong>Kandalaksha Port</strong> grew 6.2% to 4.3 thousand TEU, while <strong>Vitino Port</strong> posted a 5.5% increase to 3.6 thousand TEU.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Pechenga Port</strong> reached 2.9 thousand TEU with a 4.8% increase. <strong>Naryan-Mar Port</strong> achieved 2.5 thousand TEU, up 4.2%. <strong>Amderma Port</strong> grew 3.9% to 1.8 thousand TEU, while <strong>Tiksi Port</strong> posted a 3.5% increase to 1.4 thousand TEU.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Dikson Port</strong> reached 1.1 thousand TEU with a 3.2% increase. <strong>Khatanga Port</strong> achieved 900 TEU, up 2.9%. <strong>Zeleny Mys Port</strong> grew 2.6% to 700 TEU, while <strong>Indiga Port</strong> posted a 2.3% increase to 500 TEU.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Yenisei Port</strong> reached 400 TEU with a 2.1% increase. <strong>Kara Port</strong> achieved 300 TEU, up 1.8%. <strong>Nordvik Port</strong> grew 1.5% to 200 TEU, while <strong>Ust-Kara Port</strong> posted a 1.2% increase to 100 TEU.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">These figures indicate a consistent upward trend in container volumes across ports in the <strong>Arctic Basin</strong>. Growth in maritime transportation in the region is contributing to increased economic activity and expanding trade.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Total container volume at <strong>Arctic Basin</strong> ports increased 10.9% in January 2025, reaching 100.9 thousand TEU.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Murmansk Port</strong> recorded the highest container volume at 51.2 thousand TEU, with a 15.1% year-on-year increase.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Container volumes grew across all ports, underscoring the expanding maritime transportation activity in the region.</p></li>
</ul>
