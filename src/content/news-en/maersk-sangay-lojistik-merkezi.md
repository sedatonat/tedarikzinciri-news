<p style="text-align: justify">A.P. Moller–Maersk has formally opened its <strong>$140 million flagship logistics center</strong> in Shanghai's Lin-gang district, marking one of the company's largest warehouse investments on a global scale. The facility, built on a <strong>113,000 square-meter site</strong>, offers a total of <strong>147,000 square meters of storage capacity</strong> and aims to make trade flows between China and the Asia-Pacific region faster, more flexible, and more resilient.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Maersk CEO <strong>Vincent Clerc</strong> highlighted at the opening that China is both the world's largest exporter and one of the most important consumer markets:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"This center strengthens our omnichannel fulfilment capabilities and further connects China to international markets."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">The opening ceremony was attended by local government representatives, Maersk executives, and key customers. This participation demonstrates the center's strategic importance for regional supply chains.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Shanghai's role in the global supply chain strengthens further</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The Lin-gang region is a rapidly growing center in Shanghai's trade and manufacturing ecosystem. The center's proximity to <strong>Yangshan Deep Water Port</strong> enables Maersk's ocean, air, and land services to work in an integrated manner, delivering:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">faster deliveries,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">lower costs,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">higher operational visibility<br>
.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This integration exemplifies Maersk's "one network – one solution" strategy.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Four core capabilities: export, import, global hub, and e-commerce</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The center brings together four fundamental services in its omnichannel fulfilment architecture:</p>
<ol>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Export distribution</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Import distribution</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Regional and global hub operations</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Cross-border e-commerce logistics</strong></p></li>
</ol>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Temperature-controlled storage</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Specialized packaging and value-added services</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Customized B2B and B2C models</strong><br>
are also offered as differentiated functions.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Fast customs clearance with AEO certification</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Since security and speed are critical for high-volume import-export operations, Maersk leverages <strong>AEO (Authorised Economic Operator)</strong> certification at the center to deliver:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">expedited customs clearance,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">lower inspection rates,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">unified management of <strong>bonded + non-bonded</strong> operations under one roof<br>
.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This represents a significant advantage for reducing operational costs, particularly for brands engaged in cross-border e-commerce.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A new era of unified inventory management for brands</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Maersk Greater China General Manager <strong>Silvia Ding</strong> noted that the center directly responds to companies' growing omni-channel needs:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Brands are demanding unified inventory management across exports, imports, and transit shipments. This center enables our customers to meet their B2B and B2C requirements with high efficiency."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to Ding, the center will become a "multi-layered fulfilment node" that will alleviate the burden on both regional and global distribution centers.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Highlights:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Maersk opened a <strong>$140 million</strong> logistics center in Shanghai Lin-gang.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The facility spans <strong>113,000 m²</strong> and offers <strong>147,000 m² of storage</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Ocean-air-land services are integrated into a single network.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">AEO-certified operations manage bonded + non-bonded business under one roof.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Four main functions: export, import, global hub, e-commerce.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The center strengthens China's role in the global supply chain.</p></li>
</ul>
