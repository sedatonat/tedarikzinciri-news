<p>Following a report in a German newspaper about arsenic-contaminated water at the Bou Azzer mine, owned by Moroccan company Managem, which is also a supplier to BMW, the automaker has launched an independent audit of the mine.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Although no issues have been reported so far, the audit is continuing in the context of environmental standards, according to reports.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>BMW sources cobalt from this mine for batteries produced in its electric vehicles.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n
