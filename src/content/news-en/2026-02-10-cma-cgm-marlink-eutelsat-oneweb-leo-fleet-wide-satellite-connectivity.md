<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>CMA CGM Group</strong>, Marlink and Eutelsat will deploy OneWeb LEO connectivity across CMA CGM's global fleet, integrating it with existing GEO and LEO networks through <strong>Marlink's XChange NextGen platform</strong>. Under the multi-year agreement, more than <strong>300 vessels</strong> will be equipped with OneWeb LEO services over the next nine months. Delivered and integrated by Marlink, the solution layers multi-orbit satellite networks to provide <strong>high-resilience, high-throughput, low-latency connectivity</strong> at an unprecedented fleet scale.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The combination enables operational efficiency, real-time data exchange and crew services comparable to shore-based digital performance. The system also supports CMA CGM's decarbonisation goals: <strong>routes, speeds and fuel consumption</strong> can be optimised in real time. Built on Marlink's <strong>XChange NextGen edge cloud platform</strong>, the architecture provides a central backbone for application deployment and data analytics on vessels.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">For CMA CGM, a <strong>persistent connectivity</strong> architecture in which the vessel almost feels onshore is a critical infrastructure cornerstone for advanced digitalisation applications. The connectivity can power predictive maintenance, digital-twin applications and AI-driven route optimisation. The <strong>multi-orbit</strong> architecture moves shipping into a new connectivity era beyond traditional satellite.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong><br>
1. CMA CGM, Marlink and Eutelsat will deploy OneWeb LEO connectivity to more than 300 vessels.<br>
2. Hardware rollout will complete within 9 months; Marlink XChange NextGen will deliver multi-orbit integration.<br>
3. The architecture provides high resilience, high throughput and low latency.<br>
4. The system supports decarbonisation goals through real-time optimisation of routes, speeds and fuel consumption.<br>
5. The edge cloud platform provides the backbone for predictive maintenance, digital twin and AI route optimisation.</p>
