<p>Toward the end of 2023, Darcy MacClaren, Chief Revenue Officer at SAP, is expecting the impact of AI in 2024.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>AI Setting New Standards: </strong>In 2024, AI-powered digital assistants will provide rapid responses across the supply chain. For example, an employee can ask an AI assistant which truck should be unloaded first. This will deliver numerous improvements in the supply chain, from speed to cost reduction.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>The New Backbone of Supply Chains: </strong>AI will form the foundation of supply chains in 2024. Businesses will use AI to respond more quickly to potential disruptions and strengthen their supply chains. AI will be integrated into every layer of the supply chain network, transforming logistics.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>Sustainable Supply Chains: </strong>In 2024, sustainable supply chains will become the norm. Businesses will invest in technology to reduce environmental impact. This will deliver both innovation and long-term profitability.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>2023-12-11</p>\n
