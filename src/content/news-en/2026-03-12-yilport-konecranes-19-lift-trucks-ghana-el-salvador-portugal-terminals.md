<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>CoreX Ports & Terminals / YILPORT Holding Inc.</strong> has added <strong>five reach stackers, five empty container handlers, and nine forklifts</strong> to boost container handling efficiency at terminals in <strong>Ghana, El Salvador, and Portugal</strong>. The trucks, ordered in batches throughout 2025, are now in service. YILPORT operates <strong>22 marine and 5 inland terminals</strong> across 12 countries and relies on modern container handling equipment to meet strict customer deadlines in challenging environments.</p><p style="text-align: justify">To maintain performance, the operator replaced older equipment at four terminals with <strong>19 new Konecranes lift trucks</strong>, ensuring safe, efficient operations and maximum uptime. YILPORT Holding Deputy CEO & CMO <strong>Erhan Çiloğlu</strong> said: "We appreciate Konecranes' continued participation in YILPORT's equipment purchase tenders and their responsiveness to our operational requirements."</p><p style="text-align: justify">According to Çiloğlu, the added reach stackers, empty container handlers, and forklifts will directly boost efficiency in container operations. The new equipment features Konecranes' latest designs offering higher safety standards, lower fuel consumption, and improved ergonomics for operators. YILPORT continues its strategic partnership with Konecranes as part of its fleet renewal strategy in Latin America, Africa, and Europe.</p>
