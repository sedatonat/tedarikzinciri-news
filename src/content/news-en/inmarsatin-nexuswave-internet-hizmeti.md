<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Inmarsat Maritime</strong>, a part of <strong>Viasat</strong>, has demonstrated maximum download speeds of 330–340 megabits per second (Mbps) and maximum upload speeds of 70–80 Mbps in real-world trials of its <strong>NexusWave</strong> service developed for the maritime sector. During these tests, average download speeds reached 120–150 Mbps and average upload speeds of 20–45 Mbps, with network availability on vessels using the <strong>NexusWave</strong> service exceeding 99.99%.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>NexusWave</strong> combines multiple subnetworks into a single unified connection, unlike the load balancing approach typically used by hybrid connectivity solutions, which provide bandwidth from the best available subnetwork. This approach allows applications to benefit from the total capacity of all available networks rather than remaining tied to a single network. Through its unique network bonding technology, <strong>NexusWave</strong> delivers superior speeds along with high reliability and redundancy.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">In real-world trials, the quality of a video conference was virtually unaffected by the availability or quality of any single subnetwork. <strong>NexusWave</strong> dynamically adjusts traffic routing to provide seamless and uninterrupted service. This enhances operational efficiency in the maritime sector and meets crew communication needs.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Inmarsat Maritime</strong> President <strong>Ben Palmer</strong> stated, "The ability to achieve 340 Mbps speeds stems from the power of the network bonding technology within <strong>NexusWave</strong>: no individual subnetwork in the package can achieve these speeds on its own. With <strong>NexusWave</strong>, <strong>Inmarsat</strong>'s maritime customers secure the capacity of multiple solutions with the convenience of a single provider."</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>NexusWave</strong> delivers "connected confidence" by offering unlimited data, global coverage, and enterprise-grade firewall protection. Additionally, <strong>Inmarsat Maritime</strong> experts provide round-the-clock technical support to customers worldwide. As a unified service, the solution guarantees managed performance levels regardless of the vessel's location or requirements, and provides complete transparency regarding total cost of ownership.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Looking ahead, <strong>NexusWave</strong> plans to further increase combined connection speeds by integrating next-generation ultra-high-capacity, high-speed service following the deployment of <strong>ViaSat-3</strong> Ka-band service. This will mean higher data speeds and more reliable connectivity for the maritime sector.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Highlights:</strong></p>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>NexusWave</strong> achieved maximum download speeds of 330–340 Mbps and maximum upload speeds of 70–80 Mbps in real-world trials.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Average download speeds were recorded at 120–150 Mbps, with average upload speeds of 20–45 Mbps.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Network availability on vessels using the <strong>NexusWave</strong> service exceeded 99.99%.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>NexusWave</strong> combines multiple subnetworks into a single unified connection, delivering high speed, reliability, and redundancy.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">In the future, integration of <strong>ViaSat-3</strong> Ka-band service is planned to further increase combined connection speeds.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>---</p>\n<p><strong>News Links:</strong></p>\n<ul>\n  <li><p><a href="https://www.porttechnology.org/news/inmarsat-maritimes-connectivity-service-hits-340-mbps/"><u>https://www.porttechnology.org/news/inmarsat-maritimes-connectivity-service-hits-340-mbps/</u></a></p></li>\n  <li><p><a href="https://gcaptain.com/inmarsat-verifies-unparalleled-340-mbps-nexuswave-download-speeds-to-demonstrate-power-of-network-bonding-for-maritime-market/"><u>https://gcaptain.com/inmarsat-verifies-unparalleled-340-mbps-nexuswave-download-speeds-to-demonstrate-power-of-network-bonding-for-maritime-market/</u></a></p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>-------------------</p>\n<p><strong>!!!
