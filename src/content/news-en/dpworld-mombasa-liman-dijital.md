<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DP World</strong> has begun deploying a <strong>Port Community System (PCS)</strong> in Kenya to increase trade efficiency and strengthen transparency.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This digital infrastructure is regarded as <strong>a significant milestone in the modernization of Mombasa Port</strong>. The country, surrounded by more than 12 landlocked nations in East and Central Africa, routes a large portion of its foreign trade through this port.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Shared Digital Platform for Public and Private Sector</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The PCS was jointly developed with <strong>EMEA Port Logistics</strong> and implemented in collaboration with the <strong>Kenya Ports Authority (KPA)</strong> and the <strong>Kenyan Government</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
Although DP World does not operate Mombasa Port, the system will serve <strong>both public and private sector users</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new platform provides importers, exporters, customs brokers, freight forwarders, and logistics firms with tools including <strong>cargo tracking, gate reservations, invoicing, payment processing, and real-time updates</strong> under a single platform.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>30% Faster Customs and Operational Efficiency</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Once the system is fully operational, <strong>customs processing times for cargo are expected to be reduced by up to 30%</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
This will deliver significant time savings in transaction processing for <strong>more than 3,000 users annually</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">For the <strong>Kenya Ports Authority (KPA)</strong>, the PCS will facilitate <strong>streamlining gate operations, better control of cargo movements</strong>, and <strong>reducing dwell times at the port</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
This will strengthen Mombasa's position as <strong>a strategic maritime hub connecting East and Central Africa to global markets</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Digital Transformation Push in African Trade</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">DP World's PCS investment is part of its strategy to accelerate digital commerce and logistics transformation in Africa.</p>
<p><br>
The company aims to strengthen connections between ports through digital platforms, thereby advancing <strong>supply chain integration across the continent</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DP World</strong> is a global logistics and supply chain company headquartered in <strong>Dubai, United Arab Emirates</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
It operates ports, terminals, industrial parks, and logistics services in <strong>more than 60 countries</strong> worldwide.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>DP World</strong> has deployed a <strong>Port Community System (PCS)</strong> in Kenya.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The system aims to improve <strong>Mombasa Port</strong> efficiency and accelerate customs processing by up to 30%.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The PCS provides importers, exporters, transporters, and customs brokers with <strong>tracking, payment, and notification capabilities from a single platform</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>Kenya Ports Authority (KPA)</strong> plans to reduce <strong>dwell time</strong> through digitalization of gate operations.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Mombasa will strengthen its position as <strong>a digital logistics hub for East and Central Africa</strong> through this system.</p></li>
</ul>
