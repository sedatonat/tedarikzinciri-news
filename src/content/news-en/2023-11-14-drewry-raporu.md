<p>According to Drewry's November 14 report, all Shanghai-based routes — with the exception of Shanghai-Rotterdam — have recorded a year-to-date decline of -40%.<br>
<br>
Despite remaining stable over the past week, the downward trend is expected to continue.<br>
<br>
The shipment of automobiles in containers is driven in part by the need to return empty containers.<br>
<br>
We continue to monitor developments.</p>
