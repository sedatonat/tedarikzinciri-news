<p><a href="https://www.evyapport.com/"><u>Evyapport</u></a> is adopting <a href="https://www.portchain.com/portchain-connect/"><u>Portchain Connect</u></a> to enhance berth coordination with customers in terms of quality and speed through digital handshakes and secure data sharing. The company is the first Turkish container terminal to join the <a href="https://www.portchain.com/portchain-connect/"><u>Portchain Connect</u></a> Network.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><a href="https://www.portchain.com/portchain-connect/"><u>Portchain Connect</u></a> is expected to enable <a href="https://www.evyapport.com/"><u>Evyapport</u></a> to receive real-time schedule and move count updates directly from carrier systems, respond quickly, and make counter-proposals, thereby aligning the vessel schedule with the terminal berth plan.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><a href="https://www.evyapport.com/"><u>Evyapport</u></a> Container Terminal Manager <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/kayhan-alipasaoglu-411544142/"><u>Kayhan Alipaşaoğlu</u></a> said, "<a href="https://www.evyapport.com/"><u>Evyapport</u></a> is the first Turkish container terminal to join the <a href="https://www.portchain.com/portchain-connect/"><u>Portchain Connect</u></a> Network," and added, "We hope our new collaboration with <a href="https://www.portchain.com/"><u>Portchain</u></a> will be inspiring for all Turkish ports."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><a href="https://www.portchain.com/"><u>Portchain</u></a>'s Chief Commercial Officer and Co-founder <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/thor-thorup-06708132/"><u>Thor Thorup</u></a> stated, "We are excited to work with <a href="https://www.evyapport.com/"><u>Evyapport</u></a> and aim to help them improve efficiency in berth coordination processes by facilitating data exchange."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Late last year, <a href="https://www.portchain.com/"><u>Portchain</u></a> announced that <a href="https://www.adports.ae/ports-terminals/terminals/csp-abu-dhabi-terminal/"><u>CSP Abu Dhabi Terminal</u></a> (CSPADT) would join the <a href="https://www.portchain.com/portchain-connect/"><u>Portchain Connect</u></a> network to simplify the berth coordination process with shipping lines. Earlier this year, it was announced that <a href="https://dchc.com.eg/"><u>Damietta Container &amp; Cargo Handling Company (DCHC)</u></a> joined the network.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5>Key Highlights</h5>
<ul>
  <li><p><a href="https://www.evyapport.com/"><u>Evyapport</u></a> is the first Turkish container terminal to join the <a href="https://www.portchain.com/portchain-connect/"><u>Portchain Connect</u></a> Network.</p></li>
  <li><p><a href="https://www.portchain.com/portchain-connect/"><u>Portchain Connect</u></a> will provide real-time schedule and move count updates.</p></li>
  <li><p>The partnership aims to increase efficiency and speed in berth coordination.</p></li>
  <li><p><a href="https://www.portchain.com/"><u>Portchain</u></a> adopts a continuous improvement approach and is open to user feedback.</p></li>
</ul>
