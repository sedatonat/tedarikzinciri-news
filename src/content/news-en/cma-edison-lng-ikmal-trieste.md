<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM and Edison have successfully completed <strong>Italy's first LNG ship-to-ship (STS) bunkering operation</strong> at the Port of Trieste. This operation is regarded as a strategic turning point in the development of the country's LNG fuel bunkering infrastructure.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The bunkering took place aboard the LNG-fueled container vessel <strong>CMA CGM SALAMANQUE</strong>, supplied by the <strong>Ravenna Knutsen</strong>, an LNG bunkering vessel operated by Edison. The successful completion of the operation has demonstrated the commercial and technical viability of the LNG supply chain in the Adriatic region.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Edison: "Opening new market segments"</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Fabrizio Mattana</strong>, Vice President of Gas Assets at Edison, emphasized that the Trieste operation has proven the company's LNG logistics capacity:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"The first container vessel LNG bunkering in the Adriatic is an indicator of the operational flexibility Edison has achieved in recent years and our capacity to create new market segments in Italy."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">Edison, by expanding its LNG production and distribution infrastructure, stands as one of the key players supporting Italy's energy transition. The Ravenna Knutsen is considered the backbone of the future LNG network for the country's maritime fuel supply.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A new step in CMA CGM's alternative fuel strategy</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>CMA CGM Group</strong>, based in Marseille, is among the global leaders in LNG-powered vessels. The group operates an extensive <strong>LNG-dual-fuel fleet</strong> aimed at reducing CO₂ emissions and is actively contributing to the widespread development of low-carbon fuel infrastructure across Europe.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The first STS operation in Trieste strengthens CMA CGM's low-carbon strategy by enhancing LNG supply security in the Mediterranean and Adriatic regions. Furthermore, this operation marks an important beginning in adding LNG to Italy's fuel bunkering map.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Why is Trieste important?</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The Port of Trieste:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Is one of the busiest trade centers in the North Adriatic,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Serves as a crucial hub for intermodal transport to Central and Eastern Europe,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Is a strategic gateway for Italy in terms of energy and fuel logistics.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">The introduction of low-carbon fuels such as LNG here will provide in the long term:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>cleaner maritime transport</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>expansion of the alternative fuel network in the EMEA region</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>a sustainability advantage for CMA CGM on Adriatic routes</strong><br>
.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Company profiles</strong></h4>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>CMA CGM S.A.</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">A Marseille-based global shipping and logistics group,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">One of the world's largest players in container shipping,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Offers a broad portfolio including maritime, road, air transport, terminal operations, and warehousing,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">A privately held company owned by the Saadé family.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Edison S.p.A.</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">EDF Group's energy subsidiary in Italy,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Specializes in electricity and natural gas generation, distribution, and infrastructure management,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Also plays an important sector role through LNG logistics and energy transition investments.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">The LNG ship-to-ship bunkering operation carried out in Trieste represents an important beginning in Italy's alternative fuel ecosystem and strengthens sustainable maritime transport in the region.</p>
