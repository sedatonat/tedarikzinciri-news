<p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mediterranean_Shipping_Company"><u>MSC</u></a> announced today that it will partner with <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_shipping_network"><u>Global Shipping Business Network (GSBN)</u></a>, a neutral, non-profit consortium that enables paperless shipping data exchange, to enhance the safe transportation of lithium battery shipments. The initiative gains importance due to the increasing presence of lithium-ion batteries transported on container vessels and the growing risk of misclassification of these cargoes.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>To mitigate this risk, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mediterranean_Shipping_Company"><u>MSC</u></a> will integrate the lithium battery shipment booking process with <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_shipping_network"><u>GSBN</u></a> and gain access to immutable secure transport certificates shared directly by product safety laboratories through <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_shipping_network"><u>GSBN</u></a>'s blockchain network.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_shipping_network"><u>GSBN</u></a> provides these certificates through leading Chinese testing laboratories and certification providers. <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_shipping_network"><u>GSBN</u></a> aims to expand its collaboration with additional certification and laboratory networks in the future.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Additionally, other ocean carriers such as <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hapag-Lloyd"><u>Hapag-Lloyd</u></a> are also benefiting from <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_shipping_network"><u>GSBN</u></a>'s digital solutions by gaining the capability to issue electronic bills of lading.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5>Key Points</h5>
<ol>
  <li><p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mediterranean_Shipping_Company"><u>MSC</u></a> is enhancing the safety of lithium battery shipments through partnership with <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_shipping_network"><u>GSBN</u></a>.</p></li>
  <li><p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_shipping_network"><u>GSBN</u></a> provides secure transport certificates through its blockchain network.</p></li>
  <li><p>Leading Chinese laboratories and certification providers are involved in this process.</p></li>
  <li><p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_shipping_network"><u>GSBN</u></a> plans to expand its collaboration and offer more certificates.</p></li>
  <li><p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hapag-Lloyd"><u>Hapag-Lloyd</u></a> is adopting <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Global_shipping_network"><u>GSBN</u></a>'s electronic bill of lading (eBL) solution and aims for 100% electronic bills of lading by 2030.</p></li>
</ol>
