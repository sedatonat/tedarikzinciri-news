<p>COSCO SHIPPING has officially announced the establishment of a self-operated logistics warehouse in the United States. The new facility spans 177,141 square meters and is located in the greater Los Angeles area, approximately 60 minutes from Los Angeles International Airport and the Port of Los Angeles. The warehouse has the capacity to deliver 95% of orders within 24 hours and 98% within 48 hours.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Leveraging COSCO SHIPPING's global transportation network, the facility offers a comprehensive range of services including transit services for large and medium-sized goods, end-to-end truck routing, and comprehensive in-warehouse operations.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>COSCO SHIPPING has also enhanced its EDI process through advanced OMS/WMS systems and provides management capabilities with comprehensive IT-enabled operations that ensure continuous and full visibility. Customers can access real-time cargo dynamics and effectively manage various operational challenges.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The new facility will focus particularly on meeting the needs of mid-sized and large product sellers, offering both standard and customized logistics solutions for products such as home appliances and furniture.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5><strong>Key Points</strong></h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>COSCO SHIPPING has opened a self-operated logistics warehouse in the United States.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The warehouse is located in the Los Angeles area, 60 minutes from the port and airport.</p></li>\n  <li><p>95% of orders can be delivered within 24 hours; 98% within 48 hours.</p></li>\n  <li><p>A comprehensive range of services is offered, utilizing a global transportation network.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The EDI process has been enhanced through OMS/WMS systems.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Real-time cargo tracking and effective management of operational challenges are provided.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Services tailored to mid-sized and large product sellers are highlighted.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.porttechnology.org/news/cosco-shipping-unveils-warehouse-in-the-us/"><u>https://www.porttechnology.org/news/cosco-shipping-unveils-warehouse-in-the-us/</u></a></p>
