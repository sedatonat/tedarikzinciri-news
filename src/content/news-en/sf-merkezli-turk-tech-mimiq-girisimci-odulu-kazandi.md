<p>San Francisco-based Turkish high-tech company <a href="https://mimiq.io/"><u>Mimiq</u></a> has achieved significant success in the logistics and supply chain sector. The company won the "Entrepreneur of the Year Award" with its SIM-card-free tracking device Mimiq Track.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The device has broad applications and provides an effective solution, particularly for container tracking, cold chain management, and monitoring of valuable goods.
