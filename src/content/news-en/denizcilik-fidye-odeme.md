<p style="text-align: justify">A study of the maritime sector reveals that 7% of companies have been forced to pay ransoms in the last 12 months. This situation demonstrates the impact of cyber security threats on the maritime industry and how vulnerable companies are against these threats.​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The research indicates that ransomware attacks in the maritime sector are increasing and seriously affecting companies' operational processes. Attacks are impacting a wide range of activities, from ship operations to port management, which negatively affects the sector's overall productivity.​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">As cyber attacks increase, maritime companies are investing more in strengthening their information technology infrastructure and training employees on cyber security. However, concerns persist about whether these efforts are sufficient.​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Experts stress that maritime companies need to update their cyber security strategies and implement more proactive measures. Otherwise, warnings are being issued that ransomware attacks could cause even greater damage to the sector.​</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">7% of maritime companies were forced to pay ransoms in the last 12 months.​</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Ransomware attacks are affecting a wide range of operations, from ship operations to port management.​</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Companies are increasing cyber security investments, but concerns remain about the adequacy of these measures.​</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Experts emphasize that more proactive cyber security strategies need to be adopted.​</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This information highlights the importance of cyber security in the maritime sector once again and demonstrates that companies need to be more careful and better prepared in this area.</p>
