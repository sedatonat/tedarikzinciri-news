<p>Accenture has acquired Spanish consulting firm Boslan to expand its sustainability and net zero carbon infrastructure advisory services. The strategic move is seen as a step to strengthen Accenture's leadership position in energy transition and green infrastructure projects. Boslan is recognized for its expertise in renewable energy, water management, and environmentally friendly infrastructure. The merger will enable both companies to expand their client portfolios and deliver more effective solutions in sustainability-focused projects.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Accenture plans to combine Boslan's technical knowledge base with its own digital transformation and innovation capacity to deliver more comprehensive and integrated solutions to clients.
