<p style="text-align: justify">Date: March 26, 2026; Time: 1:00 PM ET; Live Web Seminar: 1 hour. Running a global supply chain resembles making fine wine—it requires the right terroir, impeccable processes, and the courage to navigate disruptions such as unexpected frost or shifting tariffs. In this inaugural episode of "<strong>Let's Wine about Supply Chain</strong>," host <strong>Bart A. De Muynck</strong> joins a visionary leader to explore how procurement has evolved into a complex, globally balanced undertaking. From a supply chain perspective, <strong>Bart A. De Muynck</strong> brings extensive experience as <strong>VP Analyst</strong> at <strong>Gartner</strong>, followed by his role as Chief Industry Officer at <strong>project44</strong>, and now as founder of <strong>BAM Research</strong>. In the realm of <strong>thought leadership</strong>, he publishes regularly on platforms including <strong>FreightWaves</strong>, <strong>SCB</strong>, <strong>LinkedIn</strong>, and <strong>Logistics Management</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Whether you are an experienced supply chain professional or simply a wine enthusiast, join us for a full-bodied conversation about the future of procurement and purchasing. Stay tuned to watch us. From a supply chain perspective, the <strong>procurement</strong> field comprises sub-disciplines including <strong>strategic sourcing</strong>, <strong>category management</strong>, <strong>supplier relationship management</strong> (<strong>SRM</strong>), <strong>contract lifecycle management</strong> (<strong>CLM</strong>), <strong>spend analytics</strong>, and <strong>e-procurement</strong>. <strong>SAP Ariba</strong>, <strong>Coupa</strong>, <strong>Jaggaer</strong>, <strong>Oracle Procurement Cloud</strong>, <strong>Ivalua</strong>, <strong>GEP</strong>, <strong>Workday Strategic Sourcing</strong>, <strong>Zycus</strong>, and <strong>Keelvar</strong> are among the market-leading platforms. <strong>CIPS</strong> (<strong>Chartered Institute of Procurement &amp; Supply</strong>), <strong>ISM</strong> (<strong>Institute for Supply Management</strong>), and <strong>NAPM</strong> are professional certification bodies.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">From a supply chain perspective, the modern procurement mix entails balancing <strong>direct procurement</strong> (BOM components, raw materials) with <strong>indirect procurement</strong> (MRO, <strong>professional services</strong>, <strong>IT</strong>, <strong>marketing</strong>, <strong>travel</strong>, <strong>facilities</strong>). <strong>Tariff engineering</strong>, <strong>landed cost optimization</strong>, <strong>FTA</strong> (<strong>Free Trade Agreement</strong>) exploitation, <strong>customs broker</strong> relationship management, and proper <strong>HTSUS</strong> classification are daily challenges for modern procurement leaders. <strong>E-auction</strong>, <strong>RFP</strong> (<strong>Request for Proposal</strong>), <strong>RFQ</strong> (<strong>Request for Quotation</strong>), <strong>RFI</strong> (<strong>Request for Information</strong>), and <strong>tender</strong> mechanisms are standard tools for global sourcing. <strong>Generative AI</strong> is accelerating procurement automation in tasks including <strong>contract review</strong>, <strong>supplier discovery</strong>, <strong>spec writing</strong>, and <strong>negotiation analysis</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">From a supply chain perspective, the "<strong>terroir</strong>" metaphor evokes the combination of geography, politics, climate, and infrastructure for supply chain. <strong>Geopolitical risk scoring</strong> is provided by platforms such as <strong>Resilinc</strong>, <strong>Everstream Analytics</strong>, <strong>Riskmethods</strong>, <strong>Interos</strong>, and <strong>Sayari</strong>. <strong>Climate risk</strong> is modeled by <strong>Munich Re</strong>, <strong>Swiss Re</strong>, <strong>Climate X</strong>, <strong>Jupiter Intelligence</strong>, and <strong>Cervest</strong>. <strong>Tariff tracking</strong> is managed through platforms including <strong>Avalara</strong>, <strong>Thomson Reuters ONESOURCE</strong>, <strong>Vertex</strong>, and <strong>Descartes CustomsInfo</strong>. <strong>De Muynck</strong>'s podcast format delivers an accessible, conversational learning experience for supply chain managers. In conclusion, the inaugural episode of <strong>LWASC</strong> promises to address the complexity of modern procurement through creative metaphors.</p>
