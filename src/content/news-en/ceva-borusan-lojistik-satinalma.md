<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>CEVA Logistics</strong> has announced the formal acquisition of a <strong>100% stake in Borusan Supply Chain Solutions and Technology Company</strong>, significantly expanding its Turkish presence. This company serves as the legal entity behind <strong>Borusan Lojistik</strong>, one of Turkey's most recognized local logistics operators. The transaction, valued at <strong>$383 million</strong>, has now closed following receipt of all necessary regulatory approvals.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The sale structure comprised two principal shareholders:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Borusan Holding</strong> → 69.47%</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Borusan Yatırım</strong> → 30.53%</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Borusan Port operations will remain with Borusan Holding.<br>
Additionally, CEVA has assumed control of Borusan Lojistik's overseas affiliates and the <strong>eTA</strong> and <strong>Bukoli</strong> brands in Turkey as part of the acquisition.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Turkey Now CEVA's Third-Largest Country by Revenue</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">CEVA Logistics CEO <strong>Mathieu Friedberg</strong> emphasized that Turkey has become one of the group's priority markets due to its rapidly growing economy and strategic location:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"With this acquisition, Turkey has become CEVA's third-largest country by revenue."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">Turkey's <strong>critical bridging role on the India–Middle East–Europe corridor</strong> positions the country as a key logistics hub in CEVA's expansion strategy.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>CEVA's Turkish Operations Now Operating at Significantly Greater Scale</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Following the acquisition, CEVA Logistics has dramatically increased its logistics capacity in Turkey:</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Warehousing and Contract Logistics</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Total managed area: <strong>1,190,000 m²</strong><br>
This positions CEVA among Turkey's largest contract logistics providers.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Road Transportation</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Annual FTL (full truckload) transportation volume of approximately <strong>1 million</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Borusan Lojistik's robust <strong>LTL</strong> (less-than-truckload) network significantly expands CEVA's Turkey–Europe connections.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>FVL – Finished Vehicle Logistics</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">CEVA will now be among the leading players in finished-vehicle logistics in Turkey.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Air and Sea Freight</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Borusan Lojistik's existing networks will strengthen CEVA's global air-sea freight capacity from Turkish origins.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Post-Acquisition Process: Integration and Rebranding</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Following the closing, CEVA Logistics will commence <strong>integration and rebranding operations</strong>. This process aims to bring Borusan Lojistik operations under the CEVA banner while creating greater synergies under a single brand.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Through this merger, CEVA becomes a:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>3PL leader</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">major local distribution player for high-volume operations,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">strong e-commerce logistics supporter,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">principal operator of Europe-connected multimodal transportation<br>
in Turkey.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>About CEVA Logistics</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Marseille-based <strong>CEVA Logistics</strong>, part of a global 3PL giant operating in 170 countries with 110,000 employees, provides end-to-end supply chain solutions. With 2024 revenues of <strong>$18.3 billion</strong>, CEVA manages a comprehensive portfolio encompassing sea, land, air, and terminal services, forming a critical component of CMA CGM Group's integrated transportation vision.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">With this acquisition, CEVA has not only expanded in the Turkish market but also achieved a scale that will <strong>fundamentally reshape competitive dynamics in the local logistics sector</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Highlights:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">CEVA Logistics acquired Borusan Lojistik's legal entity for <strong>$383 million</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Turkish operations gained <strong>1.19 million m² of warehouse space</strong>, <strong>1 million FTL</strong>, robust LTL and FVL capacity.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Turkey became CEVA's <strong>3rd largest country by revenue</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The sale includes eTA and Bukoli brands; Borusan Port remained with Holding.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Integration and rebranding process commencing post-acquisition.</p></li>
</ul>
