<p>Amazon has opened its logistics network to third-party sellers, beginning to provide fulfillment services to users of Walmart, Shopify, and Shein. The new model is operated through Amazon Multi-Channel Fulfillment (MCF). This system enables cross-brand integration in inventory management and delivery processes. According to experts, this move strengthens the logistics-as-a-service (LaaS) concept.
