<p>Turkey's foreign trade faces serious challenges due to difficulties in obtaining Schengen visas. <a href="https://www.utikad.org.tr/"><u>UTİKAD</u></a> Chairman Bilgehan Engin emphasizes that obstacles encountered by both truck drivers and business professionals in visa acquisition must be resolved.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Visa acquisition problems for road transport are negatively affecting the logistics sector and, consequently, foreign trade, undermining the international competitiveness of Turkish transport companies.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Disruptions in visa processes affect not only trade to Europe but also commerce with other regions accessible via road networks. <a href="https://www.utikad.org.tr/"><u>UTİKAD</u></a> management is calling for urgent action to resolve the issue. The problem extends beyond truck drivers alone; business professionals also face difficulties in visa applications, which has a direct negative impact on foreign trade.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><a href="https://www.utikad.org.tr/"><u>UTİKAD</u></a> Board Member Cumhur Erzurumluoğlu notes that the visa problem is obstructing the flow of goods between the EU and Turkey and states that the issue is growing increasingly worse.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Points</h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>Truck drivers and business professionals face serious difficulties in obtaining Schengen visas.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Visa problems negatively impact Turkey's foreign trade and logistics sector.</p></li>\n  <li><p><a href="https://www.utikad.org.tr/"><u>UTİKAD</u></a> Chairman and executives are calling for swift action to resolve the issue.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Obstacles in the visa process are reducing the international market competitiveness of Turkish transport companies.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The problem affects not only trade with Europe but also commerce with other regions accessible by road.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Uncertainties and lengthy processes in visa applications are putting the sector in an increasingly difficult situation.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.lojiport.com/vize-sorunu-turkiyenin-dis-ticaretini-tehdit-ediyor-116402h.htm"><u>https://www.lojiport.com/vize-sorunu-turkiyenin-dis-ticaretini-tehdit-ediyor-116402h.htm</u></a></p>
