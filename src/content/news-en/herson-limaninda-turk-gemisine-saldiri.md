<p>A Turkish-flagged dry bulk carrier named "Kuruoğlu 3" anchored at Ukraine's Kherson port came under attack from an unidentified source. The assault caused severe damage to the vessel, which subsequently capsized. A missile or artillery round struck the bridge, triggering a fire that was eventually brought under control.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The "Kuruoğlu 3," one of several vessels trapped in the port due to the Ukraine-Russia war that began on February 24, 2022, had its crew evacuated. Only one security personnel remained on board for protective purposes, and this person was not on the vessel at the time of the attack.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>More than 10 additional Turkish vessels remain in Kherson port besides the "Kuruoğlu 3." The incident underscores the war's detrimental impact on maritime trade and the safety of Turkish seafarers. Experts stress the importance of ensuring the security of Turkish vessels in the region and taking necessary measures to end the conflict.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h3>Key Points:</h3>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>The Turkish-flagged vessel "Kuruoğlu 3" was struck by an unknown source.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The vessel capsized and caught fire following a missile or artillery impact.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The vessel had been stranded in Kherson port due to the Ukraine-Russia war.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The crew was evacuated, with only one security officer remaining on board.</p></li>\n  <li><p>More than 10 additional Turkish vessels are waiting in Kherson port.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The incident demonstrates threats to maritime commerce and Turkish seafarer safety.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Experts emphasize the importance of securing Turkish vessels in the region and taking steps to end the conflict.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.lojiport.com/ukraynada-limanda-demirli-turk-gemisini-vurdular-116269h.htm"><u>https://www.lojiport.com/ukraynada-limanda-demirli-turk-gemisini-vurdular-116269h.htm</u></a></p>
