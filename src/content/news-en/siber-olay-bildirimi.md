<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Cydome</strong> has released <strong>a free tool</strong> designed to <strong>streamline cyber incident notification processes</strong> for organizations. This solution targets standardization and speed in the early stages of cyber incident management, particularly in sectors with complex and distributed <strong>IT/OT infrastructure</strong> —such as maritime and logistics.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The tool provides security teams with an operational framework through <strong>standardized reporting templates</strong>, <strong>evidence collection guidance</strong>, and <strong>regulatory mapping</strong> features. This enables incident reports generated after detection to achieve compliance with legal requirements in both form and content, ensuring consistency and assurance in audit processes.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In environments with dispersed systems —such as ship, port, and transportation operations— <strong>incident triage</strong> and <strong>initial containment</strong> steps play a critical role. Cydome's tool encourages these steps to be executed in a <strong>consistent and documentable</strong> manner, reducing errors in incident management.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, <strong>customer/vendor notification</strong> templates and <strong>forensic handoff</strong> procedures bring clarity and speed to stakeholder communication. This standardizes information sharing with both internal and external parties, reducing the risk of inaccurate or incomplete reporting.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In summary, Cydome's low-barrier tool represents an important step in <strong>scaling cybersecurity maturity across wider ecosystems</strong>. From SMEs to large logistics operators, numerous organizations can now respond to cyber incidents in a more <strong>prepared and systematic</strong> manner by reducing complex compliance and reporting burdens.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p><strong>Key Points:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li><p>Standard reporting and evidence collection guidance are provided.</p></li>
  <li><p>Regulatory mapping provides assurance.</p></li>
  <li><p>Triage is accelerated in IT/OT environments.</p></li>
  <li><p>Notification and handoff templates reduce errors.</p></li>
  <li><p>Maturity scales across wider ecosystems.</p></li>
</ul>
