<p>Egypt has successfully completed the trial run of a 10-kilometre new canal extension built at the southern end of the Suez Canal. During the trial, two vessels transited smoothly through the new bi-directional section of the channel.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>This expansion was accelerated following the 2021 grounding of the container ship <strong>Ever Given</strong>, which blocked the canal for six days. The new extension aims to increase the canal's daily capacity by 6 to 8 vessels and strengthen its ability to manage potential emergencies. With this expansion, the total length of the canal's bi-directional section increased from 72 kilometres to 82 kilometres, while the canal's overall length now stands at 193 kilometres.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>However, the Suez Canal has experienced significant revenue losses due to attacks on vessels in the Red Sea by Houthi militants from Yemen. Egyptian President <strong>Abdel Fattah al-Sisi</strong> noted that canal revenues declined by approximately $7 billion in 2024, representing a decline of more than 60 percent compared to 2023.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Egypt continues to evaluate additional expansion projects to further increase the canal's capacity and respond more effectively to emergencies.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>\n<ul>\n  <li><p>Egypt successfully completed the trial run of a 10-kilometre new canal extension at the southern end of the Suez Canal.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Two vessels transited smoothly through the new bi-directional section during the trial.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The expansion was accelerated following the 2021 grounding of the <strong>Ever Given</strong> vessel that caused the blockage.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The new extension aims to increase the canal's daily capacity by 6-8 vessels.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The total length of the canal's bi-directional section reached 82 km, with the overall length now at 193 km.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Canal revenues declined by approximately $7 billion in 2024 due to attacks by Houthi militants from Yemen.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Egypt is evaluating additional expansion projects to increase the canal's capacity.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>---</p>\n<p><strong>News Link: </strong><a href="https://gcaptain.com/egypt-completes-trial-run-of-new-suez-canal-channel-extension/"><u>https://gcaptain.com/egypt-completes-trial-run-of-new-suez-canal-channel-extension/</u></a></p>\n<p>-------------------</p>\n<p><strong>!!!
