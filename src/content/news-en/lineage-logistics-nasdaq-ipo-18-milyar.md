<p>Lineage Logistics is a leading global player in cold storage and logistics, commanding a dominant position in the sector worldwide. The company conducted its initial public offering on the Nasdaq stock exchange at $78 per share, raising $4.4 billion and achieving a total valuation of $18 billion. Lineage plans to deploy the proceeds toward debt repayment, employee bonuses, and general corporate purposes. CEO Greg Lehmkuhl underscores the company's growth trajectory in cold storage and its mission to transform the food supply chain.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5><strong>Key Highlights:</strong></h5>
<ol>
  <li><p>Lineage Logistics raised $4.4 billion, achieving an $18 billion valuation.</p></li>
  <li><p>The company is positioned as the world's largest cold storage and logistics provider.</p></li>
  <li><p>Proceeds will be allocated to debt repayment, employee bonuses, and general corporate expenses.</p></li>
  <li><p>The company's objective is to transform the global food supply chain and reduce waste.</p></li>
