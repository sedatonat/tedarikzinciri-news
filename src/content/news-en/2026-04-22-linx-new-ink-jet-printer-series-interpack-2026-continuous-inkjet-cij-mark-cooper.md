<p style="text-align: justify">Industrial coding and marking systems manufacturer Linx Printing Technologies will unveil its new ink jet printer series at interpack 2026 in Düsseldorf. The company will showcase its latest innovations in continuous ink jet (CIJ) technology at stand C59 in Hall 8B.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify">According to Mark Cooper, Senior Product Director at Linx, the new series has been designed to deliver higher reliability, lower maintenance cost, and improved print quality on production lines. CIJ technology is used as an industry standard for printing expiry dates, batch numbers, and traceability codes on high-speed packaging lines in food, beverage, pharmaceutical, and cosmetics applications.</p>

<p style="text-align: justify">As demands for serialisation, traceability, and anti-counterfeiting protection grow across global supply chains, the importance of automated coding equipment continues to rise. Linx's new series also stands out with connectivity features that simplify integration into the digital manufacturing ecosystem.</p>
