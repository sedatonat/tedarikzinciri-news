<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Configura</strong> is the developer of <strong>Parametric Graphical Configuration (PGC)</strong> software, used in fields such as industrial equipment, office furniture, and laboratory equipment. This software simplifies the design, sales, and manufacturing of complex products, thereby enhancing company productivity. <strong>BEUMER Group</strong> is a global leader in intralogistics systems and automation solutions. It provides solutions in material handling, loading, packaging, palletizing, sorting, and distribution technologies.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The two companies have formed a strategic partnership in digital system planning and visualization. This collaboration aims to improve the planning and visualization processes of <strong>BEUMER Group</strong>'s intralogistics systems. Through <strong>Configura</strong>'s PGC technology, <strong>BEUMER Group</strong>'s customers and employees can create and customize system designs more quickly and efficiently.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This partnership underscores the importance of digitalization in the intralogistics sector. Digital planning and visualization tools enable companies to deliver more flexible and customer-centric solutions. Additionally, such tools reduce error rates and shorten project timelines.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Configura</strong>'s PGC technology allows users to easily design and visualize complex systems. This technology features a drag-and-drop interface and can be customized according to user needs. Furthermore, thanks to real-time 3D visualization capabilities, users can preview how designs will look and function.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>BEUMER Group</strong> plans to use this technology to provide better service to its customers. In particular, the ability to respond quickly and flexibly to customer demands provides a competitive advantage. Additionally, through digital planning tools, project costs and timelines can be better managed.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This partnership also offers significant advantages in terms of sustainability. Digital planning and visualization tools reduce the number of physical prototypes and trials, thereby providing material and energy savings. Moreover, through more efficient system designs, operations can reduce their energy consumption and carbon footprint.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In conclusion, this strategic partnership between <strong>Configura</strong> and <strong>BEUMER Group</strong> once again demonstrates the importance of digital transformation in the intralogistics sector. The use of digital system planning and visualization tools increases companies' competitiveness and helps them achieve sustainability goals.</p>
