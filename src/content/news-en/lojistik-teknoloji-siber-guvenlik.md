<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>The global logistics sector</strong> is <strong>accelerating digital transformation</strong> as it heads into 2026, yet <strong>cybersecurity and regulatory compliance</strong> remain on the back burner. According to a new survey published by <strong>OntegosCloud</strong>, approximately <strong>40% of logistics companies</strong> plan to dedicate <strong>at least a quarter of their next year's budget to technology investments</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The survey included <strong>912 logistics and supply chain professionals from North America, Europe, and Asia</strong>. The results reveal that while the sector is rapidly digitalizing, <strong>protective mechanisms are lagging behind this pace</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>2026's focus: Predictive visibility</strong></h5>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>44% of respondents</strong> identified <strong>predictive visibility</strong> technologies as the most critical investment area for 2026. This figure surpasses other priorities such as <strong>automation, digital twin</strong>, and <strong>cybersecurity</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Only <strong>11%</strong> cited <strong>cybersecurity and compliance</strong> as a primary priority. This situation signals that <strong>the gap between innovation and protection is widening</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Oliver Gritz, Founder and Managing Director of OntegosCloud</strong>, assessed the situation with these words:</p>\n<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Logistics leaders are no longer asking whether to digitalize, but how quickly they can do it. However, the pace of innovation is outpacing the sector's capacity to protect itself."</blockquote>\n<blockquote><br></blockquote>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Technology's growing weight in budgets</strong></h5>\n<p style="text-align: justify">According to the survey results:</p>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>38%</strong> are allocating <strong>more than 25% of their 2026 budget to technology</strong>.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>25%</strong> plan to increase their budget by <strong>10–25%</strong>.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>23%</strong> are exercising caution by dedicating <strong>less than 10% of resources to technology</strong>.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>14%</strong> have yet to finalize their technology budget.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p style="text-align: justify">One-third of companies now view technology not as a tool supporting operations but as <strong>a driver of strategy</strong>. This finding indicates that <strong>the logistics industry is transitioning to a "digital-first" structure</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A dangerous gap in cybersecurity</strong></h5>\n<p style="text-align: justify">According to the report, companies' <strong>focus on artificial intelligence, data analytics, predictive planning, and automation</strong> is <strong>overshadowing cybersecurity investments</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Experts emphasize that this imbalance <strong>can produce high-risk consequences</strong>. Rising <strong>cyberattacks, ransomware, and supply chain manipulation</strong> pose a significant threat, particularly for <strong>3PL companies</strong>.</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Oliver Gritz</strong> issues a clear warning on this matter:</p>\n<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"2026's biggest question won't be whether innovation continues, but whether it proceeds securely. True resilience lies in combining data, automation, and cybersecurity within a single intelligent ecosystem."</blockquote>\n<blockquote><br></blockquote>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Data infrastructure: The backbone of transformation</strong></h5>\n<p style="text-align: justify">OntegosCloud's research emphasizes that robust <strong>data infrastructure</strong> sits at the center of all digital transformation strategies. Particularly in 2026, when <strong>tariffs, trade restrictions, and geopolitical uncertainty</strong> will persist, <strong>solid data management systems</strong> will deliver both <strong>operational resilience</strong> and <strong>supply chain agility</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Within this framework, logistics firms' priorities are concentrated on three key areas:</p>\n<ol>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Predictive data management and analytics systems,</strong></p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Operational automation and process integration,</strong></p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Strengthening cybersecurity infrastructure.</strong></p></li>\n</ol>\n<p style="text-align: justify">However, the third item still ranks low in investment plans for many firms.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Commentary and future expectations</strong></h5>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Experts anticipate that <strong>the gap between digitalization pace and security investments will widen further in 2026</strong>. As <strong>AI-powered operations</strong> and <strong>cloud-based supply chain platforms</strong> become more widespread, <strong>data breaches and identity management risks</strong> will present a significant test.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">For this reason, <strong>OntegosCloud</strong> argues that <strong>technological innovation must transform into "secure innovation"</strong>. In Gritz's words:</p>\n<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Resilience comes not from speed, but from intelligent integration. Security, data, and automation can no longer be considered separately."</blockquote>\n<blockquote><br></blockquote>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">In a survey of <strong>912 logistics professionals</strong>, <strong>38%</strong> allocate <strong>more than 25% of their budget to technology</strong>.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Predictive visibility</strong> and <strong>forecasting analytics</strong> are 2026's top priority.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Cybersecurity</strong> was viewed as a priority by only <strong>11%</strong> of respondents.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>OntegosCloud CEO:</strong> "The sector is rapidly advancing digitalization but protection is falling behind."</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Data infrastructure and intelligent integration</strong> will be fundamental to <strong>supply chain resilience</strong> in the future.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">2026's central question: <strong>"How secure will logistics innovation be?"</strong></p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>----------</p>\n\n<p>--------------------</p>\n<p><strong>Author: </strong><a href="http://sedatonat.com/"><u>SedatOnat.com</u></a></p>\n<p>--------------------</p>\n<p><strong>!!!
