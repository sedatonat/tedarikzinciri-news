<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Postrack, one of Europe's leading GPS-based asset tracking firms,</strong> has <strong>announced its entry into the United Kingdom market.</strong> This move has been realized through <strong>the establishment of a new UK-based unit,</strong> marking part of the company's <strong>international growth strategy.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new operation will deliver <strong>advanced asset management solutions across a broad range of sectors including road transport, warehousing, international freight forwarding, and container management.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Postrack CEO and Founder Niclas Holm</strong> commented:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Our comprehensive asset tracking portfolio has proven itself in increasing operational efficiency, reducing costs, and improving equipment utilization rates. It also minimizes the risks of loss, theft, and misuse. Building on our successes in Scandinavia and Europe, we are ready to provide strong support to UK businesses and technology providers."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Advanced asset tracking technology</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Postrack's solutions enable tracking of both <strong>powered and unpowered assets (containers, trailers, vehicles, pallets, transport cages, high-value cargo, and temperature-controlled shipments).</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Long-life battery (up to 20 years),</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>IP68/IP69K certified durability,</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Both indoor and outdoor tracking capability,</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Cloud-based data integration</strong> — features that enable the system to deliver <strong>uninterrupted performance even in the most demanding environmental conditions.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This allows users to track their assets in <strong>real-time, knowing exactly where they are, where they came from, and how they are moving.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Proven success stories</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Postrack technology is already in active use by major logistics operators across Europe.</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>PostNord, Scandinavia's leading logistics giant,</strong> uses Postrack devices across thousands of containers, achieving <strong>substantial gains in container utilization rates</strong> and <strong>a 24% reduction in rental costs.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Pan-European trailer operator NTEX</strong> has achieved <strong>100% visibility across its fleet of more than 1,200 trailers,</strong> reducing <strong>waiting times and operational costs,</strong> while also <strong>lowering carbon emissions.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">These results demonstrate that Postrack's <strong>intelligent data-driven decision support systems</strong> deliver concrete financial and environmental benefits to its customers.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Growth targets in the United Kingdom</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Niclas Holm noted that the United Kingdom is <strong>a market that rapidly adopts logistics technologies,</strong> adding:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Postrack enables our customers to better manage their assets and fully unlock their operational potential. Interest from the United Kingdom has been very strong, and we see significant opportunities in this new market."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">The company plans to announce <strong>new models that will expand its product range</strong> and <strong>projects that will strengthen local business partnerships</strong> before the end of 2025.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Expanding network across Europe</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Founded in 2008, Postrack today <strong>has offices in Sweden, the Netherlands, and the United Kingdom</strong> and provides <strong>fully integrated, end-to-end asset tracking solutions.</strong> The company's customer base extends beyond <strong>logistics and transport sectors,</strong> also encompassing <strong>construction, waste management, and agriculture.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Postrack's strategic vision is to scale <strong>intelligent tracking infrastructure that provides operational visibility across Europe</strong> on a global basis.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Holm summed up the company's mission with these words:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Our goal is to optimize our customers' asset utilization, reduce operational costs, and enhance security. Our UK operation represents a step that reinforces Postrack's leadership in Europe on a global scale."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Postrack</strong> continues its international expansion by <strong>opening a new office in the United Kingdom.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Target sectors: <strong>Road transport, warehousing, international freight forwarding, and container management.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Device features: <strong>20-year battery life, IP68/IP69K durability, indoor/outdoor tracking.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Reference projects: <strong>PostNord</strong> (24% reduction in container rental costs), <strong>NTEX</strong> (100% visibility across 1,200 trailers).</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">New solutions and partnerships to be announced <strong>by the end of 2025.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Company headquarters in <strong>Sweden,</strong> operations centers in <strong>the Netherlands and the United Kingdom.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Mission: <strong>To deliver asset optimization, cost reduction, and operational security</strong> through digital data.</p></li>
</ul>
