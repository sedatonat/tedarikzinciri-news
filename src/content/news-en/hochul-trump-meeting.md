<p><br>
New York Governor Kathy Hochul is meeting with Donald Trump to defend the Congestion Pricing policy.&nbsp;</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>The meeting covers critical topics including federal funding and regulatory support. The governor's office emphasizes that the policy will provide sustainable financing for public transit and reduce in-city emissions. Opposition groups highlight potential burdens on businesses and low-income drivers. Implementation design considerations include exemptions, credits for low-income drivers, and measures to limit traffic diversion risks.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Key Points:<br>
1. The meeting encompasses federal support and funding.<br>
2. The policy targets public transit financing.<br>
3. Opposition focuses on cost pressures.<br>
4. Exemption and credit mechanisms are under discussion.<br>
5. Measures are planned to mitigate traffic diversion risks.</p>
