<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Packsize</strong> is demonstrating <strong>automated right-sized packaging systems</strong> at its booth <strong>B16532</strong> at Modex 2026. The company is showing visitors how packaging operations can be sped up and waste reduced. While manual packing processes slow operations and drive up costs, Packsize's automated solutions aim to remove that pressure.</p><p style="text-align: justify">In live demonstrations, the <strong>CVP Impack</strong> and <strong>EM7</strong> systems were shown packing items into ready-to-ship boxes in seconds. Visitors could also bring their own products and test the systems under real-world conditions. By producing a custom-sized box for every package according to the product inside, Packsize reduces shipping volume; this increases container and road-freight loading efficiency.</p><p style="text-align: justify">Right-sized packaging has moved to the warehouse agenda along with e-commerce growth and rising fulfilment speeds. By reducing empty volume, the approach lowers the carbon footprint and supports sustainability targets. Packsize officials said their customers are both lowering operational costs and increasing customer satisfaction. The Modex demos proved that automation can eliminate the traditional "packing bottleneck" of the warehouse.</p>
