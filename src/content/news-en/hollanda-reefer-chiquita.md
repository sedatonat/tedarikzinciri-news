<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Dutch Container Merchants (DCM), a Netherlands-based container leasing and trading company,</strong> announced it has signed a supply agreement with <strong>Chiquita Brands International</strong> for <strong>1,000 next-generation refrigerated containers (reefer).</strong> This partnership is regarded as the final piece of <strong>Chiquita's strategy to build an energy-efficient container fleet.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Under the agreement, the containers to be delivered will be <strong>40 feet high cube (40HC)</strong> in size and will be equipped with <strong>Carrier Transicold Container Refrigeration OptimaLine</strong> cooling systems and <strong>Controlled Atmosphere (CA)</strong> technology. This system aims to <strong>extend product shelf life while reducing energy consumption.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Optimized for high-volume, lightweight shipments</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to a statement by Dutch Container Merchants, the new containers feature <strong>expanded internal volume,</strong> allowing <strong>lightweight but high-volume cargo</strong> to be transported more efficiently. This will provide significant advantages particularly in <strong>high-volume cold chain transportation</strong> of products such as <strong>bananas, pineapples, and other tropical fruits.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The company statement read:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"We are proud to announce our 1,000-unit next-generation Reefer container agreement with Chiquita, the world's largest banana producer. Equipped with Carrier Transicold's OptimaLine technology and Controlled Atmosphere systems, these containers are setting a new industry standard in energy efficiency and performance."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Chiquita's energy efficiency targets</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">In recent years, Chiquita has focused on <strong>reducing energy consumption by renewing its refrigerated container fleet.</strong> According to company data, <strong>in the latest fleet renewal program, energy usage decreased by 11 percent</strong> while <strong>transportation capacity increased by 25 percent.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Through this new agreement, Chiquita aims to <strong>reduce the carbon footprint of cooling operations</strong> and <strong>optimize logistics costs.</strong> Tropical fruits, particularly bananas, <strong>require continuous refrigeration during transport,</strong> so energy efficiency directly impacts operational profitability.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Dutch Container Merchants stated that <strong>flexibility, competitiveness, and reliability</strong> were determining factors in Chiquita's supplier selection.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Technology: Controlled Atmosphere and OptimaLine systems</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Controlled Atmosphere (CA)</strong> system controls oxygen, carbon dioxide, and humidity levels inside the container, thereby <strong>slowing the fruit ripening process.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Carrier OptimaLine</strong> cooling unit can <strong>reduce energy consumption while maintaining consistent temperature ranges.</strong> This ensures that <strong>product quality is preserved at maximum levels during transport.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Thanks to these technologies, the containers become one of the most advanced solutions in the industry for <strong>maintaining product freshness and achieving energy savings</strong> in long-distance transportation.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>DCM's global growth strategy</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Dutch Container Merchants has recently focused on <strong>growth in the reefer segment.</strong> The company aims to increase its market share in next-generation container supplies equipped with <strong>energy efficiency and digital monitoring systems.</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">DCM officials stated that the Chiquita agreement is not merely a commercial partnership, but also <strong>an indicator of the company's vision for sustainable transportation:</strong></p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"This partnership demonstrates our commitment to solutions that strengthen sustainability in the supply chain while improving energy efficiency."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Exemplary partnership for sustainable supply chain</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to experts, this agreement exemplifies <strong>environmentally conscious logistics approaches</strong> in fruit transportation. In the face of rising energy costs and carbon regulations, food producers are shifting toward <strong>smarter container systems that consume less energy.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The partnership between Dutch Container Merchants and Chiquita is viewed as an indicator of <strong>digitalization of cold chain transportation and sustainability-focused transformation.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
