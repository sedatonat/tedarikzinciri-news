<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Three Group Solutions</strong> announced that it will deploy a private 5G network at three main facilities of <strong>Hutchison Ports UK</strong>, one of the UK's leading port operators. Under the agreement, high-reliability and low-latency private mobile network infrastructure will be provided to <strong>the Port of Felixstowe</strong>, <strong>Harwich International</strong>, and <strong>London Thamesport</strong> facilities. This deployment is described as a significant milestone in the UK's port digitalisation strategy.</p><p style="text-align: justify">The private 5G network is designed to support <strong>autonomous ground vehicle (AGV)</strong> fleets, remote-controlled cranes, and real-time cargo tracking systems. Some automation projects at the ports had slowed due to coverage limitations and latency issues with existing Wi-Fi infrastructure. The new network provided by Three Group Solutions will cover the entire terminal area without interruption and will particularly enable crane operators to work remotely.</p><p style="text-align: justify">Hutchison Ports UK officials announced that the private 5G investment will increase terminal efficiency and improve operator safety. Moving operators from high crane cabs to control rooms will improve ergonomic conditions and increase workforce diversity. Three Group Solutions stated that it aims to make similar deployments at other critical infrastructure facilities in the UK in the future.</p>
