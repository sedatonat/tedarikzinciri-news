<p>Port Canaveral is planning a $500 million investment to develop cruise capacity and cargo berths. The project encompasses berth expansion, dredging, terminal modernization, and shore power infrastructure. The investment aims to achieve turnaround efficiency by reducing ship turnaround times and accelerating cargo operations. On the energy side, shore power and efficient equipment provide emissions reduction and air quality improvements. The regional economy is expected to benefit from increased employment and tourism revenues; meanwhile, it ensures supply chain capacity security centered in Florida.
