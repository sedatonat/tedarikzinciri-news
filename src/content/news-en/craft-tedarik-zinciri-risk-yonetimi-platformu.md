<p>Craft has launched a new user-friendly and configurable supplier risk management (SRM) platform. The platform aims to revolutionize supply chain management by fostering team collaboration and proactively reducing risks. The innovative SRM platform leverages comprehensive data fabric to simplify smarter supplier decision-making across all risk domains. The scalable platform, powered by artificial intelligence-driven risk alerts, streamlines the ability to predict and respond to supply chain risks ahead of time.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The platform delivers "hyper-relevant" risk signals and the latest user-friendly collaboration workspace, helping users stay aligned and minimize supply chain disruptions. Custom configurability provides a simple view for forecasting and prioritizing risks by eliminating unnecessary "noise." The collaboration-focused design enhances visibility, communication, and risk mitigation by centralizing data across the enterprise. According to Mimi Spier, Craft's Marketing Manager, the platform was designed with a "user-centric" approach and is highly intuitive and enjoyable to use.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Spier notes that some large government organizations and commercial enterprises use the system to provide reliable data and actionable insights for managing risks in their operations.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5><strong>Key Takeaways</strong></h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>Craft's SRM platform is user-friendly and configurable.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The platform enables proactive response to supply chain risks through AI-powered risk alerts.</p></li>\n  <li><p>It offers 360-degree supplier visibility, hyper-relevant risk signals, and a modern collaboration workspace.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Through its collaboration-focused design, the platform enhances data centralization, communication, and risk mitigation functions.</p></li>\n  <li><p>It is used by major government and commercial organizations.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n
