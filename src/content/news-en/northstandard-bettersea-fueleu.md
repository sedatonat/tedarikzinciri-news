<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>NorthStandard</strong>, in partnership with leading maritime digital decarbonization specialist <strong>BetterSea</strong>, is offering members discounted access to the <strong>BetterSea FuelEU Maritime Compliance</strong> service. This partnership facilitates shipowners' compliance with <strong>FuelEU Maritime</strong> regulations and reduces compliance costs.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FuelEU Maritime</strong> reporting requirements came into effect on January 1, 2025. This new legislation requires vessels to monitor the greenhouse gas (GHG) intensity of energy consumed and achieve a series of reduction targets. Non-compliant vessels are subject to financial penalties, which can only be mitigated through alternative energy use or a compliance mechanism called 'pooling.' Pooling refers to bringing together compliant and non-compliant vessels in a fleet to collectively meet regulatory targets.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Through this special partnership with <strong>BetterSea</strong>, <strong>NorthStandard</strong> members can access the <strong>FuelEU Platform Solution</strong> at a significant discount. This platform provides digital support to shipowners for <strong>FuelEU Maritime</strong> compliance, supporting both internal and external pooling within data processing, contractual and financial frameworks, and includes final pool verification.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">For <strong>NorthStandard</strong> members benefiting from this offer, <strong>FuelEU</strong> platform pooling transaction fees will be reduced to zero for the first six-month reporting period, with <strong>BetterSea</strong> charging only a management fee for establishing the necessary escrow accounts.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In an example provided by the partners, a 20,000 TEU container vessel consuming 29,000 tons of fossil fuel under the <strong>FuelEU Maritime Regulation</strong> can save approximately 45% of its compliance costs through pooling. With the <strong>NorthStandard</strong> offer, the member can additionally save 45,000 EUR in transaction fees.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>BetterSea FuelEU Platform Solution</strong>, with its integrated pooling marketplace, allows users to precisely monitor their compliance balances and clearly see their regulatory responsibilities. The functionality encompasses both internal and external pooling, including legal and financial pooling frameworks, banking and lending mechanisms, and submission of final pool data for verification. For each <strong>NorthStandard</strong> member joining the <strong>BetterSea FuelEU</strong> platform, up to 30 hours of consulting services on decarbonization regulatory matters are provided.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Mark Smith, Loss Prevention Director – Decarbonization at <strong>NorthStandard</strong>, stated: "The BetterSea platform provides our members with a seamless transition to <strong>FuelEU</strong> compliance and offers full security for sensitive data. Our confidence in its value for maritime is reflected in the special savings we are offering to <strong>NorthStandard</strong> members."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Maximilian Schroer, co-founder of <strong>BetterSea</strong>, previously led compliance strategy and implementation projects at <strong>Maersk</strong> for <strong>CII</strong>, <strong>EU ETS</strong>, <strong>FuelEU</strong>, <strong>Clean Shipping Act</strong>, and air emissions. Schroer stated: "FuelEU Maritime creates a complex landscape of stakeholders with very different requirements and compliance mechanisms. Our end-to-end solution simplifies and manages all of these. Such an approach enables us to equip our customers with a complete package of options to balance their compliance strategies throughout the year against unforeseen operational and market dynamics. FuelEU Maritime delivers a simple, intuitive single-platform experience that minimizes compliance costs and maximizes profitability."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>BetterSea</strong>, founded in 2023 by two former <strong>Maersk Group</strong> environmental and compliance specialists, possesses extensive domain expertise in maritime decarbonization and offers a comprehensive decarbonization product suite ranging from compliance and primary <strong>Scope 3</strong> emission data tools. Currently, approximately 5,000 vessels are enrolled in the <strong>FuelEU</strong> platform.</p>
