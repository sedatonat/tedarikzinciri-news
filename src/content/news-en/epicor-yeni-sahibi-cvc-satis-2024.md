<p>Epicor is an enterprise resource planning software provider serving the manufacturing, distribution, retail, and services sectors. The company has recently been acquired by private equity firm CVC. Its previous owner, Clayton, Dubilier & Rice (CD&amp;R), oversaw significant progress in the firm's transition to SaaS and international expansion. Epicor has bolstered its cloud-based platforms through recent acquisitions of companies such as Kyklo and Smart Software. The company serves 23,000 customers across more than 150 countries and has grown its annual recurring revenue to over $1 billion.</p>
