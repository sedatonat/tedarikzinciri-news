<p>Project44 is adding electronic bill of lading (eBOL) capability to its freight software, aiming to reduce time losses and error rates caused by paper bills of lading. This new feature enables users to seamlessly adopt new eBOL standards, thereby increasing logistics process efficiency through accurate and rapid data exchange. In turn, transportation processes become more transparent while billing operations are automated. This innovation is particularly supporting significant digital transformation in the less-than-truckload (LTL) sector.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5><strong>Key Highlights:</strong></h5>
<ol>
  <li><p>Electronic bill of lading (eBOL) reduces manual operations while improving accuracy and efficiency.</p></li>
  <li><p>Project44 automatically sends eBOL data to carriers without the need to create API endpoints.</p></li>
  <li><p>eBOL usage provides improvements in areas such as real-time visibility and automation of invoice processes.</p></li>
  <li><p>Project44 aims to advance digital transformation and supply chain efficiency across the industry.</p></li>
</ol>
