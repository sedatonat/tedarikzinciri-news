<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FESCO Transport Group</strong> has announced the successful launch of its first <strong>40' flexitank block train</strong> operation from <strong>Omsk</strong> to China, marking a significant step in strengthening the company's intermodal capacity. The train, composed entirely of 40-foot flexitank-laden containers, departed from <strong>Karbyshevo</strong> station on November 22. The train carries a total of <strong>62 units of 40' containers</strong>, with the entire shipment consisting of <strong>rapeseed oil</strong>. The shipment is being transported via the <strong>Kazakhstan</strong> transit route, with delivery to <strong>Chengdu</strong>, China expected within approximately <strong>14 days</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">This shipment is viewed as part of FESCO's strategy to expand both its product portfolio for liquid cargo transport and its block train services. Flexitank technology offers a solution that enables non-hazardous liquid cargo to be transported within containers in an economical, hygienic, and safe manner. Consequently, flexitanks used in 40' containers are increasingly preferred for food products, vegetable oils, fruit juices, and non-hazardous liquids.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">FESCO had introduced its proprietary new flexitank model in August. This model has been optimized specifically for <strong>non-hazardous liquid cargo</strong> transportation and designed for full compatibility with 40-foot containers. Through the development of this product, the company aims to expand its liquid cargo segment across both rail freight and combined transport operations. Testing the new model in high-volume shipments such as block trains represents an important phase in validating the product's operational viability.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The selection of the Omsk–Chengdu route offers a flexible and competitive solution within the existing <strong>Eurasian logistics corridors</strong> between Russia and China. This route through Kazakhstan has undergone significant improvements in recent years in terms of both customs procedures and transit times. The route is considered advantageous for block train shipments, particularly for time-sensitive freight such as liquid food products. FESCO states that this corridor will be strengthened as a foundational link for further liquid cargo and flexitank operations in the coming period.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The general advantages of block train operations include fixed transit times, lower delay risk, consolidated transport capacity, and the movement of containers under a single organization. These characteristics offer a reliable solution particularly for Russia's growing agricultural and food product exports to China. Rapeseed oil transportation ranks among the rapidly growing segments of liquid food products that Russia sends to the Chinese market, and flexitank utilization provides cost advantages.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Another aspect emphasized by FESCO's shipment is the company's <strong>intermodal integration</strong> strategy. The coordinated management of road, rail, and maritime transport networks enhances FESCO's customer portfolio with competitive strength in both regional and international markets. The company's implementation of its new flexitank model in its own railway operations is regarded as a significant milestone in terms of both product innovation and operational flexibility.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Broadly speaking, this shipment demonstrates FESCO's intention to place greater emphasis on flexitank-based solutions in liquid transportation. At the same time, as part of the process of developing alternative logistics solutions between Russia and China, it elevates the strategic importance of Central Asian transit corridors. According to FESCO's statement, the company plans to expand flexitank block train operations in the coming period in terms of both volume and route diversity.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h3 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Highlights:</strong></h3>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">FESCO has launched its first 40' flexitank block train shipment on the Omsk–Chengdu route.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The train carries 62 units of 40' containers; cargo is rapeseed oil.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Transit time is 14 days; the route passes through Kazakhstan.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">FESCO's new flexitank model was introduced in August.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Flexitank solutions provide cost advantages in liquid cargo transportation.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The operation is viewed as part of FESCO's intermodal strategy.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The company plans to expand flexitank block train operations.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>----------</p>\n\n<p>--------------------</p>\n<p><strong>Author: </strong><a href="http://sedatonat.com/"><u>SedatOnat.com</u></a></p>\n<p>--------------------</p>\n\n<p>We would be delighted to receive your feedback.</p>\n<p>Wishing you happy reading in advance.</p>\n
