<p style="text-align: justify">Toyota Material Handling UK has launched <strong>MyToyota</strong>, a new customer portal, at a time when expectations for transparency, efficiency, and data-driven operations in forklift fleet management are rising rapidly. The platform enables businesses to manage all costs, energy consumption, contracts, service plans, and safety protocols across their entire forklift fleet through a single digital ecosystem.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Toyota positions MyToyota not merely as a reporting tool but as a <strong>multi-layered service platform</strong> that enables businesses to manage their fleets more strategically and effectively. The portal delivers reliable and up-to-date insights into every aspect of the fleet, helping users make swift, data-based, and cost-effective decisions.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Cecilia Nilsson</strong>, Head of Digital Business at Toyota Material Handling Europe, emphasizes that the development of MyToyota stems from a transformation in customer expectations:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Expectations regarding fleet management have changed significantly in recent years. MyToyota is our strategic response to that change. This is not just a portal; it is a business partner guiding operations through the complexity of modern fleet management."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>I_Site integration: operational insight meets strategic analysis</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">One of MyToyota's strongest features is its seamless integration with <strong>I_Site</strong>, Toyota's proven fleet management platform. The next-generation version of I_Site was also announced around the same time. This integration enables users to:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">access operational KPIs in real time,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">analyze forklift performance,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">monitor critical safety indicators such as collision sensors and lift safety,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">seamlessly transition between real-time usage data and strategic reports.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This structure delivers <strong>immediate, actionable information</strong> to fleet managers in meetings, reporting processes, daily operations, or in the field, minimizing the risk of poor decision-making.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Transparency and data-driven approach increase</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">MyToyota provides businesses with clear visibility of cost line items in forklift fleets, enabling them to:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">monitor total cost of ownership (TCO),</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">evaluate energy efficiency,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">manage contract terms and service history from a single screen,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">track compliance with safety protocols<br>
</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">In this way, businesses both <strong>strengthen their workplace safety culture</strong> and reach a new level in operational efficiency and cost control.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A major step in Toyota's strategic transformation</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The launch of MyToyota is seen as the strongest reflection of Toyota Material Handling's vision focused on digital transformation in its United Kingdom operations. Toyota has long been developing solutions that enable customers to use forklift fleets more efficiently through sensor data, telematics technologies, and user-friendly interfaces.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">MyToyota consolidates all these digital services in <strong>a single integrated customer touchpoint</strong>, reducing management burden for businesses and streamlining processes.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The launch of the portal also signals that Toyota Material Handling UK will continue to increase its digitalization investments for modern warehouse, factory, and distribution center operations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Toyota MH UK has introduced the new <strong>MyToyota</strong> customer portal.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The platform consolidates cost, energy, safety, contract, and service management on a single screen.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">It works in full integration with the I_Site fleet management tool.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The portal enables data-driven and transparent fleet management, improving decision quality.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Toyota has taken a major step forward in its digital transformation strategy.</p></li>
</ul>
