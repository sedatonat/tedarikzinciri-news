<p style="text-align: justify">Intel's <strong>Global Logistics Org (GLO)</strong> and <strong>Corporate Strategic Procurement (CSP)</strong> teams have long struggled with <strong>high manual workloads</strong>, <strong>repetitive data collection</strong>, <strong>slow quote processes</strong>, and <strong>excess costs from non-contracted pricing</strong> in managing transportation rates, contract administration, and logistics cost optimization.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">These challenges manifested particularly in:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Data collection for new transportation rates,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Manual data uploads to external quote systems,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Extended megabid cycles involving numerous carriers,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Undetected use of expensive non-contracted tariffs,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Missed contract renewal windows<br>
creating operational bottlenecks.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">At this point, <strong>Missi Anderson</strong> (Center of Excellence – Sourcing Manager) reached out to Intel's <strong>Supply Chain Innovation Lab</strong> team for support. The result: <strong>Rate Request Automation (RRA)</strong>, an AI-powered platform that completely redesigned Intel's transportation procurement processes from end to end.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Rate Request Automation (RRA): A revolution in transportation costs</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">RRA transformed the quote request and contract renewal process into <strong>a fully digital, rapid, traceable, and intelligent workflow</strong>. Thanks to the system:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Bid processes became automated</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>A common interface</strong> was established for all Commodity Managers (CM),</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Suppliers engaged in processes through <strong>electronic forms and e-signatures</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Implementation of new rates took <strong>days instead of weeks</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">In essence, RRA transformed a labor-intensive, weeks-long, high-error-risk workflow into a <strong>semi-automated, transparent, and rapid</strong> model.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Megabid cycles now take days instead of months</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">In previous years, Intel's large global transportation procurements ("megabids") required:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Data preparation spanning months,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Manual review of hundreds of tariffs across different modes,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Labor-intensive negotiation rounds<br>
to be completed.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">RRA radically shortened this process using <strong>advanced analytics, artificial intelligence (AI), and machine learning (ML)</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Now Intel:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Can run megabid processes multiple times throughout the year,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Obtains <strong>much fresher and more competitive rates</strong> against rapid market price fluctuations,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Creates <strong>downward price pressure</strong> by competing hundreds of carriers simultaneously.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Non-contracted spending is automatically detected</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Intel's preliminary analysis revealed that expensive non-contracted shipments created <strong>a significant burden</strong> on overall costs.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">To address this, RRA:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Automatically detects non-contracted shipments via AI</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Automatically generates rate requests</strong> for them,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Once approved by CM, transitions them to contracted pricing.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This enables Intel to proactively close hidden cost leaks.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>AI-powered auditing (VAA): Real-time compliance and anomaly detection</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">RRA is integrated with Intel's <strong>Virtual Audit Agents (VAA)</strong> system:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Carrier compliance is monitored,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Contracted rates are compared against actual costs,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Unusually high costs are detected and automatic alerts are triggered.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This ensures <strong>complete spending visibility</strong>, <strong>contract discipline</strong>, and <strong>regulatory compliance</strong> in a fully digitized manner.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Results: Record levels of savings, speed, and efficiency</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to Intel's official results achieved through RRA:</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">BenefitOutcome<strong>Contracted rate savings$20 millionNon-contracted rate savings$4 millionMegabid prep time50%+ reducedNew contract speed10x increasedCM team size</strong>Reduced from <strong>10 to 2 people</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Thanks to RRA, the CM team can now focus on <strong>strategic procurement optimization</strong> rather than operational burden.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Missi Anderson emphasizes that the project created benefits not only for Intel but also for <strong>the carrier ecosystem</strong>:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Carriers can now prepare their rate cards much faster. This makes the process more competitive and efficient for both sides."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Intel achieved end-to-end automation in transportation rate management through an <strong>AI/ML-based RRA system</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Megabid cycles are now <strong>faster and more competitive</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Non-contracted rates are automatically detected and cost leaks are plugged.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">RRA + VAA integration delivers <strong>full visibility, compliance, and cost control</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The initiative has generated <strong>$24 million in total savings</strong> for Intel and <strong>significantly accelerated contract timelines</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
