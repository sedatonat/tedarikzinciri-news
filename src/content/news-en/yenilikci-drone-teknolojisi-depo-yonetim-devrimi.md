<p>Vienna-based mobile data capture and analytics company <a href="https://anyline.com/"><u>Anyline</u></a> announced on February 19 the launch of a new autonomous drone inventory management software for warehouses. <a href="https://anyline.com/"><u>Anyline</u></a> disclosed that it has partnered with multinational paper and packaging company <a href="https://www.mondigroup.com/"><u>Mondi</u></a> to develop this autonomous drone barcode scanning technology.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Before drones were deployed, scanning inventory in large warehouses required multiple workers to manually lift items using forklifts. However, according to Anyline, this technology enables a single drone operator to easily scan inventory, creating a safer and more accurate tracking process for warehouses. <a href="https://anyline.com/"><u>Anyline</u></a> CEO and co-founder <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/lukas-kinigadner/"><u>Lukas Kinigadner</u></a> said, "Both Anyline and Mondi are passionate about innovation. We are proud to be part of this project that is redefining what is technologically possible in inventory management, though this is also applicable to other industries. We look forward to continuing to work with Mondi and other organizations and to inspiring others to innovate and transform the way they work."</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5><strong>Key Takeaways</strong></h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p><a href="https://anyline.com/"><u>Anyline</u></a> launched autonomous drone inventory management software for warehouses.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The technology was developed through a collaboration between Anyline and multinational company Mondi.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Previously, scanning inventory in large warehouses required multiple workers.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Inventory scanning is now possible with a single drone operator.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The technology provides a safer and more accurate tracking process in warehouses.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Anyline and Mondi are committed to innovation.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The project is redefining technological boundaries in inventory management and is applicable to other industries.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.supplychainbrain.com/articles/39130-anyline-launches-autonomous-drone-barcode-scanning-technology"><u>https://www.supplychainbrain.com/articles/39130-anyline-launches-autonomous-drone-barcode-scanning-technology</u></a></p>
