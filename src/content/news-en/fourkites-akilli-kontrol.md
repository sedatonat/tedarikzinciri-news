<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FourKites</strong> has introduced its <strong>Intelligent Control Tower™</strong> platform to advance supply chain operations. The platform integrates real-time data, digital twins, and AI-powered automation, setting it apart from traditional control towers. While conventional control towers typically focus on retrospective analysis, <strong>Intelligent Control Tower</strong> enables proactive decision-making and autonomous actions within supply chain workflows. <a href="https://www.porttechnology.org/news/fourkites-launches-intelligent-control-tower-for-advanced-supply-chain-automation/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">porttechnology.org</a></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">A core feature of the platform is <strong>FourKites</strong>' extensive supply chain data network, which tracks more than 3.2 million shipments daily across more than 200 countries. It also provides visibility over shipments, orders, and assets using digital twins and automates tasks such as supplier management, appointment scheduling, and order management through a digital workforce of AI agents. <a href="https://www.porttechnology.org/news/fourkites-launches-intelligent-control-tower-for-advanced-supply-chain-automation/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">porttechnology.org</a></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FourKites</strong> CEO and founder <strong>Mathew Elenjickal</strong> noted that real-time transportation visibility has become standard practice, and companies now seek to extract greater value from their supply chain data. With <strong>Intelligent Control Tower</strong>, he emphasized that the industry is progressing from viewing data to automating actions. <a href="https://www.porttechnology.org/news/fourkites-launches-intelligent-control-tower-for-advanced-supply-chain-automation/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">porttechnology.org+1porttechnology.org+1</a></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Intelligent Control Tower</strong> offers seven specialized packages:​</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Supplier Connect AI</strong>: Streamlines inbound collaboration.​</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>PO Connect AI</strong>: Optimizes purchase orders.​</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Customer Connect AI</strong>: Enhances customer satisfaction.​</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">These packages aim to simplify workflows, reduce manual processes, and improve performance across the supply chain. <a href="https://www.porttechnology.org/news/fourkites-launches-intelligent-control-tower-for-advanced-supply-chain-automation/" rel="noopener" target="_blank">porttechnology.org</a></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FourKites</strong> joined the <strong>Scheduling Standards Consortium (SSC)</strong> in May 2024, becoming the first real-time visibility platform to do so. The consortium aims to enhance the efficiency of supply chain technology. <strong>SSC</strong> comprises members including <strong>Arrive Logistics</strong>, <strong>BlueYonder</strong>, <strong>Coyote Logistics</strong>, <strong>DHL</strong>, <strong>e2open</strong>, <strong>Echo</strong>, <strong>J.B. Hunt Transport</strong>, <strong>Lineage</strong>, <strong>Mastery</strong>, <strong>One Network Enterprises</strong>, <strong>Oracle</strong>, <strong>Ryder</strong>, <strong>TI NTG</strong>, <strong>Uber Freight</strong>, <strong>Werner</strong>, and <strong>Worldwide Express</strong>. ​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>SSC</strong> was established in 2022 by <strong>J.B. Hunt</strong> and <strong>Uber Freight</strong> to develop the first global appointment scheduling standard in API format. The standard was scheduled to launch on October 2, 2023, enabling carriers to communicate through a single interface.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FourKites</strong> introduced <strong>Fin AI</strong>, a new natural language interface, in September 2023. This interface helps customers uncover hidden insights. <strong>Fin AI</strong> provides insights from <strong>FourKites</strong>' extensive data network, enabling customers to analyze the impact of supply chain disruptions and quickly identify and act on affected shipments.​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, <strong>FourKites</strong> launched a new <strong>Inbound Visibility</strong> solution in November 2023 to manage inbound orders and shipments across all modes. This solution enables users to analyze real-time data throughout the entire order lifecycle, aiming to enhance companies' ability to accurately manage all inbound shipments and orders. ​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FourKites</strong> made several improvements to its <strong>Dynamic Ocean</strong> ocean visibility solution in February 2024. These new features aim to help users mitigate disruptions and reduce preventable detention and demurrage fees. <strong>Dynamic Ocean</strong> delivers end-to-end real-time tracking with advanced document management capabilities, collaboration features, and reservation support. ​</p>
