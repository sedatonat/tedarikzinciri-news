<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Walmart</strong>'s white-label delivery service <strong>Walmart GoLocal</strong> is being integrated with <strong>IBM</strong>'s order management system <strong>IBM Sterling Order Management</strong>, offering retailers easier access and implementation capabilities. This integration aims to enable retail customers to integrate <strong>Walmart GoLocal</strong> into their business systems in a "user-friendly and highly customizable" manner.&nbsp;</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>IBM Sterling Order Management</strong> helps retailers accurately monitor inventory levels, coordinate third-party logistics services, and organize customer orders with shipping options. When an order is placed and ready to be shipped, users can take advantage of various delivery options offered by <strong>Walmart GoLocal</strong>, such as same-day and next-day delivery. ​</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">This partnership between <strong>Walmart GoLocal</strong> and <strong>IBM</strong> is viewed as part of the service's strategy to expand its customer base. The service became available on <strong>Salesforce Commerce Cloud</strong> in 2023, which enabled retailers like <strong>Hibbett</strong> to access the service more easily. Since its launch in 2021, <strong>Walmart GoLocal</strong> has completed over 30 million deliveries and has diversified its service offerings beyond light parcel delivery from stores.</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">This integration enables retailers to enhance efficiency and grow their businesses by leveraging <strong>Walmart GoLocal</strong>'s extensive last-mile delivery network and strong delivery capabilities. The integration of <strong>Walmart GoLocal</strong> with <strong>IBM</strong>'s order management system further simplifies retailers' delivery processes and improves customer experience. ​</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Walmart GoLocal</strong> is integrated with <strong>IBM Sterling Order Management</strong> to provide retailers with easier access and implementation options.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>IBM Sterling Order Management</strong> helps retailers monitor inventory levels, coordinate logistics services, and manage customer orders.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Walmart GoLocal</strong> has completed over 30 million deliveries since 2021 and has expanded its service offerings.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">This integration simplifies retailers' delivery processes and improves customer experience.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p>---</p>\n<p><strong>News Link: </strong><a href="https://www.supplychaindive.com/news/walmart-golocal-ibm-sterling-order-management/738103/"><u>https://www.supplychaindive.com/news/walmart-golocal-ibm-sterling-order-management/738103/</u></a></p>\n<p>-------------------</p>\n<p><strong>!!!
