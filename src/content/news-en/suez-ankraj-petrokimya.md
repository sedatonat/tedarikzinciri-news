<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>The Suez Canal Authority (SCA)</strong> signed a strategic partnership agreement on <strong>October 22, 2025</strong> with <strong>Anchorage Investments</strong> to establish a <strong>large-scale petrochemical complex</strong> in the <strong>Ain Sokhna</strong> region within the <strong>Suez Canal Economic Zone (SCEZ)</strong>, located northwest of the Gulf of Suez. This agreement is considered a significant step toward diversifying Egypt's industrial base and strengthening its export capacity in the energy sector.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The initial phase of the project constitutes the launch phase of the <strong>Anchor Benitoite Complex</strong>. This phase will involve <strong>over $2 billion in investment</strong> and will produce <strong>polypropylene derived from propane</strong> as the primary product. <strong>Hydrogen</strong> will be obtained as a byproduct. Polypropylene, a high-value-added raw material widely used in packaging, textiles, automotive, and construction sectors, will <strong>directly contribute to Egypt's export revenues</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>second phase</strong> of the project will encompass increased production capacity and the establishment of <strong>complementary industrial units</strong>. In this phase, <strong>additional petrochemical products</strong>, <strong>sustainability-focused production facilities</strong>, and <strong>export-oriented production lines</strong> will be added. Upon completion of the second phase, the total investment is expected to reach <strong>$4.5 billion</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">In a statement by the Suez Canal Authority, it was noted that the project will <strong>enable efficient utilization of institutional assets</strong>, form part of <strong>a strategy to diversify revenue streams</strong>, and <strong>generate foreign exchange earnings for Egypt's economy</strong>. Additionally, the facility is expected to create <strong>more than 2,500 employment opportunities</strong>, both direct and indirect.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The SCA, established following Egypt's nationalization of the Suez Canal in 1956, is a <strong>state-owned authority</strong> responsible for the <strong>operation, maintenance, and development of the canal</strong>. The authority also manages canal revenues, <strong>oversees expansion projects</strong>, and <strong>regulates maritime traffic</strong> on this strategically vital waterway <strong>connecting the Mediterranean to the Red Sea</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The SCA President stated that the agreement will accelerate Egypt's <strong>transformation in the petrochemical and energy sector</strong>, saying: "This project will be a watershed moment for our country not only from an economic standpoint but also in terms of technological innovation and sustainability."</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The project's private-sector partner, <strong>Anchorage Investments</strong>, is an Egypt-based <strong>industrial investment and development company</strong> focused on <strong>petrochemicals, chemicals, and mining</strong> sectors. The company is recognized for its <strong>export-oriented production model</strong> and <strong>sustainable growth strategy</strong>. Its founder, <strong>Dr. Ahmed Moharram</strong>, stands out as a figure who has <strong>pioneered large-scale industrial investments</strong> in Egypt's economic zones.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The project aligns with objectives to <strong>strengthen industrial infrastructure</strong> around the Suez Canal, <strong>optimize the use of energy resources</strong>, and establish <strong>Egypt as a regional petrochemical hub</strong>. It was also announced that the production process will incorporate environmental criteria such as <strong>decarbonisation</strong> and <strong>waste recycling</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">According to analysts, this partnership has the potential to create not only benefits for Egypt but also establish <strong>a new energy and petrochemical hub on the Mediterranean–Red Sea trade corridor</strong>. Once the project becomes operational, the region will become a strategic production point <strong>providing direct access to Middle Eastern and African markets</strong>.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>SCA</strong> and <strong>Anchorage Investments</strong> signed an agreement for a <strong>$2 billion petrochemical complex in Ain Sokhna</strong>.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The complex forms the initial phase of the <strong>Anchor Benitoite</strong> project.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Primary product: <strong>Polypropylene derived from propane</strong>; byproduct: <strong>Hydrogen.</strong></p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Total investment will reach $4.5 billion upon completion of the second phase</strong>.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The project aims to <strong>increase Egypt's foreign exchange earnings and create more than 2,500 jobs</strong>.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>SCA</strong> is the state-owned authority responsible for operating and developing the Suez Canal.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Anchorage Investments</strong> is an Egypt-based private company known for <strong>sustainability-focused, export-oriented industrial investments</strong>.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>----------</p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://en.portnews.ru/news/383698/"><u>https://en.portnews.ru/news/383698/</u></a></p>\n<p>--------------------</p>\n<p><strong>Author: </strong><a href="http://sedatonat.com/"><u>SedatOnat.com</u></a></p>\n<p>--------------------</p>\n<p><strong>!!!
