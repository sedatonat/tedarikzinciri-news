<p>The X5 Nozomi system, developed through a collaboration between Packsize and EFI, combines box production and full-color direct printing in a single workflow, adding a new capacity layer to e-commerce and retail order fulfillment operations.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The system cuts, folds, and assembles boxes to custom dimensions on-demand in a single line, while simultaneously printing product and shipping information, regulatory labels, and brand visuals directly onto the surface. This eliminates pre-printed inventory, labeling, and secondary printing steps; reduces in-line cycle time; and minimizes unnecessary movement and waste. Right-sizing minimizes empty space, thereby controlling shipping costs and emissions.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>At the core of the solution, EFI Nozomi's industrial inkjet printing capability merges with Packsize's on-demand box manufacturing technology. The synchronized operation of on-line measurement, cutting/folding, and printing stations enables previously separate processes to be controlled in a single pass. This integration adapts to SKU diversification during high-demand periods and allows customization in small batches. Particularly in reverse logistics and returns management, dynamic data printing on the box surface increases speed and accuracy in the return flow.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The system operates with lower minimum order thresholds compared to traditional pre-print approaches, reducing supply and planning complexity. On the operational side, improved takt time balance results from reduced labor movement and wait times through optimized station layout in a single box flow. For quality assurance, camera-based inspections and in-line measurement sensors monitor print alignment, cutting accuracy, and box integrity. Color profiles, brand standardization, and regulatory requirements are managed through digital templates, reducing human error.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>From a customer experience perspective, the "unboxing" quality improves through personalized designs and campaign messages on the box surface. Easy field installation, flexible single-operator shift planning, and RaaS-like pricing models lower the CAPEX barrier. As scale increases, multi-box production per minute and lights-out operation scenarios become possible. This enables rapidly growing brands to manage capacity during promotional periods and peak seasons.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Ultimately, the X5 Nozomi delivers an integrated "box-as-a-service" architecture that simultaneously supports cost, speed, quality, and sustainability objectives. Warehouse floor space efficiency increases, in-line cycle time decreases, and shipping optimization and brand perception improve simultaneously. After installation, the management of the measure-print-assemble workflow in a single pass creates a stock-independent, demand-driven packaging operation.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong><br>\n- X5 Nozomi can produce boxes and print full-color graphics in a single workflow.<br>\n- On-demand right-sizing reduces shipping costs and carbon footprint.<br>\n- Digital templates and in-line quality control reduce waste.<br>\n- Single-operator flexibility and RaaS-like models lower the CAPEX barrier.<br>\n- Dynamic data and personalization on the box surface enhance customer experience.</p>\n<p><br>\n---<br>\n<strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.supplychainbrain.com/articles/42609-packsize-efi-debut-combined-packaging-and-printing-system"><u>https://www.supplychainbrain.com/articles/42609-packsize-efi-debut-combined-packaging-and-printing-system</u></a><br>\n-------------------</p>\n<p><strong>!!!
