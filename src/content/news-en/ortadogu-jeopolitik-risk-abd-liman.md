<p>Emre Eldener, Vice Chairman of the Board at Kıta Logistics, told Bloomberg HT that geopolitical risks in the Middle East are negatively impacting Turkey's logistics sector. Flight permits are being cancelled due to Hezbollah threats, while Suez Canal disruptions are complicating container shipping. He emphasized that Turkish exporters are losing business to regions such as Iran, India, and Southeast Asia.
