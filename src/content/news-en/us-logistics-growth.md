<p>The U.S. logistics sector is experiencing a slowdown in growth rates amid declining demand and rising cost pressures. According to reports, freight demand declined 3% in the latest quarter. Warehouse utilization rates have also deteriorated. While e-commerce volume remains stable, rising fuel prices are eroding profitability. Experts note that companies are continuing their search for efficiency gains by turning to automation and AI-based optimization solutions.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>Key Points:</strong><br>\n1.
