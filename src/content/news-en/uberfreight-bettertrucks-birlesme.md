<p style="text-align: justify">Uber Freight has invested with a <strong>minority stake in Better Trucks</strong> to expand its last-mile delivery network and strengthen its technological capabilities. This move enables the expansion of the commercial partnership between the two companies and <strong>significantly increases customer delivery coverage</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Under the new arrangement, Uber Freight and Better Trucks will be able to <strong>transfer delivery volume to each other's service areas</strong>. This allows both companies to expand their geographic coverage while delivering faster and more consistent delivery experiences to customers.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Uber Freight: Coverage Expands from 68% to 85%</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to the partnership announcement, with Better Trucks' capacity added, Uber Freight's last-mile network now reaches <strong>approximately 68% of the U.S. population</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
<strong>Hany Elkordy</strong>, SVP of Uber Freight Last Mile, said the company aims to <strong>increase coverage to 85% by the end of 2026</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Better Trucks has strong operational capabilities, particularly in <strong>densely populated urban areas</strong>.<br>
According to Elkordy, this combination is critical:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Uber Freight:</strong> broad regional connections and end-to-end network</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Better Trucks:</strong> cost-effective last-mile distribution in high-density areas + strong technology</p></li>
</ul>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"We bring the end-to-end connectivity; they provide incredibly strong technology in these areas from a cost perspective," said Elkordy.</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Address Validation and Geocoding: Technology That Reduces Costs</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Better Trucks' advanced address validation and geocoding technologies are viewed as one of the most valuable assets the company brings to the partnership.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This technology:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Validates the address provided by the sender,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Identifies incomplete or incorrect addresses,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Converts the address to precise geographic coordinates.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">As a result:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">First-attempt delivery success rates increase,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Error-related additional costs (redelivery, missing access codes, etc.) decrease.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Elkordy's commentary summarizes the situation:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"There's nothing worse than a failed first delivery attempt due to an incorrect address."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Open API Architecture: Integrated End-to-End Technology Ecosystem</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Better Trucks' <strong>open API</strong> structure also provides significant advantages for Uber Freight.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">For example, if an Uber Freight customer previously used tracking software that Better Trucks was integrated with, Uber Freight can <strong>activate this connection directly</strong>.<br>
This means:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Integration costs decrease,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Customer experience accelerates,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Multiple software connections are unified under one roof.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This architectural approach makes a significant difference, particularly in <strong>multi-system e-commerce operations</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Better Trucks' Opportunity: Broader Customer Base + Investment Support</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Andy Whiting</strong>, CEO of Better Trucks, said that Uber Freight's investment will increase the company's technology capacity and support it in scaling its operations.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Better Trucks is planning by 2026 to:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Launch operations in more major cities,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Open new facilities,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Expand its last-mile network nationwide<br></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Volume from Uber Freight will further strengthen Better Trucks' position in <strong>high-demand areas</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Elkordy also shared this detail:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"In some markets, Uber Direct shipments are coming entirely out of Better Trucks facilities."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">An important indicator showing how deep this integration is becoming.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Investment Growth Possible, but Uncertain</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The parties did not disclose the financial terms of the partnership.<br>
Whether Uber Freight will increase its stake in the future remains unclear.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Elkordy:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"It depends on how things progress in the coming year. We're monitoring the development."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Uber Freight became a <strong>minority shareholder</strong> in Better Trucks.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Goal: increase last-mile coverage to <strong>85%</strong> and strengthen technology.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Better Trucks: provides address validation, geocoding, open API, and strong urban performance.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Partnership includes mutual volume flow and shared network utilization.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The collaboration will open new markets for Uber Direct and other last-mile solutions.</p></li>
</ul>
