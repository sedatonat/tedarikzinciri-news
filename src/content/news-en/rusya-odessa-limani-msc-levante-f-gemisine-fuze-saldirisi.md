<p style="text-align: justify">On March 1, 2025, during a missile attack on the <strong>Odessa</strong> port conducted by <strong>Russia</strong>, the Panama-flagged container vessel <strong>MSC Levante F</strong> was targeted. In the attack, carried out with two <strong>Iskander-M</strong> missiles, the vessel sustained minor damage and departed the port safely.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">During the strike, the bulk carrier <strong>Super Sarkas</strong> was also hit. The vessel was carrying 21,000 tons of corn and soybeans. As a result of the attack, the <strong>Super Sarkas</strong> vessel sustained severe damage.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Odessa</strong> regional governor <strong>Oleg Kiper</strong> stated that the attack damaged port infrastructure and affected a civilian vessel. Ukrainian First Deputy Prime Minister <strong>Oleksiy Kuleba</strong> indicated that a second vessel was also struck in the attack.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Russia</strong> claimed that the <strong>MSC Levante F</strong> vessel was loaded with marine drones and weapons belonging to <strong>Britain</strong> from a <strong>NATO</strong> base in <strong>Turkey</strong>. However, no concrete evidence was presented to support these claims.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The attack is viewed as part of <strong>Russia</strong>'s ongoing military operations against <strong>Ukraine</strong>. Such strikes have resulted in negative impacts on maritime transport and port operations in the region.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Russia</strong> conducted a missile attack on <strong>Odessa</strong> port on March 1, 2025.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>MSC Levante F</strong> vessel sustained minor damage and departed the port.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>Super Sarkas</strong> vessel suffered severe damage.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Russia</strong> claimed the vessel was carrying weapons; no evidence was presented.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The attack negatively impacted maritime transport and port operations in the region.</p></li>
</ul>
