<p>CMA CGM is collaborating with Google AI to digitally transform its logistics operations. The initiative aims to optimize vessel routing, container management, and inventory control. Ceva Logistics will be the first unit to implement this technology and will adopt a data-driven approach to warehouse management. An intelligent management tool developed with Google's technology will enable demand forecasting and improve operational planning. The move aims to both reduce costs and minimize carbon footprint.</p>
