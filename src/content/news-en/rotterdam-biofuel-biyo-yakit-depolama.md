<p style="text-align: justify">International maritime fuel supplier <strong>Peninsula</strong> has expanded its infrastructure at the Port of Rotterdam by commissioning a new <strong>bio-fuel storage and blending facility</strong> at Chane Terminal. According to the company's statement, the project represents a strategic investment toward low-carbon fuel solutions, which are experiencing growing demand in the global maritime sector.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Phase 1: 30,000 m³ capacity, nine new tanks</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The first phase of the project has become fully operational and includes nine bio-fuel tanks with a total volume of <strong>30,000 cubic meters</strong>. These tanks enable safe storage and blending of <strong>ISCC-certified</strong> sustainable bio-fuels.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new installation includes:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>3 wharves</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>7 barge berths</strong> (for barges up to 135 meters),</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>A separate marine vessel wharf</strong><br>
This infrastructure also enables <strong>inter-terminal transfers</strong> with major oil companies, accelerating logistics.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Phase 2: Expansion to 110,000 m³ capacity in 2026</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The second phase is planned to come online in <strong>January 2026</strong>. This phase will add eight new tanks and bring total storage capacity to <strong>110,000 m³</strong>. This scale will position Peninsula as one of Europe's largest low-carbon fuel hubs.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Infrastructure capable of blending all types of bio-fuels</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The facility has been designed to support all bio-fuel blends used in maritime operations.<br>
Blending capabilities include:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>B5, B10, B20, B30</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Mid-level blends,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Fully bio-fuel <strong>B100</strong><br>
.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This capability gives Peninsula the advantage of <strong>controlling product quality and blending ratios in-house</strong>. This represents a critical capability for bunker suppliers seeking to offer customers customized low-carbon solutions.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>ISCC-certified facility serving the green bunker market</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new facility at Chane Terminal:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Is <strong>ISCC (International Sustainability &amp; Carbon Certification)</strong> certified,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Is <strong>NEA registered</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Complies with EU renewable fuel standards.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This positions Peninsula to offer a broader product portfolio in compliance with low-carbon fuel market regulations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A significant step in Peninsula's global strategy</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Peninsula is a commercial fuel supplier managing an extensive global bunker supply network. The company:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">manages fuel supply operations,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">distribution and logistics networks,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">coastal and offshore supply solutions<br>
using its own assets.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This expansion in Rotterdam is significant for Peninsula as it:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">responds to rising demand for low-carbon marine fuels,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">strengthens its competitive position in the European bunker market,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">prepares the company for emission regulations under IMO and EU ETS<br>
.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to Peninsula's statement, the facility will offer maritime customers greater fuel choice flexibility while making the company's portfolio more sustainable as the energy transition progresses.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Peninsula opened a <strong>30,000 m³</strong> bio-fuel facility at Rotterdam Chane Terminal.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Phase 2 will bring total capacity to <strong>110,000 m³</strong> (January 2026).</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Blending capability for all bio-fuel mixtures: <strong>B5–B100</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The facility is ISCC certified and NEA registered.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">3 wharves, 7 barge berths, and a marine vessel wharf are available.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Peninsula is substantially expanding its capacity in the low-carbon bunker market.</p></li>
</ul>
