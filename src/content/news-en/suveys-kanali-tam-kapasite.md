<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Suez Canal Authority (SCA)</strong> President <strong>Admiral Osama Rabiee</strong> announced that the canal is ready to operate at full capacity and that shipping lines have returned to their regular routes. This development demonstrates that the Suez Canal, a critical junction in global trade, has regained its former functionality.​</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>The Canal's Importance and the Crisis</strong></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The Suez Canal is the shortest sea route between Asia and Europe, with approximately 12% of world trade passing through this corridor. In 2021, the massive container vessel <strong>Ever Given</strong> ran aground, blocking the canal for six days and causing billions of dollars in losses to global commerce. This incident once again highlighted the importance of alternative routes and risk management in the shipping sector.​</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Measures and Improvements Implemented</strong></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Following the crisis, the <strong>SCA</strong> implemented various measures to prevent similar incidents from recurring. Projects to widen and deepen the canal were accelerated. Additionally, vessel traffic management systems were modernized, and training programs were organized for captains and crew members. These improvements aim to enhance the canal's safety and capacity.​</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Shipping Lines' Return</strong></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Admiral Rabiee</strong> noted that many major shipping companies have begun to favor the Suez Canal again. Particularly, firms operating on the Asia-Europe route are benefiting from the time and cost advantages offered by the canal. This return is regarded as an indicator that the canal has regained its former reliability.​</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Effects on Global Trade</strong></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The operation of the Suez Canal at full capacity is critical to the stability of global supply chains. The canal's efficiency provides significant advantages for companies operating particularly in the energy transportation and fast-moving consumer goods sectors. Furthermore, it contributes to the stabilization of freight rates and the reduction of delivery times.​</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Future Plans</strong></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>SCA</strong> is developing long-term plans to enhance the canal's capacity and security. Through digitalization and automation projects, the acceleration of ship transit processes is targeted. Additionally, investments are being made in green energy projects as part of environmental sustainability initiatives. These steps aim to ensure that the Suez Canal maintains its strategic position in global trade in the future.​</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>Suez Canal</strong> handles 12% of world trade.​</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>Ever Given</strong> crisis exposed the canal's importance and vulnerability.​</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Improvement measures implemented have enhanced the canal's safety and capacity.​</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Major shipping companies are once again favoring the Suez Canal.​</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The canal's efficiency contributes to the stability of global supply chains.​</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>SCA</strong> is investing in digitalization and environmental sustainability projects.​</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p style="text-align: justify">These developments reinforce the Suez Canal's indispensable role in global commerce and demonstrate that it will continue to maintain its strategic importance in the future.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>---</p>\n\n<p>-------------------</p>\n<p><strong>!!!
