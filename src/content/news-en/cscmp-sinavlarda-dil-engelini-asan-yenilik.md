<p>CSCMP (Council of Supply Chain Management Professionals) has introduced an "Instant Translate" tool to expand global access for its members. This tool makes the SCPro Assessment, SCPro Fundamentals, and SCPro Level 1 certification exams accessible in 22 different languages, eliminating language barriers. The interactive tool performs real-time translations in candidates' preferred languages during testing, enabling test-takers to feel more comfortable and comprehend the material more clearly. The tool's flexible functionality allows candidates to turn translation services on and off as needed during the exam.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Points:</h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>CSCMP has enhanced global member access by adding the "Instant Translate" feature to its exams.</p></li>\n  <li><p>This feature can provide translations in 22 different languages.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Candidates can highlight text during the exam to receive instant translations.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The feature helps candidates gain greater confidence and comprehension during testing.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10456-cscmp-launches-instant-translate-feature-for-scpro-exams"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10456-cscmp-launches-instant-translate-feature-for-scpro-exams</u></a></p>
