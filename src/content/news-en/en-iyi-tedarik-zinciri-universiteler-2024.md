<p>Gartner has ranked the leading universities in North America for supply chain education. <strong>University of Arkansas once again topped the undergraduate program rankings. University of Tennessee maintained its leadership in graduate programs.</strong> These rankings are based on criteria such as program scope, industry value, and program size. Additionally, it was noted that interest in analytics within supply chain education is growing, while manufacturing-focused content is declining.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Highlights:</h5>\n<ul>\n  <li><p>University of Arkansas ranks first in undergraduate programs.</p></li>\n  <li><p>University of Tennessee ranks first in graduate programs.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The importance of analytics in supply chain education is increasing.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Manufacturing-focused content is declining.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10579-gartner-ranks-best-colleges-for-studying-supply-chain"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10579-gartner-ranks-best-colleges-for-studying-supply-chain</u></a></p>
