<p style="text-align: justify">As sustainability pressure mounts on port operations across Europe, <strong>Port of Bilbao</strong> is hosting a project positioned to solve hydrogen technology's most critical challenge in port logistics—high power requirements. <em>H2TowTractor</em> is being field-tested with a <strong>retrofit hydrogen kit</strong> that can be easily applied to existing fleets rather than replacing entire industrial tractor units. This test stands out as the first real-world operational application of its kind in Europe.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h3 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Why is hydrogen particularly relevant for ports?</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: justify">Electric vehicles are becoming more common in port operations, but they have serious limitations:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Long charging times,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Power loss in continuous start-stop heavy operations,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Insufficient charging infrastructure for 24/7 port operations.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">For ports, this equation translates to: high power + fast refueling = <strong>hydrogen</strong>. The greatest advantage demonstrated by H2TowTractor in this test is its hybrid use of both battery-electric and hydrogen power.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h3 style="text-align: justify"><strong>What does the plug-and-play retrofit kit deliver?</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: justify">At the heart of the project lies a <strong>modular retrofit kit</strong> integrated into the ATA E405AC model. The plug-and-play system consists of:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">An optimized <strong>fuel cell</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">A high-durability <strong>lithium-ion buffer battery</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">working in combination.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The most critical benefit of this model is:</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">👉 <strong>No fleet replacement investment required.</strong><br>
👉 Zero-emission transition is achieved by installing the kit on existing diesel or electric tractors.<br>
👉 Accelerates compliance with strict European regulations.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This enables ports to achieve transformation at relatively low cost rather than spending hundreds of thousands of euros per vehicle on new purchases.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h3 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Strategic objectives of the H2TOW project</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: justify">The project, funded by EIT Urban Mobility, is built on four clear targets:</p>
<ol>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Increase hydrogen adoption</strong>: Pave the way for hydrogen proliferation in logistics and industrial transport.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Develop a viable retrofit kit</strong>: A modular design adaptable to various tractor and forklift models.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Reduce transition costs and regulatory barriers</strong>: Make certification and permitting processes suitable for retrofit applications.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Improve perception of hydrogen safety</strong>: Demonstrate safe usage scenarios in real operating environments.</p></li>
</ol>
<p style="text-align: justify">All of these objectives are fully aligned with European ports and airports' carbon-neutral strategies.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h3 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Project ecosystem: A multi-stakeholder collaboration</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: justify">Organizations participating in the consortium:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>AESS – Modena Energy Agency</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>ATA Tow Tractors</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Arco Technologies</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>CAAB Bologna Agro-Food Center</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Basque Mobility &amp; Logistics Cluster</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, operational tests are being conducted at the <strong>Bunge Ibérica</strong> terminal. Fuel supply is provided by <strong>Petronor</strong>, and on-site support by <strong>IBIL</strong>. This demonstrates that the project has both a strong technical and operational infrastructure.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h3 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Why did Port of Bilbao become the pioneer?</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: justify">Port of Bilbao's sustainability agenda includes:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Carbon-neutral operations,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Development of alternative fuel ecosystems,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Smart logistics solutions,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Energy transition programs</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">which is why testing hydrogen-powered tractors stands out as a strategic pilot initiative.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Port management believes this technology carries significant potential especially for high-power vehicles such as <strong>container carrier tractors</strong>, <strong>RoRo operations</strong>, and <strong>airport baggage tractors</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h3 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Why does it matter?</strong></h3>
<p style="text-align: justify">This test opens three critical doors for European ports:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>A high-power solution that overcomes electric vehicle limitations</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>100 percent zero-emission tractor operations</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Low-cost retrofit conversion instead of new vehicle investment</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">For this reason, H2TowTractor presents a model that can drive transformation in port logistics not only environmentally but also economically and operationally.</p>
