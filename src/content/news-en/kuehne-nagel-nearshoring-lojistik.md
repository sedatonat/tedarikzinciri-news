<p>Kuehne+Nagel has announced the opening of a new logistics facility spanning 363,000 square meters in Texas, located at the U.S.-Mexico border. The new site consolidates four warehouses from the other side of the border under one roof. This consolidation, occurring amid the nearshoring trend where global companies are increasing their investments in Mexico, offers customers the opportunity to redesign their supply chain processes. The facility will become operational on August 5 and will feature advanced security systems, modern offices, 54 loading gates, front and rear loading-unloading capacity, 125 truck parking spaces, and additional vertical racking storage areas.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The company notes that combining four warehouses at a single point will enhance efficiency by enabling all trailer traffic to be controlled from one location. This move will reduce dwell times through the addition of extra loading gates that enable trucks to be picked up and unloaded more quickly, and will improve cargo flow and organization.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5><strong>Key Points</strong></h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>Kuehne+Nagel is opening a 363,000 square-meter logistics facility in El Paso, Texas.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The new facility consolidates four separate warehouses at a single location.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Opening date: August 5.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Facility features: Advanced security, modern offices, 54 loading gates, cross-docking capacity, 125 truck parking spaces, additional vertical racking storage areas.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The facility's opening provides customers with advantages by delivering efficiency in supply chain processes and improving cargo flow.</p></li>\n  <li><p>This step supports the nearshoring trend, encouraging global companies to invest in Mexico.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10323-kuehnenagel-opens-texas-facility-to-support-nearshoring-trend"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10323-kuehnenagel-opens-texas-facility-to-support-nearshoring-trend</u></a></p>
