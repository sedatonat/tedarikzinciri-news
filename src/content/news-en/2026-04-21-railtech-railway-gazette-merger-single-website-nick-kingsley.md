<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>RailFreight.com</strong>'s sister publications <strong>RailTech.com</strong> and <strong>Railway Gazette International</strong> merged on <strong>Monday, 20 April</strong> to continue as a single website. The merger of the publications was presented as part of a strategy to cover the global railway sector with a stronger editorial team. Railway Gazette International Editor-in-Chief <strong>Nick Kingsley</strong> said: "More than ever, the global railway sector needs reliable, authoritative, and independent reporting."</p><p style="text-align: justify">After the merger, the Railway Gazette website switched to a new design. Visitors to RailTech.com will now be redirected to <strong>railwaygazette.com</strong>. RailTech's <strong>archive containing all articles written over the past ten years</strong> has also moved to Railway Gazette. Current RailTech subscribers will receive an equivalent Railway Gazette subscription free of charge and their expiry dates will be preserved.</p><p style="text-align: justify">The merger was seen as a response to financial pressures facing the publishing sector. A larger editorial team will cover infrastructure developments, technology innovations, policy changes, and climate transition projects in greater depth. Railway Gazette is known as a publication that has witnessed the history of the railway sector since 1835, and with this merger has strengthened its position in the digital age.</p>
