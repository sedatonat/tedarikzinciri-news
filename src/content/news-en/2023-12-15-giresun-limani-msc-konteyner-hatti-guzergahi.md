<p><a href="https://www.msc.com/"><u>Through collaboration with MSC (Mediterranean Shipping Company)</u></a>, Giresun Port has been added to MSC's container line.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>At Giresun Port, containers valued at approximately 2 million dollars and containing pet food, minerals, and refined oil products were loaded onto the Gözde Bayraktar vessel, which will operate a route from Giresun to Tekirdağ.&nbsp;</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><strong>Several key aspects of this shipment:</strong></p>
<ol>
  <li><p><strong>Regional Economic Development</strong>: Giresun Port's entry into container shipping represents an important step for regional economic development. This advance will boost the export capacity of Giresun and surrounding provinces, revitalizing the regional economy.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>Expansion of Logistics Network</strong>: The addition of Giresun Port to the route of a major shipping company like MSC increases the region's visibility and accessibility within the international logistics network. This creates opportunities for increased trade and investment.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>Export Diversification</strong>: The variety of products being shipped—pet food, minerals, and refined oil—expands the region's export portfolio. This diversification provides access to different markets and ensures economic stability.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>Infrastructure and Workforce Development</strong>: This new functionality of the port may require infrastructure improvements and the creation of additional employment. This can have positive effects on the region's labor market.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>International Relations and Reputation</strong>: The voyage of the Gözde Bayraktar vessel, flying the Turkish flag, can enhance Turkey's reputation and credibility in international commerce.</p></li>
</ol>
