<p>Swiss-based logistics giant <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuehne_%2B_Nagel"><u>Kuehne+Nagel</u></a> is reorganizing its corporate structure to become more agile and better positioned to adapt quickly to market conditions. The company is eliminating its regional management structure and shifting to a model in which country organizations report directly to the board of management. The restructuring aims to strengthen customer proximity and enhance the company's ability to adapt rapidly to shifting market dynamics.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The new reporting structure will also unlock additional efficiency gains and a path to profitable growth. The historically evolved regional structure will be discontinued, and responsibilities will be integrated into <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuehne_%2B_Nagel"><u>Kuehne+Nagel Group</u></a> functions. Cluster and Country Managers will report directly to <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuehne_%2B_Nagel"><u>Kuehne+Nagel</u></a> International AG's Board of Management. The company did not specify whether the restructuring would result in job cuts. <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuehne_%2B_Nagel"><u>Kuehne+Nagel</u></a> currently employs approximately 81,000 staff across nearly 1,300 locations in approximately 100 countries. With this structural change, Kuehne+Nagel aims to streamline its organizational structure and create conditions for faster and more flexible operations during an increasingly dynamic global trade environment.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5><strong>Key Points</strong></h5>\n<ul>\n  <li><p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kuehne_%2B_Nagel"><u>Kuehne+Nagel</u></a> is transitioning from a regional management structure to a direct board of management reporting model.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The restructuring aims to increase customer proximity and enable rapid adaptation to market changes.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The organizational change targets efficiency gains and a path to profitable growth.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The regional structure will be discontinued, with responsibilities integrated into group functions.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The company provided no information on whether job cuts would occur.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Kuehne+Nagel employs approximately 81,000 staff across nearly 1,300 locations in approximately 100 countries.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10077-kuehnenagel-streamlines-reporting-structure-to-boost-agility"><u>https://www.thescxchange.com/articles/10077-kuehnenagel-streamlines-reporting-structure-to-boost-agility</u></a></p>
