<p>Recent months have seen growing interest in the potential applications of Generative AI (GenAI) in procurement and supply chain processes. According to Gartner's latest research, this technology could achieve widespread adoption within two years. Generative AI is a technology capable of producing various types of content including text, images, and video, and is particularly known through popular examples such as ChatGPT. Gartner notes that GenAI is currently at the peak of "inflated expectations." However, this indicates that GenAI will rapidly mature and play a significant role in supply chains.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The article provides examples of how GenAI can be utilized in procurement and supply chain processes. Particular emphasis is placed on how GenAI can improve business operations in areas such as contract management, supplier sourcing, and supplier management. According to Gartner's research, widespread adoption of this technology could occur within two to five years. At the start of the year, 73% of supply chain leaders indicated they planned to adopt this technology by year-end.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Additionally, the article discusses how GenAI could revolutionize supply chain processes. For example, thanks to this technology, complex processes such as contract management can be performed more quickly and efficiently. However, the challenges encountered in the adoption of new technologies such as GenAI are also addressed. These include the technology's maturation process, user adaptation difficulties, and integration into existing business processes.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>\n<ol>\n  <li><p>GenAI is expected to be widely adopted in procurement and supply chain processes within two years.</p></li>\n  <li><p>GenAI has the potential to improve business processes in areas such as contract management and supplier management.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Gartner predicts that GenAI is currently at the peak of "inflated expectations," but will rapidly mature and become widespread.</p></li>\n  <li><p>73% of supply chain leaders plan to adopt GenAI by the end of 2024.</p></li>\n  <li><p>As the technology becomes widespread, significant increases in business process efficiency and speed are expected.</p></li>\n</ol>\n<p><br></p>\n
