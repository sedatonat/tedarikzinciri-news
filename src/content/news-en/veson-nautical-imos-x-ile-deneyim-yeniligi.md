<p>Veson Nautical has introduced <strong>IMOS X</strong>, a new user experience for the industry-leading IMOS Platform. While the existing IMOS Platform continues to be the primary solution for commercial freight contract management and voyage execution, the company is rapidly developing and validating new IMOS X functionalities alongside its customers. These innovations include significant improvements in demand workflows, voyage emissions data capture and reporting, and counterparty connectivity.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>John Veson</strong>, Founder and CEO of Veson Nautical, said: "Throughout Veson's journey, we have partnered with companies at the forefront of our industry. <strong>IMOS</strong> was built to accommodate complex cargo trading styles and diverse operational requirements. Our goal has always been to create a product that meets complex requirements while avoiding unnecessary complications."</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>"With IMOS X, we are further simplifying complexity for our users by streamlining workflows and seamlessly integrating data collaboration," he added.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Veson Nautical aims to provide its customers with a more efficient and integrated platform through IMOS X.
