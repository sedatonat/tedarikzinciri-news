<p>The use of electric motors in vessels is becoming a reality as the maritime sector moves to achieve zero-emission targets while simultaneously improving operational efficiency.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>The Port of Rotterdam Authority has taken action toward this goal, commissioning four 90-metre vessels to handle its inland logistics on interior waterways.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>For charging, the port plans to use containerized <a href="https://zeroemissionservices.nl/en/zespack-2/"><u>ZESPack</u></a><a href="https://zeroemissionservices.nl/en/zespack-2/"> </a>battery units. These 1MVA power packs are designed to reach a full charge in approximately three hours.</p>
