<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Georgian Railways</strong> announced the launch of a comprehensive modernization program aimed at increasing transport capacity along the <strong>Baku–Tbilisi–Kars (BTC) Railway Line</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
As part of this plan, construction of <strong>a new terminal in the Akhalkalaki region, near Georgia's border with Turkey, is set to begin in 2026.</strong></p>
<p><br>
Additionally, an <strong>electronic customs clearance system (e-customs clearance)</strong> will be deployed to reduce transit times and accelerate procedures.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The BTC line is one of the most critical links in the <strong>Middle Corridor</strong>, known as the <strong>China–Central Asia–Caucasus–Europe trade route.</strong> Georgia's investments aim to enhance the line's <strong>logistics capacity, efficiency, and competitiveness.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>New Akhalkalaki Terminal: Capacity Expansion and Connectivity</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new terminal will be constructed as an addition to the existing <strong>Akhalkalaki freight terminal</strong> currently in operation.</p>
<p><br>
Located <strong>in very close proximity to the Turkish border,</strong> the terminal will play a critical role, particularly in <strong>cargo transfer and gauge conversion</strong> processes.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The existing infrastructure on the BTC line has caused significant time losses in cargo transfers due to different track gauges between Europe and Asia.</p>
<p><br>
The new terminal will substantially reduce this timeframe through <strong>automated cargo transfer systems.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to Georgian Railways officials, upon project completion, <strong>regional trade volume will increase</strong> and the country will <strong>strengthen its position as the logistics hub of the Middle Corridor.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Digital Customs for Shorter Transit Times</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Another component of the project is the <strong>electronic customs clearance system.</strong></p>
<p><br>
Through this system, <strong>paper-based procedures at border crossing will be eliminated,</strong> and customs data will be <strong>shared across digital platforms.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new system aims to <strong>reduce bureaucratic delays,</strong> <strong>manage shipments on a 24-hour basis,</strong> and <strong>provide time advantages to international transport companies.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to experts, this step will create a <strong>seamless corridor</strong> between <strong>Turkey, Azerbaijan, and Central Asian countries.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Strategic Importance of the Middle Corridor Rising</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">In recent years, the Middle Corridor has gained strategic importance in Europe-Asia transport due to the <strong>Russia–Ukraine war and increased traffic through Caspian crossings.</strong></p>
<p><br>
The BTC line stands out as the <strong>safest and shortest connection</strong> of this corridor.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Georgia's infrastructure investments strengthen <strong>integration with Azerbaijan and Turkey</strong> while supporting the continuity of <strong>multimodal transportation extending from China to Europe.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">These developments are also seen as part of <strong>Tbilisi's goal to become a regional logistics hub.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Regional Impact and Future Outlook</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The Akhalkalaki terminal is expected to be completed by the end of 2026.</p>
<p><br>
When the terminal becomes operational, <strong>annual cargo volume transported on the BTC line is projected to double.</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Furthermore, Georgia plans to deploy <strong>green logistics</strong> practices following this investment.</p>
<p><br>
The digital nature of the new system will provide advantages such as <strong>reducing carbon footprint,</strong> <strong>fuel efficiency,</strong> and <strong>optimized train scheduling.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In conclusion, Georgia's initiative aims to transform the <strong>BTC line from merely a transit route into</strong> the <strong>digital and sustainable core of the Middle Corridor.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Georgian Railways</strong> is planning to construct a new terminal in <strong>Akhalkalaki</strong> in 2026.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The terminal will provide additional capacity to the existing BTC infrastructure.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Electronic customs clearance system</strong> will be deployed to <strong>reduce transit times.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The investment aims to accelerate transport along the <strong>Middle Corridor.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The new system will support <strong>digitalization by reducing bureaucratic procedures.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>The BTC line's logistics capacity will double,</strong> positioning Georgia as a regional trade hub.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The project is being implemented in integration with <strong>green logistics and digital transformation objectives.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
