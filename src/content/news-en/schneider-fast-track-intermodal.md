<p style="text-align: justify">Schneider National has officially launched <strong>Fast Track</strong>, a new premium service redefining speed and reliability standards in intermodal transportation. The goal is to deliver intermodal shipping at near-truck speeds and precision, providing shippers of time-sensitive freight with <strong>seamless transportation experience</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to the company's announcement, Fast Track integrates <strong>Schneider's own truck and intermodal assets</strong> with <strong>railroad partnerships</strong> to create <strong>the market's fastest and most stable intermodal lanes</strong> between the US and Mexico.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Schneider Transportation & Logistics Vice President <strong>Jim Filter</strong> summarizes the service's core objective:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Reliability is the foundation of our operations. Fast Track delivers unique speed and consistency backed by our professional drivers and company assets."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>US–Mexico Routes: Up to 2 Days Faster Delivery</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The Fast Track system is currently deployed on automotive freight, achieving <strong>2-day faster transit times than competitors</strong> for shipments from Mexico to Kansas City and Chicago.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to Schneider's information:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Core objective: <strong>just-in-time</strong> and <strong>expedited</strong> freight, reducing delay risk to zero,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Maintain <strong>95%+ on-time</strong> performance on critical lanes,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Deliver both speed and cost advantages simultaneously.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Behind this performance are:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Priority rail placement,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Optimization-focused planning,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Expedited recovery solutions.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Security: 99.99% Theft-Free Operations</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Schneider reported achieving a <strong>99.99% theft-free shipment rate</strong> across its US and Mexico operations throughout 2024. This rate provides a competitive advantage in intermodal transportation, particularly given Mexico-related theft and smuggling risks in recent years.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Why Fast Track? Who Does It Target?</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Fast Track is specifically tailored for:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Automotive OEMs</strong> (for just-in-time production lines),</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>High-value freight</strong> shippers,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Companies implementing supply chain inventory reduction strategies</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Operations requiring expedited – guaranteed delivery</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Schneider Intermodal SVP <strong>Michael Baumgardt</strong> describes the service design:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"We designed this service for shippers with zero tolerance for delays. Every mile delivers speed, control, and peace of mind."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Expansion Underway</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Fast Track is currently operational on US-Mexico corridors, but Schneider says growing demand will extend the service to other US domestic corridors. While specific new lanes have not been announced, the company plans to:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">expand cross-border OTR + rail combinations,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">establish new premium routes connecting major metro areas.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Industry Impact: Intermodal Gaining Momentum</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Rising fuel costs, driver shortages, and increasing expenses in long-haul trucking are making intermodal transportation increasingly attractive. However, lack of speed and predictability had been intermodal's weakest point for many shippers.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Fast Track closes this gap by delivering:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Speed + cost balance</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Rail reliability</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Truck-like SLAs</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Proactive tracking and security</strong>,<br>setting a new industry standard.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Fast Track delivers <strong>2-day faster</strong> intermodal transit versus competitors.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Achieves <strong>95%+ on-time delivery</strong> on US-Mexico routes.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Schneider's 2024 theft rate: <strong>99.99% theft-free</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Premium service targets time-sensitive sectors including automotive.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Expansion to new corridors planned.</p></li>
</ul>
