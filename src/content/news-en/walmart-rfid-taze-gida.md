<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Walmart</strong> is implementing <strong>RFID (Radio Frequency Identification)</strong> technology in its fresh food supply chain to increase efficiency and reduce food waste. The company announced a strategic partnership with <strong>technology provider Avery Dennison</strong> to support the initiative.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new system will use <strong>RFID labels</strong>, particularly on <strong>meat, bakery, and deli products</strong>, to enable <strong>freshness tracking</strong> through <strong>digital expiration dates</strong> on products.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Digital freshness tracking begins</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to Walmart's <strong>announcement published on October 22, 2025</strong>, the RFID labels will <strong>digitally track products' shelf life and freshness</strong>. This will enable store employees to manage <strong>product rotation</strong> (stock replenishment and removal of older products) more efficiently.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The system will also accelerate price reduction decisions for <strong>products approaching their expiration dates</strong>. This will both <strong>offer customers attractive pricing</strong> and <strong>reduce the amount of unsold food.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Christyn Keef, Senior Vice President of Front-End Transformation</strong> at Walmart, stated:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"We believe technology should make things easier for both our associates and our customers. By reducing manual workload, we're creating more time for our associates to focus on serving our customers."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Goal: Cut food waste in half</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The RFID project is being carried out as part of Walmart's <strong>target to reduce operational food waste by 50% by 2030</strong>. The company is integrating <strong>inventory management, data analytics, and supply chain digitalization</strong> to achieve this goal.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Walmart has made significant progress in its current efforts to reduce food waste:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>As of 2022</strong>, <strong>78% of unsold products, packaging, and materials</strong> were redirected to <strong>food banks</strong> and <strong>local relief organizations</strong> rather than being sent to landfills or incineration facilities.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The company developed <strong>specialized digital tools to measure and report food waste</strong> at every stage of the supply chain.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, by <strong>investing in reprocessing technologies</strong>, the company is <strong>redirecting unsold food for reuse to reach those in need.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Advantages of RFID technology</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">RFID labels enable <strong>automatic product identification without requiring visual scanning</strong>. By using this technology, Walmart will:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Achieve real-time inventory visibility</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Reduce waste on short shelf-life products</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Eliminate manual inventory tracking</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Improve inventory accuracy to prevent stockouts or overstock situations.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, the system will <strong>enhance traceability across the supply chain</strong>, enabling Walmart to digitally oversee <strong>freshness, quality, and food safety</strong> processes from suppliers to stores.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Transition to sustainable retail</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Walmart views <strong>RFID use not only as operational efficiency but also as part of its sustainability goals</strong>. The company highlights the potential of <strong>digitalization to reduce energy consumption in supply chains, prevent waste, and optimize resource use.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to experts, this move will accelerate the <strong>"smart food management" trend in the retail sector</strong>. Thanks to RFID technology, <strong>the journey of food from farm to table</strong> will become more transparent.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Walmart's new project is regarded as <strong>a pioneering step in the industry</strong>, given that <strong>14% of food loss occurs at the retail stage.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Walmart plans to expand RFID use in the coming years to include <strong>cold chain logistics, supplier shipments, and e-commerce distribution centers.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Walmart</strong> is partnering with <strong>Avery Dennison</strong> to begin using <strong>RFID labels</strong> on fresh food products.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The system will provide <strong>digital expiration date tracking</strong> in <strong>meat, bakery, and deli</strong> sections.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Objective: <strong>Reduce operational food waste by 50% by 2030.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>In 2022</strong>, <strong>78% of unsold products</strong> were redirected to relief organizations instead of landfills.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>RFID technology</strong> will increase inventory visibility, <strong>reduce manual controls</strong>, and <strong>accelerate product rotation.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The project serves as an example of <strong>sustainable food management and digital supply chain optimization in retail.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
