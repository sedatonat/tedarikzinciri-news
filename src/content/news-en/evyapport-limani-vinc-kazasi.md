<p>A major accident occurred at <a href="https://www.marinetraffic.com/en/ais/details/ports/17802?name=EVYAP&country=Turkey"><u>Evyapport</u></a> in the Körfez district of Kocaeli. A moving vessel lost control and struck massive cranes positioned along the shoreline. The impact triggered a domino effect, causing the cranes to collapse one after another. The incident was captured by security cameras at the port, revealing harrowing moments.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Emergency services, police, and firefighters were immediately dispatched to the area following the crash. Teams arriving rapidly at the scene began work to check for possible injuries and implement necessary safety measures. Fortunately, no workers or crane operators were present in the vicinity at the time of the incident, preventing what could have been a much greater tragedy. Workers who witnessed the accident recorded their moments of fear and panic on their mobile phone cameras.</p>
