<p style="text-align: justify">The Bangladesh government has formally approved the <strong>Laldia Container Terminal project</strong>, which is of critical importance for <strong>Chattogram</strong>, the country's largest port. The project, valued at over <strong>550 million dollars</strong>, stands out as one of Bangladesh's largest Public-Private Partnership (<strong>PPP</strong>) investments to date.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The agreement was signed between <strong>Chittagong Port Authority</strong>, <strong>APM Terminals (APMT)</strong>, and local partner <strong>QNS Container Services Ltd.</strong> The <strong>30-year operating concession</strong> granted to APMT is renewable subject to performance criteria. The financing, design, construction, and operation of the terminal will be undertaken entirely by investors; this model represents a first for Bangladesh port infrastructure.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A strategic project under the Denmark-Bangladesh "Sustainable Green Framework"</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The Laldia Terminal is part of the <strong>Sustainable Green Framework Engagement</strong> program being carried out between Denmark and Bangladesh. The program aims to promote <strong>green investment projects</strong> that will contribute to Bangladesh's long-term economic growth.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Danish representatives emphasized that an investment of this scale is an indicator of international commercial confidence in Bangladesh.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Terminal to become operational in 2030, increasing capacity by 800,000 TEU</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to Chittagong Port Authority, the Laldia Terminal will:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Increase the country's container handling capacity by <strong>over 800,000 TEU annually</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Be capable of accommodating <strong>vessels up to 6,000 TEU</strong> due to coastal depth (current limit: 2,800 TEU),</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Thus both <strong>reducing congestion</strong> and <strong>lowering logistics costs</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Bangladesh derives a large portion of its exports from ready-made garments, textiles, and light manufacturing sectors. The terminal will directly increase the speed and reliability with which these sectors connect to global markets.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Local authorities characterize the project as a strategic initiative under the banners of:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>transparency</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>capacity expansion</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>competitive port performance</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Green port features: One of South Asia's lowest-emission facilities</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new Laldia Terminal will be among the first examples of an <strong>energy-efficient, low-emission modern terminal</strong> in South Asia. Planned sustainability practices include:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Electric terminal equipment</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Large-scale solar energy installations</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Shore-power-ready</strong> infrastructure,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Hybrid RTG cranes</strong> (the first four units delivered by GENMA),</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Designs that provide low noise and air pollution.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">These practices both <strong>reduce GHG emissions</strong> and create a healthier environment for communities living around the terminal.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A strong lever for employment and the local economy</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The project will:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Create <strong>more than 1,000</strong> jobs during construction,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Create <strong>more than 500</strong> jobs during operations.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, hundreds of additional jobs are expected to be created throughout the supply chain. Training and apprenticeship programs are planned for the local workforce as part of the terminal; this will also strengthen Bangladesh's skill base in port operations and logistics.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Bangladesh has approved the <strong>550 million dollar Laldia Terminal</strong> concession agreement.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">APMT has been granted a <strong>30-year operating right</strong>; renewable based on performance.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The terminal will increase Bangladesh's capacity by <strong>800,000 TEU+</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Scheduled to become operational in 2030; vessels of <strong>6,000 TEU</strong> will be able to dock at the port.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The project is part of the <strong>green investment framework</strong> being carried out with Denmark.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">With electric equipment, solar energy, and shore power infrastructure, it will be a <strong>low-emission</strong> facility.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">1,000+ jobs will be created in construction; 500+ in operations.</p></li>
</ul>
