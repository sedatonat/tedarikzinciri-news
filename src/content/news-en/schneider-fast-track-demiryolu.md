<h5 style="text-align: justify">Schneider National has announced <strong>Fast Track</strong>, a new premium rail service, as demand for speed and reliability in intermodal transportation continues to surge. The company says Fast Track delivers an experience approaching <strong>truck-speed</strong> while preserving the advantages of intermodal structure.</h5>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to the company's announcement, Fast Track combines Schneider's <strong>owned truck transportation capacity</strong>, <strong>intermodal expertise</strong>, and <strong>strategic rail partnerships</strong> on a single platform, providing some of the fastest and most reliable intermodal lanes in the industry.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Michael Baumgardt</strong>, Schneider Intermodal SVP, noted that the service was designed specifically for customers where delays on critical shipments are unacceptable:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"We designed this specifically for shippers who need a solution that delivers speed, control, and reliability on every mile."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Up to Two Days Faster Transit Times on US-Mexico Lanes</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Schneider reports that on intermodal lanes running specifically between the US and Mexico, Fast Track provides delivery <strong>one to two days</strong> faster than competitors, and in some cases <strong>up to three days</strong> faster.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This performance aligns with Schneider's plan to expand strategically important Mexico intermodal operations. The company had set a target in 2021 to <strong>double Mexico intermodal transportation by 2030</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In pursuit of this goal, significant growth has been seen in volumes originating from Mexico:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Q3 intermodal revenue of <strong>$281.4 million, up 6%</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Intermodal volumes <strong>increased 10%</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Mexico-origin lanes continue to be preferred by customers due to <strong>significant speed advantages</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">CEO <strong>Mark Rourke</strong> noted that Mexico-to-US shipments are 1–3 days faster than competitors, and this advantage is driving improved customer satisfaction.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>95% On-Time Delivery and Priority Access</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Fast Track commits to <strong>95% on-time delivery</strong>.<br>
The key elements supporting this include:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Priority rail placement:</strong> Dedicated slots on trains,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Priority planning</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Optimized drayage</strong> processes,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Expedited recovery</strong> capability (rapid resolution in case of damage or delay risks).</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This structure reduces risks particularly for customers shipping <strong>time-sensitive freight</strong>. According to Schneider, on shipments from Mexico to Kansas City, Chicago, and other Midwest centers, this structure already delivers "proven performance."</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Security: Nearly 100% Theft-Free Operations in 2024</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Schneider reports achieving <strong>nearly 100% theft-free</strong> security performance in its US and Mexico operations in 2024.</p>
<p><br>
This creates a strong value proposition for Fast Track, particularly for manufacturers with high security expectations on Mexico-origin shipments.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>24/7 Tracking and Nationwide Flexibility</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Service features include:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>24/7 real-time tracking</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Flexible national network</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Continuous monitoring for sensitive loads,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Hybrid structure combining intermodal and truckload advantages.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Fast Track is said to address growing <strong>just-in-time</strong> and <strong>expedited</strong> demand trends in the industry. Schneider plans to expand its service network, though details on new lanes have not yet been disclosed.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Schneider launched <strong>Fast Track</strong>, a premium rail service.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Offers transit times <strong>up to 2 days faster</strong> on US-Mexico lanes.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>95% on-time delivery</strong>, priority rail access, optimized drayage.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Nearly 100% theft-free</strong> operations performance in 2024.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Q3 intermodal revenue of <strong>$281.4 million</strong>, volumes <strong>up 10%</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Company targets <strong>doubling Mexico intermodal business by 2030</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Fast Track was developed for just-in-time and expedited shipments.</p></li>
</ul>
