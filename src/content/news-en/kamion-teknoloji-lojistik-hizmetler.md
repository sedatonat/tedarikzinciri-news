<p>Founded in 2020, Kamion entered the sector as a digital logistics platform. The platform aims to enhance efficiency in logistics processes by leveraging its technological infrastructure to ensure trucks and loads meet at the right time and place. Kamion works with approximately 200 major corporate firms including Vestel and PepsiCo, managing their road transport operations. The platform manages some 16,000 trucks and has recorded substantial annual revenue growth. As of 2023, Kamion achieved revenues of 10 million dollars and provides logistics services both domestically and internationally.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Kamion's technology plays an important role in reducing costs in logistics processes. Berkay Adlim, the company's founder, has provided insight into the complexity of logistics teams and the costs that must be managed, noting that Kamion's technology can reduce these costs by half. Adlim has also highlighted the negative impact of empty truck runs in Turkey on both the economy and environment, explaining how Kamion is addressing this problem.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Kamion is also planning to offer services for measuring and reducing carbon emissions, is preparing to provide supply services to truck owners, and is taking steps toward globalization.
