<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>CMA CGM Group</strong> has once again earned international recognition for its sustainability performance.</p>
<p><br>
The company scored <strong>80/100</strong> in the 2025 assessment conducted by <strong>EcoVadis</strong>, one of the world's leading sustainability rating organizations, successfully placing itself in the <strong>top 3% among 85,000 companies evaluated globally</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
This score represents <strong>a 5-point increase</strong> compared to the company's 2024 score and reinforces the strength of <strong>CMA CGM's Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)</strong> strategy.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Scope of Assessment and Priority Areas</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The EcoVadis assessment measures companies' sustainability performance across <strong>four main areas</strong>:</p>
<ol>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Environmental Protection</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Human Rights & Working Conditions</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Responsible Purchasing</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Ethics & Compliance</strong></p></li>
</ol>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>CMA CGM</strong> achieved significant progress across all these areas, demonstrating strong performance particularly in <strong>commitment to ethical standards</strong> and <strong>responsible purchasing practices</strong>.<br>
This achievement demonstrates that the group has made systematic progress in both environmental and social impact targets.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>CMA CGM's Approach: Measurable Progress and Transparency</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>François Comby, Senior CSR Manager at CMA CGM</strong>, made the following statement following the assessment results:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"EcoVadis is playing an increasingly central role for our customers. Sustainability ratings have become one of the determining criteria in tenders, in particular."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">Comby emphasized that the <strong>80/100 score objectively proves the strength of the company's sustainability approach</strong>, stating:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"This score is not only an indicator of our progress, but also a concrete expression of our commitment to transparency. These elements, which are the two fundamental pillars of reliable business relationships, are at the center of our commitments to our customers."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>EcoVadis Assessment in Global Context</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>EcoVadis</strong> evaluates companies operating in more than 200 sectors across more than 160 countries based on environmental, social and ethical criteria.</p>
<p><br>
As of 2025, <strong>more than 85,000 companies</strong> have been subjected to assessment, and <strong>CMA CGM's placement in the top 3%</strong> demonstrates that the group's <strong>sustainability management systems comply with international standards</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This achievement demonstrates that CMA CGM pursues a sustainability vision aligned with its objectives in both <strong>maritime shipping and logistics services</strong>, including reducing carbon emissions, developing circular economy practices, and <strong>increasing green energy investments</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Leadership Strategy in Sustainability</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM has taken comprehensive steps in recent years to reduce its environmental impact:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Alternative fuel investments:</strong> Reducing carbon emissions by increasing the use of LNG (liquefied natural gas) and biofuels.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Green fleet transformation:</strong> Reducing CO₂ emissions with next-generation energy-efficient vessels.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Responsible supply chain:</strong> Making environmental and social criteria mandatory in supplier selection.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Human-centered management:</strong> Raising standards for occupational health, safety, and employee welfare across global operations.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">These initiatives were among the main factors that strengthened <strong>CMA CGM's leading position in environmental and social responsibility</strong> in the EcoVadis assessment.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Conclusion: CMA CGM Sets an Example in Sustainability on a Global Scale</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM's 2025 EcoVadis success demonstrates that the group has become a reference point not only in maritime shipping, but also in <strong>global sustainability management</strong>.<br>
This achievement confirms the company's consistent progress toward <strong>transparency, ethical management, and long-term environmental goals</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">With this assessment, CMA CGM is solidifying its position not only <strong>in the logistics sector</strong>, but also among <strong>global leaders in sustainable business practices</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Highlights:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>CMA CGM Group</strong> scored <strong>80/100</strong> in the 2025 EcoVadis assessment, placing itself in the <strong>global top 3%</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>5-point increase</strong> compared to 2024 demonstrates <strong>continued progress in the company's CSR strategy</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The EcoVadis assessment focuses on four main areas: <strong>Environment, Human Rights, Responsible Purchasing, and Ethics Management.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>François Comby</strong> noted that the score is a concrete indicator of the company's <strong>transparency and trust-based sustainability approach</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">CMA CGM stands out with <strong>LNG-powered vessels</strong>, <strong>biofuel use</strong>, <strong>green fleet investments</strong>, and <strong>ethical supply chain policies</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">This achievement positions CMA CGM as <strong>one of the global leaders in sustainable maritime shipping</strong>.</p></li>
