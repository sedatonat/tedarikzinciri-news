<p style="text-align: justify">A major accident occurred at UPS's primary air hub in Louisville, Kentucky. An <strong>MD-11 cargo aircraft</strong> that crashed near Louisville Muhammad Ali International Airport on the <strong>evening of November 4</strong> has claimed the lives of <strong>at least 9 people</strong>. The death toll is expected to rise.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">According to UPS, the aircraft carried <strong>three crew members</strong> and was headed to <strong>Honolulu</strong>. However, the impact of the crash was not limited to the crew: at least six additional people in the surrounding area perished as a result of the collision.</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Kentucky Governor Andy Beshear</strong> announced on X that at least nine people had been killed and noted that "there may be additional casualties."</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">UPS issued a statement following the tragedy:</p>\n<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Our hearts are with everyone affected by this tragedy. Louisville is our air hub and home to thousands of UPS employees."</blockquote>\n<blockquote><br></blockquote>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Worldport fully halted — package delivery delays expected</strong></h5>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Following the crash, UPS immediately <strong>halted Worldport operations</strong>.</p>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>All sort operations were suspended Tuesday night</strong>,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">In an update Wednesday morning, <strong>Second Day Air sort was also cancelled</strong>.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Worldport has the capacity to handle:</p>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>300 flights</strong> per day</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>400,000 packages</strong> per hour,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">It is the <strong>backbone</strong> of UPS's global air operations</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p style="text-align: justify">For this reason, the impact of the crash is not limited to Kentucky; <strong>delays in domestic and international shipments across the United States</strong> are expected.</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">In its notice to customers, UPS stated:</p>\n<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Delivery times may be affected. Our contingency plans are active to move shipments to their destinations as quickly as possible."</blockquote>\n<p style="text-align: justify">However, details of these plans were not disclosed.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>What's happening? How will the impact be felt?</strong></h5>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The crash could create <strong>new supply chain pressures</strong> particularly for e-commerce companies, manufacturers, and the logistics sector as they prepare for the holiday season.<br>\nEven a temporary shutdown of Worldport:</p>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Affects overnight operations,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Disrupts morning flight connections,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Impacts priority package workflow at the transfer hub<br>\ndirectly.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p style="text-align: justify">In such emergencies, UPS typically:</p>\n<ul>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Activates other regional air hubs,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Uses additional ground transportation to support mainline,</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Routes some high-priority loads to competing or leased carriers.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p style="text-align: justify">However, given the magnitude of the incident, recovery could take several days.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>MD-11 safety history</strong></h5>\n<p style="text-align: justify">The MD-11 model is known for <strong>structural sensitivity, particularly during takeoff and landing phases</strong>.<br>\nWhile the cause of this crash has not yet been confirmed, the fact that the type has been involved in numerous serious accidents in the past warrants attention.<br>\nNTSB's investigation is expected to take weeks.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5 shipments may largely continue normally.</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Delays are possible for expedited services (Next Day Air, 2nd Day Air).</p></li>\n  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">E-commerce companies are advised to communicate delivery timeframes to customers <strong>clearly and
