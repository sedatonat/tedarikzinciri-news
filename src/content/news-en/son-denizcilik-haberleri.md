<p style="text-align: justify">The maritime sector has experienced several significant developments in recent times. According to reports from <strong>gCaptain</strong>, the <strong>Guyana</strong> government is accelerating debris removal operations in its waterways to enhance the safety of oil supply vessels. This measure aligns with the country's growth objectives in the energy sector.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Meanwhile, <strong>Russia</strong> declared a state of emergency in the <strong>Crimea</strong> region following an oil spill in the <strong>Black Sea</strong>. Authorities are making intensive efforts to minimize the environmental impact of the spill.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, <strong>Finland</strong> announced that it has begun port state control inspections of the tanker <strong>Eagle S</strong>. The vessel in question is accused of damaging a submarine power cable and four telecommunications cables. This incident has heightened maritime security concerns in the region.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The <strong>Mexico</strong> government announced that it has postponed for six months the tax planned to be applied to cruise passengers in its ports and exempted wheat from tariffs. This decision aims to support the country's tourism and agriculture sectors.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Finally, <strong>U.S. President Joe Biden</strong> decided to ban new oil and gas development activities in an area of approximately 625 million acres along the <strong>Atlantic</strong> and <strong>Pacific</strong> coasts. This action is regarded as part of environmental protection efforts.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Guyana is clearing debris in its waterways to enhance the safety of oil supply vessels.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Russia declared a state of emergency in Crimea due to an oil spill in the Black Sea.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Finland launched inspections of the Eagle S tanker on suspicion of damaging submarine cables.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Mexico postponed the cruise passenger tax and exempted wheat from tariffs.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">U.S. President Joe Biden banned new oil and gas development activities on the Atlantic and Pacific coasts.</p></li>
</ul>
