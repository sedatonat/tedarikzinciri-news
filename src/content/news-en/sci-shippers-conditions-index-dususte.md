<p style="text-align: justify"><a href="https://www.ftrintel.com/"><u><strong>FTR Transportation Intelligence</strong></u></a>'s <a href="https://www.ftrintel.com/shippers-conditions-index"><u><strong>Shippers Conditions Index (SCI)</strong></u></a> has turned negative for the second time since August 2023, registering at -0.9 in November 2024. This development indicates that shippers are facing increased difficulties and that market conditions have become less favorable for them.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The <a href="https://www.ftrintel.com/shippers-conditions-index"><u><strong>Shippers Conditions Index (SCI)</strong></u></a> is a gauge that evaluates market conditions by taking into account freight costs, capacity, fuel prices, and other relevant factors facing shippers. Positive values point to more favorable conditions for shippers, while negative values indicate more challenging conditions.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>The index stood at 2.9 in August 2023, then recorded 0.5 and 0.3 in September and October respectively. Values of 4.5 in May 2023 and 3.0 in June 2023 demonstrated that the index was more positive during that period.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The index's shift into negative territory underscores rising costs and operational challenges facing shippers. This condition stems from factors such as increased freight costs, capacity constraints, and fluctuations in fuel prices. Shippers are reassessing their supply chain strategies to address these challenges and exploring new approaches to optimize costs.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FTR Transportation Intelligence</strong> analysts note that market conditions are expected to remain volatile in the coming months and that shippers will need to adapt to this environment. They recommend adopting more proactive approaches, particularly in capacity management and cost control.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Shippers Conditions Index (SCI)</strong> registered at -0.9 in November 2024.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The index's negative reading reflects increasing difficulties faced by shippers.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Freight costs, capacity constraints, and fuel price fluctuations rank among the key factors contributing to this situation.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Shippers are reassessing their supply chain strategies and seeking new methods to optimize costs.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FTR Transportation Intelligence</strong> analysts indicate that market conditions will continue to fluctuate and that shippers must adapt accordingly.</p></li>
</ul>
