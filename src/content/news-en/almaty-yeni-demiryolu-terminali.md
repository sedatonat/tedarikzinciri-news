<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Kedentransservis</strong>, Kazakhstan's largest logistics terminal operator, is planning to build a new rail freight terminal near Almaty in partnership with Chinese firm <strong>China Xinjiang Commercial Logistics</strong>. The new terminal will be designed with the capacity to handle various types of cargo.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The partnership between <strong>Kedentransservis</strong> and <strong>China Xinjiang Commercial Logistics</strong> aims to strengthen commercial relations between Kazakhstan and China and develop logistics infrastructure in the region. Almaty is positioned as Central Asia's largest passenger and cargo hub. Consequently, the construction of the new terminal is seen as an important step in increasing trade volumes in the region and optimizing logistics processes.​<a href="https://tavhavalimanlari.com.tr/tav-almatida-yeni-terminali-acti" rel="noopener" target="_blank">tavhavalimanlari.com.tr</a></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The construction of the new terminal is being evaluated as a project within the scope of Kazakhstan's "Nurly Zhol" economic policy and China's "Belt and Road Initiative" (BRI). These projects aim to increase infrastructure investments in the region, develop trade corridors, and support economic growth.​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new rail freight terminal to be built in Almaty will be equipped with modern technologies and constructed in accordance with international standards. The terminal is planned to have the capacity to handle various types of cargo, including container transport, bulk cargo, and hazardous materials. Additionally, the terminal will provide customs clearance, storage, and distribution services.​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">With the completion of the project, a significant increase in Almaty's logistics capacity and growth in trade volumes in the region are expected. This supports Kazakhstan's goal of becoming a logistics hub in Central Asia and contributes to the country's economic development.</p>
