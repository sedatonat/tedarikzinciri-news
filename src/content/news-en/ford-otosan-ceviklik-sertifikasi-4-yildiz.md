<p>Ford Otosan has achieved significant success in agile management systems. It became the first Turkish automotive manufacturing company to receive the "4-Star Organization" certification from the <a href="https://businessagility.institute/"><u>Business Agility Institute</u></a>, which evaluates the agility levels of companies on a global scale.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>This success emerged as a result of agile transformation efforts launched in 2019 and encompassing the entire organizational structure. Ford Otosan's agility level placed it in the top 11 percent among 1,800 companies evaluated on a global scale, making it the highest-scoring automotive manufacturer in the region.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>The company focused on four main areas in its agile transformation journey: organizational and cultural transformation, change management, and the transformation of business practices. Throughout this process, agile working methods were fully implemented across the entire value chain.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Ford Otosan's Head of Human Resources and Transformation, <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/başak-çalıkoğlu-akyol-95982a1/"><u>Başak Akyol</u></a>, noted that agile transformation not only increased operational efficiency but also delivered significant improvements in customer satisfaction and process improvements.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Highlights</h5>\n<ul>\n  <li><p>Ford Otosan became the first Turkish automotive manufacturing company to receive the "4-Star Organization" certification from the <a href="https://businessagility.institute/"><u>Business Agility Institute</u></a>.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Agile transformation efforts began in 2019 and encompass the transformation of the entire organizational structure.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Ford Otosan ranked in the top 11 percent among 1,800 companies evaluated on a global scale.</p></li>\n  <li><p>In agile transformation, organizational and cultural transformation, change management, and the transformation of business practices are key focus areas.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Agile working methods have been implemented at 100 percent across the company's entire value chain.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Agile transformation has led to improvements in operational efficiency as well as customer satisfaction and process improvements.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n
