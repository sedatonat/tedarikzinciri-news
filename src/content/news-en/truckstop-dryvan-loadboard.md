<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Truckstop.com</strong> announced the launch of a <strong>new low-cost load platform (Van Load Board)</strong> designed for small-scale carriers and independent drivers. Developed exclusively for <strong>dry van</strong> equipment, this system provides users with access to <strong>verified load data</strong> and <strong>real-time rate data</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Available at an introductory price of just <strong>$35 per month</strong>, the platform offers <strong>unlimited load searches</strong>. The new offering aims to enhance the competitive position of <strong>small fleets</strong> and <strong>independent carriers</strong> in the marketplace.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Scott Moscrip, CEO of Truckstop.com</strong>, addressed industry challenges with the following statement:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Small fleets and independent operators are the backbone of the trucking industry. However, this year's <strong>freight recession</strong> has been particularly challenging for dry van carriers. Van freight rates have remained lower compared to other equipment types. For this reason, we developed a Van Load Board that delivers <strong>verified loads and real-time market data</strong> at a low cost."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Real-Time Price Data and Advanced Visualization</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new <strong>Dry Van Load Board</strong> comes with <strong>FreightWaves SONAR Insights</strong> integration. This enables users to access <strong>live rate data</strong>, <strong>route-based comparisons</strong>, and <strong>estimated freight rates</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Tools available on the platform include:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Load comparison</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>RPM heat maps</strong> (revenue per mile maps),</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Truck count by state</strong> and other advanced analytics screens.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This integration provides <strong>small carriers with data visibility equivalent to major logistics firms</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Julie Van de Kamp</strong>, Director of Marketing and Operations at <strong>FreightWaves SONAR</strong>, explained the system's importance:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Freight rates change constantly, and small carriers need access to <strong>accurate, real-time data</strong> to remain profitable. Through <strong>SONAR rate intelligence</strong> integration, Truckstop users now have all the <strong>market visibility that large carriers possess</strong>."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A Major Advantage for Small Carriers</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Truckstop Basic Plan</strong> provides users with access to <strong>private loads</strong> and <strong>fast payment with factoring details</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
The <strong>Pro version</strong> includes <strong>SONAR rate tools</strong>, <strong>multi-trip planning</strong>, and advanced <strong>load management tools</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Both plans are designed to <strong>simplify freight matching</strong> and <strong>protect small fleet profitability</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">With this new model, <strong>Truckstop</strong> is responding to changing industry dynamics. Throughout 2025, small carriers' revenues have been pressured by low freight rates, volatile demand, and rising operating costs. The new Van Load Board gives these operators a competitive advantage by offering <strong>powerful analytics tools at a lower entry cost</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A New Step in Industry Transformation</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Data-driven decision making</strong> and <strong>real-time market information-based rate negotiation</strong> are becoming increasingly important in trucking. Truckstop.com's new platform is at the center of this transformation.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The system operates with an <strong>asset-light</strong> approach, <strong>reducing load search time for users</strong>, <strong>minimizing empty miles</strong>, and <strong>improving route efficiency</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This innovation is also seen as a step that <strong>accelerates the digitalization process for small-scale trucking firms</strong>. Truckstop's goal is to achieve <strong>competitive parity among all dry van carriers</strong> through this data-powered platform.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Truckstop.com</strong> unveiled a <strong>new low-cost load platform</strong> tailored for dry van carriers.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Monthly fee of $35</strong> with <strong>unlimited load searches</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>FreightWaves SONAR Insights</strong> integration provides <strong>live rate data</strong> and <strong>market forecasts</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The platform features <strong>load comparison</strong>, <strong>heat maps</strong>, and <strong>route planning</strong> tools.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Basic</strong> and <strong>Pro plans</strong> support small fleets with <strong>faster payment</strong> and <strong>data-driven decision making</strong> processes.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Objective: <strong>Provide small carriers with a low-cost, high-transparency competitive environment</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
