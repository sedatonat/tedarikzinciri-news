<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>HMM (Hyundai Merchant Marine)</strong> has reached <strong>a major milestone in global container shipping history.</strong> The company has achieved <strong>a fleet capacity of 1,007,180 TEU</strong> according to <strong>Alphaliner</strong> data, making it the <strong>eighth shipping company</strong> to join the exclusive group known as the <strong>"TEU Millionaires Club."</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This achievement reflects <strong>impressive growth demonstrated by South Korea's national carrier HMM</strong> over the past five years. According to <strong>Alphaliner's weekly report,</strong> HMM's operating capacity <strong>has doubled since 2019.</strong> The analysis highlights that <strong>the 2020s have been the most profitable period in container shipping history to date,</strong> and HMM has benefited substantially from this trend.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Fleet size and new ship orders</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">HMM's fleet has now officially <strong>surpassed the 1 million TEU threshold,</strong> formally joining the "mega carriers" league in the shipping industry. The company is expected to maintain this growth momentum as <strong>numerous new vessels under construction at South Korean shipyards</strong> will be delivered from 2026 onward.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to the plan:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Five new vessels in the 8,500–9,000 TEU class will be delivered during 2026,</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Twelve new vessels with 13,000 TEU capacity</strong> will join the fleet before the end of the decade.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">These new builds will <strong>strengthen HMM's operational capacity</strong> and <strong>increase fleet efficiency.</strong> Additionally, the company plans to accelerate its transition to <strong>sustainable shipping by investing in next-generation container vessels equipped with carbon-neutral ship technologies and alternative fuel systems.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Strategic investment plan</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">HMM had allocated <strong>23.5 trillion South Korean won (approximately $17.5 billion)</strong> as an investment budget under its <strong>2030 strategic expansion plan announced in September 2024.</strong> In line with this plan:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>The container fleet will nearly double,</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>The tanker and dry bulk</strong> fleet will <strong>triple in size.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">The company aims to <strong>further strengthen South Korea's global position in maritime shipping by 2030</strong> and <strong>expand its market share on Asia–Europe and transpacific routes.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Global ranking position</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">HMM currently <strong>ranks eighth in Alphaliner's global carrier rankings.</strong> <strong>Taiwan-based Evergreen Marine,</strong> positioned just above HMM, is <strong>preparing to cross the 2 million TEU threshold</strong> shortly. <strong>Japan-based Ocean Network Express (ONE)</strong> recently celebrated this milestone.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">At the top of the rankings stands <strong>Mediterranean Shipping Company (MSC), led by Soren Toft.</strong> MSC is <strong>preparing to break another record by surpassing 7 million TEU capacity in the coming months.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">HMM's rise demonstrates that <strong>South Korea is regaining global strength in maritime shipping.</strong> According to experts, <strong>strategic coordination</strong> between the country's government and private sector, <strong>infrastructure investments,</strong> and <strong>cutting-edge shipyards</strong> are among the main drivers of this growth.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A new era in container shipping</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">HMM's success does not merely signify capacity expansion; it is also regarded as <strong>an important signal for the re-establishment of stability in global supply chains.</strong> <strong>Rising freight rates in the post-pandemic period,</strong> <strong>supply chain disruptions,</strong> and <strong>rising energy costs</strong> have driven maritime shipping companies toward efficiency-focused strategies.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">HMM is positioning itself as a leader in this transformation through <strong>technological modernization, digital fleet management, and sustainable fuel investments.</strong> Through these efforts, the company aims to <strong>reduce its carbon footprint while enhancing operational flexibility.</strong></p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>HMM</strong> joined the <strong>"TEU Millionaires Club"</strong> with <strong>1,007,180 TEU</strong> capacity.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The company <strong>doubled its capacity in five years.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>New builds:</strong> Five vessels of <strong>8,500–9,000 TEU</strong> in 2026, and <strong>12 vessels of 13,000 TEU</strong> by the end of the decade.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>2030 investment plan:</strong> <strong>23.5 trillion KRW ($17.5 billion).</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Target: <strong>Double container fleet capacity and triple tanker and dry bulk fleet capacity.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>HMM</strong> ranks <strong>8th</strong> in Alphaliner rankings; <strong>MSC</strong> will soon cross <strong>7 million TEU.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The growth represents <strong>South Korea's resurgence in global maritime shipping.</strong></p></li>
</ul>
