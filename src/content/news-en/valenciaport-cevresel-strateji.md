<p style="text-align: justify">Valenciaport unveiled its new <strong>environmental strategy</strong> to the public as part of the Ecofira Environmental Solutions Fair. At the center of sustainability initiatives driven by the Port Authority of Valencia (APV) are <strong>decarbonization</strong>, <strong>climate resilience</strong> and <strong>climate change mitigation</strong>. The strategy provides a framework aligned with the <strong>Net Zero Plan</strong> that shapes the port's long-term transformation roadmap.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to APV, the new plan aims to establish Valenciaport as a <strong>leader in renewable energy use</strong>, a low-emission and competitive Mediterranean gateway. The plan's main components include:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Expansion of <strong>shore-side electricity supply (OPS)</strong> infrastructure,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Testing of <strong>hydrogen</strong>, biofuels and other alternative fuels,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Creation of <strong>green maritime corridors</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Climate adaptation strategies,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Nature-based solutions and environmental compensation projects<br>
are included.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Net Zero and Renewable Energy Vision</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Under its Net Zero Plan, Valenciaport is <strong>increasing renewable energy use across all port operations</strong>. To achieve this goal:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Large-scale solar energy projects,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Wind energy integration,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Transition to electric operational equipment<br>
are being planned.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">By expanding OPS infrastructure, the port aims to eliminate fossil fuel consumption while vessels are docked. This will significantly reduce carbon emissions, particularly for container and ro-ro ships with intensive operations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Green Corridors and Hydrogen Ecosystem</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">One of the standout elements of the new strategy is the <strong>promotion of green corridors</strong>. Valenciaport aims to establish emission-reduction trade routes with Mediterranean and Northern European ports. This aligns with the Maritime Fuels Transition and the EU's FuelEU Maritime objectives.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, <strong>Hydrogen Valley</strong> initiatives in the Sagunto region involve the port's participation in pilot projects on hydrogen production, storage and utilization. These efforts will enable heavy equipment and terminal vehicles to be <strong>powered by green hydrogen</strong> in the medium and long term.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Climate Adaptation Projects</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Valenciaport's strategy is not limited to emission reduction; it also includes strengthening infrastructure to adapt to climate impacts such as <strong>flooding risk, heat waves and coastal erosion</strong>. The port's climate resilience is being enhanced through flexible sea-based design solutions and nature-based interventions.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Environmental Communications and Digitalization</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">At the Valenciaport booth at Ecofira, the port's ongoing projects were detailed:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Carbon emission reduction dashboards,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Energy efficiency programs,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Natural ecosystem restoration,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Waste management practices<br>
were detailed.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Digitalization is also a critical part of the strategy. In this context, APV signed an agreement worth <strong>€6.9 million ($7.9 million)</strong> with <strong>Orange Spain</strong> last week to deploy a dedicated <strong>5G network</strong> for the port. This network will support sustainability functions such as energy management, equipment optimization and real-time environmental monitoring.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Valenciaport's new environmental strategy places <strong>Net Zero</strong>, OPS and alternative fuels at its core.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Green corridors and <strong>Hydrogen Valley Sagunto</strong> partnerships are critical components of the strategy.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The port's goal: a low-emission, competitive and climate-resilient operating model.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">APV awarded the €6.9 million <strong>5G network</strong> tender for the port to Orange Spain.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The strategy aligns with the United Nations <strong>SDG</strong> objectives.</p></li>
</ul>
