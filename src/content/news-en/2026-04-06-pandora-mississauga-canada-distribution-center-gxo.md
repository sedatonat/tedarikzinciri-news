<p style="text-align: justify">Danish jewellery brand <strong>Pandora</strong> announced the opening of a new <strong>distribution center (DC)</strong> in <strong>Mississauga, Ontario</strong> to serve the Canadian market. <strong>GXO Logistics</strong> will operate the facility.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new center has a processing capacity of up to <strong>12,500 orders per day</strong> and is equipped with <strong>pick-to-light</strong> systems. By fulfilling Canadian orders previously shipped via the United States from this facility, Pandora aims to shorten delivery times and reduce cross-border cost items.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The investment stands out as part of the strategy to bring inventory closer to market ahead of the <strong>USMCA (United States-Mexico-Canada Agreement)</strong> review scheduled for July 2026. Pandora positions the move as a <strong>nearshoring</strong> step supporting its plan to accelerate retail growth in North America.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong><br>
1. Pandora has launched its new Canada distribution center in Mississauga.<br>
2. GXO Logistics will operate the facility.<br>
3. The site can process up to 12,500 orders per day.<br>
4. Pick-to-light systems have been installed.<br>
5. The investment timing aligns with the July 2026 USMCA review.</p>
