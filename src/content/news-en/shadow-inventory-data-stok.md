<p style="text-align: justify">Shadow inventory—stock data that exists outside of system records—is one of the primary drivers of planning failures in supply chains. According to Think Tank analysis, manual processes and siloed systems obscure true inventory levels and undermine decision-making accuracy. Gartner reports indicate that 35% of enterprises have discovered that portions of their active inventory are unrecorded.</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">This situation creates dual costs: overproduction, unnecessary procurement, and stock-outs. AI-powered data matching systems integrate inventory information from disparate sources to establish a "single source of truth." RFID, IoT sensors, and digital twin integrations make stock movements visible in real time.</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">Cloud ERP systems play a critical role in eliminating shadow inventory. Through real-time data sharing, sales, production, and procurement departments operate from the same information set. At the cultural level, however, technological solutions yield no lasting results unless data discipline and a sense of ownership are cultivated.</p>\n<p style="text-align: justify">In conclusion, eliminating shadow inventory means more than deploying technology—it means building a data culture. Transparency is the foundation of planning accuracy.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong><br>\n- Shadow inventory is a fundamental driver of planning errors.<br>\n- Gartner: 35% of enterprises have identified unrecorded portions of their inventory.<br>\n- AI-powered matching creates a "single source of truth."<br>\n- RFID and IoT enable inventory visibility.<br>\n- Without data discipline, technological solutions are not sustainable.</p>\n<p><br>\n---<br>\n<strong>News Link:</strong> <a href="https://www.supplychainbrain.com/blogs/1-think-tank/post/42320-shadow-inventory-when-supply-chain-data-lives-in-silos"><u>https://www.supplychainbrain.com/blogs/1-think-tank/post/42320-shadow-inventory-when-supply-chain-data-lives-in-silos</u></a><br>\n<strong>!!!
