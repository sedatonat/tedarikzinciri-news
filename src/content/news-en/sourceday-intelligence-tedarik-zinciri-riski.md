<p>SourceDay, a direct materials procurement platform, has unveiled SourceDay Intelligence, its new platform. Using SourceDay's patent-pending artificial intelligence and machine learning software, the new platform transforms reactive supply chain risk mitigation into proactive risk management, aiming to prevent up to 70% of internal supply issues that occur before shipment.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>SourceDay Intelligence is designed to enhance supply chain performance by identifying and predicting both known and unknown internal supply risks, recommending effective solutions, and automating low-risk purchase order (PO) changes. This enables companies to focus more resources on critical activities.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>A key differentiator of the platform is its support from SourceDay's proprietary PO lifecycle management dataset. This dataset contains over 60 billion dollars in direct spend information, 100 million PO change transactions annually, and interactions with more than 101,000 suppliers, and continues to grow. This comprehensive data enables SourceDay Intelligence to uncover risk blind spots that other applications cannot detect.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Critical risk areas in internal supply—such as supplier reliability, on-time delivery, and purchase order cost control—are highlighted and prioritized through the system's risk clustering feature, identifying issues that require the most attention.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Specific benefits offered by the platform include identifying and resolving potentially delayed POs, detecting overlooked POs, uncovering supplier-proposed PO changes, and identifying and mitigating PO delivery failures caused by email issues.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>SourceDay Intelligence equips companies with tools suited to the new era of supply chain risks. This enables companies to identify potential disruptions in their supply chains proactively, improving operational efficiency and reducing costs. The integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies revolutionizes supply chain management while helping companies strengthen their competitive advantage.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>The platform provides supply chain managers with real-time data and analytics, enabling them to make quick and informed decisions. Additionally, through automated PO changes, the need for manual intervention is reduced and risks stemming from human error are minimized. This enables processes to operate in a more fluid and reliable manner.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>With its extensive database and advanced analytical capabilities, SourceDay Intelligence stands out as an ideal solution for companies seeking to adopt a proactive approach to supply chain management. This innovative platform is viewed as a significant step toward effectively managing supply chain risks and ensuring operational continuity for businesses.</p>
