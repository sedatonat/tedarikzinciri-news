<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Greece-based ship operator OceanPal</strong> has taken a significant leap beyond maritime shipping into <strong>artificial intelligence (AI)</strong> and <strong>blockchain technologies</strong>. Through its new digital subsidiary <strong>SovereignAI</strong>, the company plans to realize a vision of <strong>universal AI sovereignty</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Nasdaq-listed OceanPal</strong> announced that it has raised <strong>$120 million through a private investment in public equity (PIPE)</strong> and will direct these funds toward the <strong>SovereignAI</strong> project. The new venture will be developed in collaboration with <strong>NEAR Foundation</strong> and built on <strong>NVIDIA-based privacy-focused artificial intelligence cloud infrastructure</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>SovereignAI: Blockchain-Based AI Infrastructure</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to OceanPal, SovereignAI will establish a <strong>"blockchain-native" infrastructure</strong> enabling AI systems to operate in a <strong>decentralized, secure, and privacy-first manner</strong>. This structure is rooted in the concept of <strong>"AI sovereignty"</strong>—meaning AI applications can function on <strong>user-owned, transparent, and secure networks</strong> without reliance on centralized clouds or singular institutions.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">As part of this initiative, OceanPal plans to acquire <strong>up to 10% of the total token supply of NEAR Protocol</strong>. This investment is part of a <strong>crypto treasury</strong> strategy that transforms the company's <strong>cryptocurrency asset management from passive positioning to active participation</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Management and Vision</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new venture will be led by <strong>Sal Ternullo</strong>, who has been appointed co-CEO of OceanPal. Ternullo is an experienced fintech executive who previously worked at <strong>State Street Bank</strong> on blockchain and digital asset infrastructure.</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"This is the first instance of a publicly traded company partnering strategically with NEAR Foundation to support a vision of <strong>universal AI sovereignty</strong>," said Ternullo, noting that SovereignAI's objective is to develop a <strong>"privacy-focused, regulation-compliant, and scalable artificial intelligence infrastructure."</strong></blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">Ternullo will be joined by <strong>David Schwed as COO</strong>. Schwed previously served as <strong>Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) at Robinhood</strong>.</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Our strategy encompasses not only digital asset management but also building <strong>user-owned, privacy-preserving AI infrastructure</strong>," said Schwed, stating that SovereignAI will deliver <strong>privacy-first solutions across finance, healthcare, and media sectors</strong>.</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Advisory Board and Investors</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The advisory board of SovereignAI will be chaired by <strong>Illia Polosukhin, CEO of NEAR Foundation</strong>. The board will also include representatives from technology leaders such as <strong>OpenAI</strong>, <strong>Fabric Ventures</strong>, and <strong>Quicknode</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The venture has received support from leading crypto investors including <strong>Kraken</strong>, <strong>Fabric Ventures</strong>, <strong>G20 Group</strong>, and <strong>Proximity</strong>. These financial and strategic partnerships will accelerate SovereignAI's goal of building a <strong>blockchain-based artificial intelligence ecosystem</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>OceanPal's Strategic Transformation</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">OceanPal operates as a subsidiary of the <strong>Diana Shipping group</strong> (led by Semiramis Paliou). While the company continues to operate <strong>dry bulk</strong> and <strong>product tanker</strong> fleets, it is structuring SovereignAI as a <strong>separate digital innovation division</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The company's statement indicated that this move signifies a <strong>"transition from passive investment-focused asset management to active digital infrastructure development."</strong> In this way, OceanPal is diversifying revenue streams beyond shipping while aiming for <strong>integration into the digital economy of the future</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Alignment with Global Trends</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to experts, this development represents a significant example of <strong>traditional maritime companies' shift toward the digital asset economy</strong>. Although the shipping sector is typically characterized by <strong>high capital intensity and low digitalization rates</strong>, firms like OceanPal are expanding their scope through <strong>investments in AI, blockchain, and data economy initiatives</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">OceanPal's SovereignAI venture also demonstrates that <strong>shipping companies could evolve into digital infrastructure providers</strong>. The company indicated its openness to potential collaborations in areas such as <strong>AI-powered autonomous systems</strong>, <strong>cybersecurity solutions</strong>, and <strong>crypto-based business models</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5><strong>Key Points:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>OceanPal</strong> established <strong>SovereignAI</strong>, a new digital subsidiary, with <strong>$120 million in PIPE investment</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>SovereignAI</strong> will develop <strong>blockchain-based artificial intelligence infrastructure</strong> using <strong>NEAR Protocol</strong> and <strong>NVIDIA technology</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The company plans to invest in <strong>up to 10% of the NEAR token supply</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The project is led by CEO <strong>Sal Ternullo</strong> and COO <strong>David Schwed</strong>, with advisory support from <strong>OpenAI</strong>, <strong>NEAR</strong>, and <strong>Fabric Ventures</strong>.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Objective: <strong>"Universal AI sovereignty"—user-owned, privacy-focused, and decentralized artificial intelligence systems.</strong></p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">OceanPal continues ship operations while developing its digital innovation division as an independent entity.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The move is viewed as a sectoral first in terms of shipping companies' integration into the crypto and artificial intelligence ecosystem.</p></li>
</ul>
