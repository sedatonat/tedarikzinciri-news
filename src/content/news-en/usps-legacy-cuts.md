<p>Recent cost-cutting measures implemented by USPS leadership have left a controversial legacy affecting service levels and labor relations. On the political front, service equity to rural communities and universal service obligation requirements remain contested issues.</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Technology investments include package sorting automation, route optimization, and strengthening digital channels in customer communications.
