<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>GenLogs</strong>, a technology company delivering innovative solutions in the U.S. trucking sector, gathers truckload transportation data through its network of roadside sensors, thereby providing transparency in freight transport. The company has recently secured a $14.6 million investment to expand its sensor network and extend operations into Mexico and Canada. ​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>GenLogs</strong> CEO Ryan Joyce states that the company currently works with more than 50 freight brokers across the United States and uses data obtained from its sensor network to help manage shipping fraud, identify sales opportunities, and assess capacity. Joyce notes that this data is also valuable for shippers and trucking companies directly, and the company aims to serve these segments as well. ​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>GenLogs</strong>' sensor network passively monitors every truck and equipment match crossing highways, thereby collecting detailed data. This information enhances operational efficiency for freight brokers and trucking companies, reduces fraud risk, and enables them to better assess market opportunities. ​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new funding secured by the company will be used to quadruple the capacity of its sensor network, thereby enabling data collection across a wider geographic area. This expansion will allow <strong>GenLogs</strong> to enter the Mexico and Canada markets and lay the groundwork for providing more comprehensive solutions to the North American transportation sector. ​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Visibility and data analytics in the freight transport sector hold critical importance for operational efficiency and security. Companies like <strong>GenLogs</strong> use innovative technologies to address these sector needs and contribute to making transportation processes more transparent and efficient. With the expansion of sensor networks, trucking companies and shippers will be able to access more accurate and real-time data, enabling them to make strategic decisions and gain competitive advantage. ​</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In conclusion, the investment secured by <strong>GenLogs</strong> represents an important step in the company's growth strategies and the valuable services it provides to the sector, once again highlighting the increasing role of technology in the transportation industry. ​</p>
