<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Leaf Home</strong> is a direct-to-consumer brand serving North America with a broad product portfolio ranging from gutter protection systems to water filtration solutions and in-home mobility products. The pandemic-driven boom in home renovation pushed the company to expand from <strong>30 to 250+ locations</strong>. However, rapid growth created a significant bottleneck in Leaf Home's <strong>logistics management, which still relied on manual processes</strong>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Because the company specializes in <strong>large, high-volume products</strong>, it manages a complex last-mile distribution model requiring significant flexibility. Products are first transported to field offices and then to customers' homes; any delay cascades throughout the entire operation.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">To address this, Leaf Home decided to migrate its entire logistics workflow to <strong>Uber Freight's modern TMS (Transportation Management System)</strong> platform to eliminate manual processes. Rather than acting as a mere software provider, Uber Freight conducted a detailed analysis of Leaf Home's operations and implemented a custom deployment using a <strong>"co-innovation"</strong> approach.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>One platform — complete visibility</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Leaf Home's previous processes were fragmented across manual operations and multiple software systems. With Uber Freight TMS:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Planning,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Load assignment,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Carrier communication,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Document management,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Pricing and capacity analysis</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">are now unified under a single digital roof.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This integration significantly enhanced the company's <strong>real-time decision-making capacity</strong>. Many previously manual operations now run on automation. Loads can now be reserved <strong>in bulk simultaneously</strong> rather than individually.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Additionally, through <strong>Uber Freight Exchange</strong>, Leaf Home gained access to a broader network of carriers for both spot and contracted freight. This resulted in:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">more competitive pricing,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">greater capacity options,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">faster load confirmation<br>
being achieved.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Data-driven supply chain</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Uber Freight provided Leaf Home with access to far more data points than before.<br>
The company can now:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">see <strong>where bottlenecks form across operations</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">identify which routes are more efficient,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">flag shipments at risk of delay<br>
from a single dashboard.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">This visibility is critical for both day-to-day operations and long-term strategic planning.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Tangible results: Cost and labor savings</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Leaf Home saw dramatic results immediately following TMS integration:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>1,000 hours of annual labor savings</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>20% cost reduction on full truckload shipments</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Improved on-time delivery rates</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Marked reduction in missed pickups and loadings</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Less waiting time and faster workflows across operations</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Michael Matisz</strong>, Leaf Home's Vice President of Supply Chain, summarizes the transformation:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Our logistics team still consists of four people — just as it did in 2018 — but today we manage a much larger network. On the day the system went live, we saved thousands of dollars."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">Matisz also notes that field teams have become more satisfied thanks to the TMS:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"They're no longer waiting for trucks. This alone made the logistics team popular within the company."</blockquote>
<blockquote><br></blockquote>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Uber Freight: The secret to success is technology plus partnership</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Steve Barber</strong>, Vice President of Product at Uber Freight, notes that Leaf Home's feedback has shaped the TMS's evolution:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Feedback is critical for us. We develop our technology together with our customers."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">Barber noted that the TMS has gained many new features over the past year and they are working on <strong>next-generation AI-powered TMS</strong>:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"Success comes not from technology alone, but from technology plus partnership."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">For Leaf Home, logistics is no longer an operational necessity but a <strong>strategic force driving business growth</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Leaf Home achieved <strong>20% cost reduction</strong> and <strong>1,000 hours of savings</strong> by transitioning from manual processes to Uber Freight TMS.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The TMS provided automation and data visibility that enabled the company to keep pace with growth.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The Exchange delivered access to a broader carrier network and improved pricing.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Uber Freight adopted a <strong>co-innovation approach</strong> with Leaf Home.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The next-generation TMS continues to be developed with emphasis on <strong>AI and automation</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
