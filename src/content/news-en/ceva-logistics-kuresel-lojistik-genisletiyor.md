<p>CEVA Logistics, a subsidiary of the CMA CGM Group, has announced that it will integrate Bolloré Logistics, which it acquired for $5.5 billion last year, into its operations.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<p>Bolloré Logistics operates in 148 countries worldwide and handles 390,000 tonnes of air cargo and 710,000 TEU of ocean freight annually. Through this integration, CEVA aims to provide its customers with seamless access to its expanded global logistics capabilities across air, ocean, land, and rail transportation.&nbsp;</p>\n<p><br></p>\n<h5>Key Points:</h5>\n<ul>\n  <li><p>CEVA Logistics is integrating Bolloré Logistics into its operations.</p></li>\n  <li><p>The integration seeks to increase global logistics capacity.</p></li>\n  <li><p>Vertical integration will accelerate the process of developing solutions to customer challenges.</p></li>\n</ul>\n<p><br></p>\n
