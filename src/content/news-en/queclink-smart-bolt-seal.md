<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Queclink Wireless Solutions</strong> has introduced a next-generation <strong>smart bolt-seal solution</strong> aimed at <strong>strengthening container security</strong> in the global supply chain.</p>
<p><br>
The new product, <strong>GES100CG</strong>, is designed for both <strong>ocean shipping</strong> and <strong>intermodal freight transport</strong> and offers full compliance with the <strong>ISO 17712 high-security standard</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Moving past traditional seals: smart, connected, and tamper-proof</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Queclink UK Sales Director Vernon Bonser</strong> noted that the solution addresses one of the most critical vulnerabilities in the maritime and land transportation sectors:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"GES100CG completely eliminates the risk of container seals being counterfeited, altered, or reinstalled. The moment a seal is breached, it sends a real-time alert and cannot be remounted."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">This feature provides supply chain companies with <strong>instant transparency and reliability</strong> in transporting <strong>high-value cargo</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Connectivity and location tracking integrated</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The new bolt-seal delivers <strong>global coverage</strong> through <strong>LTE Cat-1 bis (4G)</strong> and <strong>GSM (2G) backup connectivity technologies</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
Additionally, with support for <strong>GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System)</strong> and <strong>network-based LBS (Location-Based Services)</strong>, the device's <strong>location accuracy</strong> is protected across maritime, land, and rail transport.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This system generates an immediate alert via sensor in the event of <strong>any tampering or seal breach</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
If the device is outside coverage (for example, during open-ocean passages), alerts are <strong>stored in internal buffer memory</strong> and automatically transmitted once connectivity is restored.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Extended battery life and intelligent alert system</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Based on a <strong>twice-daily reporting frequency</strong>, the GES100CG delivers approximately <strong>90 days of uninterrupted operation</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
The device features a <strong>low battery alarm</strong> that immediately notifies users when power is declining.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This capability <strong>minimizes maintenance and inspection requirements</strong> during <strong>extended ocean voyages</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Durability and security standards</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">The smart bolt-seal is designed in compliance with <strong>ISO 17712 high-security seal certification</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
Additionally, the device features an <strong>IP67-rated enclosure</strong> that delivers <strong>resistance to dust, moisture, corrosion, and impact</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This ensures the device provides long-lasting protection in <strong>harsh maritime conditions, container yards, and terminals</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>A new era in supply chain security</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">Bonser notes that the device will help mitigate risks such as <strong>theft, cargo damage, and contamination & contraband insertion</strong> in ocean shipping:</p>
<blockquote style="text-align: justify">"GES100CG represents a shift from passive sealing to active, connected integrity monitoring. This enables maritime, logistics, and port operators to reduce risks and guarantee the secure delivery of high-value shipments."</blockquote>
<p style="text-align: justify">The device delivers <strong>visibility and reliability benefits</strong> not only for container operators but also for <strong>freight forwarders, insurance companies, terminal operators</strong>, and <strong>customs authorities</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Active container integrity through IoT technology</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify">GES100CG exemplifies <strong>the direct integration of IoT-based security technology into container transport</strong>.</p>
<p><br>
While traditional seals provide only "opened/not opened" status, this system delivers <strong>real-time monitoring, instant breach detection, and data reporting capabilities</strong>, enabling an <strong>active security model</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This solution was developed to meet growing demands for <strong>cargo security, insurance, and customs compliance</strong> in global supply chains.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to experts, <strong>smart seal technologies</strong> are becoming a <strong>new standard</strong> in security management, particularly on <strong>high-risk routes (such as the Middle East, Africa, and Southeast Asia)</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Conclusion: A digital revolution in container security</strong></h5>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Queclink GES100CG</strong> is viewed as a symbol of the transition from passive seal systems to digital and connected security.</p>
<p><br>
This innovation delivers numerous benefits including <strong>enhanced high-value cargo protection</strong>, <strong>transparency in customs processes</strong>, and <strong>reduced insurance costs</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Through this product, Queclink aims to <strong>redefine the future of maritime and intermodal transport through digital security solutions</strong>.</p>
