<p>Kinaxis has taken a pioneering step in supply chain management by introducing the industry's first Supply Chain Orchestration (SCO) Maturity Model and Self-Assessment tool to the market. This innovative tool enables businesses to evaluate their supply chain operations across 14 core dimensions and develop actionable strategies for improvement.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Designed to address common challenges in today's rapidly evolving business environment—such as improving decision-making processes, fostering collaboration, and enabling flexible planning and execution—the model allows companies to conduct a comprehensive assessment across operational, tactical, and strategic planning and execution areas.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>Andrew Bell, Chief Product Officer at Kinaxis, commented: "We are excited to launch the Supply Chain Maturity Model and Self-Assessment. This tool enables supply chain leaders to immediately diagnose their strengths and weaknesses and develop an actionable plan to support their progress."</p>
<p><br></p>
<p>The model identifies areas requiring improvement and provides a customized roadmap to close gaps, align with industry best practices, and guide companies toward building stronger supply chains. Key features of the tool include:</p>
<ul>
  <li><p><strong>Comprehensive Assessment:</strong> Evaluates the company's orchestration capabilities across 14 dimensions in operational, tactical, and strategic planning and execution areas.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>Actionable Insights:</strong> Offers customized recommendations to close gaps, align with industry best practices, and strengthen the company's supply chain.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>Internal Alignment:</strong> Promotes alignment among key stakeholders regarding priorities and investments.</p></li>
  <li><p><strong>Path to Sustainable Operational Excellence:</strong> Designed to help companies build a resilient, autonomous, and continuously improving supply chain that is prepared for future challenges.</p></li>
</ul>
<p><br></p>
<p>Kinaxis believes this model will enhance internal alignment among companies, helping key stakeholders validate priorities and investments. Additionally, it promotes continuous innovation and improvement toward the goal of building an AI-enabled, autonomous, anti-fragile, and sustainable supply chain.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h5><strong>Key Takeaways:</strong></h5>
<ul>
  <li><p>Kinaxis introduced the industry's first SCO Maturity Model and Self-Assessment tool.</p></li>
  <li><p>The tool evaluates supply chain operations across 14 dimensions.</p></li>
  <li><p>It provides customized strategies for improvement to businesses.</p></li>
  <li><p>The model focuses on decision-making, collaboration, and flexible planning.</p></li>
  <li><p>Andrew Bell noted that the tool enables supply chain leaders to identify their strengths and weaknesses.</p></li>
  <li><p>The tool aims to help companies create sustainable and resilient supply chains.</p></li>
  <li><p>Kinaxis's new tool supports the creation of AI-enabled, autonomous, and anti-fragile supply chains.</p></li>
</ul>
