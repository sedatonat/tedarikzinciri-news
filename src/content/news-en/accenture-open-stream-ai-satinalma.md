<p>Accenture has completed its acquisition of Open Stream Holdings. The Japan-based Open Stream helps customers work in a data-driven manner and redesign their businesses using advanced digital technologies. With this acquisition, Accenture will strengthen its capability to support system and application modernization by embracing cloud, data, artificial intelligence, and IoT technologies.
