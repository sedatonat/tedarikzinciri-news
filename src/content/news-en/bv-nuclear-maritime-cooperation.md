<p style="text-align: justify">Bureau Veritas (BV) has taken a significant step toward <strong>decarbonizing the maritime sector through nuclear technologies</strong>, a critical goal in Europe's energy transition. During the <strong>World Nuclear Exhibition</strong> in Paris, BV formally signed the "<strong>Declaration of Cooperation for Accelerating Nuclear for Maritime Applications</strong>" alongside <strong>more than 30 companies</strong> comprised of nuclear and maritime actors from France and other European countries.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">This declaration supports a Europe-based initiative by bringing together the nuclear, maritime, financial, regulatory, and research communities within a common framework aimed at <strong>developing safe, sustainable, and low-carbon maritime ecosystems</strong>.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Global objective: Carbon-free maritime with nuclear</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The declaration acknowledges the maritime sector's critical role in achieving global climate targets. Today, much of international shipping remains dependent on oil derivatives. In line with the IMO's zero-carbon goals, the sector must rapidly transition to low-carbon energy systems.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">In this context, <strong>Small Modular Reactors (SMR)</strong> technologies are emerging as potential solutions for future maritime and port energy infrastructure that are:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">safe,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">continuous,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">high energy-dense,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">carbon-neutral.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">The declaration emphasizes that SMRs represent a potential "key technology" for both vessels and coastal energy systems.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Alignment with France 2030 support package</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The declaration aligns with France's <strong>France 2030</strong> Investment Plan. This plan envisages <strong>1 billion euros in financing</strong> for innovative nuclear projects, including SMR technologies. BV and other signatories argue that these investments will increase the feasibility of nuclear energy applications in maritime across Europe.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Addressing technological, regulatory, and societal barriers</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">According to the declaration, signatory organizations will enhance mutual cooperation to address the fundamental barriers to nuclear energy use in maritime. These include:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>regulatory uncertainties</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>international safety standards</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>fuel cycle management</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>public confidence</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>certification models</strong>,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>pilot project selection and validation</strong>.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">BV will serve as a "key safety authority" in SMR projects, leveraging its expertise in maritime classification and safety certification.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Transparent communication and public trust emphasis</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">The declaration states that <strong>public confidence is essential</strong> for the adoption of nuclear technologies in maritime. Accordingly, signatories commit to prioritizing open and transparent communication on nuclear safety.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Call to Europe: Building a shared nuclear-maritime ecosystem</strong></h4>
<p style="text-align: justify">Participants call on European governments and regulators to take action on:</p>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Establishing a <strong>nuclear-maritime innovation ecosystem</strong> at the European level,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Strengthening international cooperation with IAEA and IMO,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Providing access to <strong>climate finance</strong> for nuclear projects,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Harmonizing</strong> safety regulations,</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Clarifying long-term strategic frameworks for nuclear technologies.</p></li>
</ul>
<p style="text-align: justify">Progress will be assessed annually and reported in alignment with global energy and climate objectives.</p>
<p><br></p>
<h4 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Points:</strong></h4>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Bureau Veritas signed the nuclear maritime declaration with 30+ European companies.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The declaration's focus: safe, sustainable, and low-carbon nuclear maritime.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>SMR technologies</strong> are emerging as the future energy solution.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">The initiative aligns with the <strong>France 2030</strong> plan's 1 billion euro nuclear support.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Technological, regulatory, financial, and societal barriers will be addressed through joint efforts.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">A call was made to European governments for regulatory clarity and a shared ecosystem.</p></li>
</ul>
