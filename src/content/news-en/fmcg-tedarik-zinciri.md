<p style="text-align: justify">Manufacturers operating in the fast-moving consumer goods sector have been forced to redefine supply chain management in recent years due to mounting global uncertainties. Extreme weather events, sudden tariff changes, labor shortages, and tightening packaging regulations are creating pressure across every stage of the <strong>Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG)</strong> value chain. This multifaceted risk environment is directly impacting not only operational continuity, but also cost structures, service levels, and sustainability goals.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">A December 2025 study prepared by <strong>Dassault Systèmes</strong> provides a detailed examination of how FMCG manufacturers are positioning themselves in this complex landscape. The research demonstrates that traditional planning approaches are proving insufficient, with end-to-end <strong>value chain planning</strong> focused, digitally-enabled, and scenario-based models emerging as the preferred approach.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Multi-sourcing</strong> strategies are standing out as a core tool for reducing vulnerability in supply chains. Structures dependent on a single region or supplier create significant risk in the face of geopolitical tensions and trade restrictions. FMCG leaders are building alternative supply networks to both enhance supply security and respond more quickly to sudden demand fluctuations.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Another key focus of the study centers on <strong>AI-driven forecasting</strong> approaches. Artificial intelligence-powered forecasting models do not merely produce projections based on historical data; they also account for external factors such as weather conditions, regulatory changes, and shifts in consumer behavior. This enables FMCG manufacturers to make more accurate demand forecasts and optimize inventory levels.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Among the technologies at the heart of this transformation, the <strong>DELMIA</strong> and <strong>DELMIA Virtual Twin</strong> approach stands out. Digital twin technology enables the entire value chain from manufacturing to distribution to be modeled in a digital environment. This allows companies to test new product reformulations, packaging changes, and capacity scenarios before implementing them in the real world. The use of <strong>virtual simulation</strong> supports the goal of "right-first-time execution," reducing the costs of defective production and rework.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The study also emphasizes that <strong>cost, service, and sustainability</strong> goals can no longer be addressed independently. FMCG manufacturers are striving to maintain service levels under cost pressure while simultaneously fulfilling environmental obligations. This three-way balance appears unsustainable without digital decision support systems and integrated data infrastructure.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">Finally, the concept of a <strong>secure digital ecosystem</strong> emerges as critical. Disconnects between people, data, and decision mechanisms are lengthening reaction times during crises. Integrated digital platforms enhance cross-functional collaboration, narrow the gap between planning and execution, and raise agility levels.</p>
<p><br></p>
<p style="text-align: justify">The overall picture demonstrates that resilience in FMCG supply chains can no longer be achieved through a single measure; instead, it requires a holistic approach in which technology, organization, and strategy are addressed together.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify"><br></p>
<h3 style="text-align: justify"><strong>Key Takeaways</strong></h3>
<ul>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">FMCG supply chains face pressure from extreme weather events, tariffs, and labor challenges.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Value chain planning</strong> provides end-to-end visibility and scenario analysis.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Multi-sourcing</strong> strategies enhance supply security and flexibility.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>AI-driven forecasting</strong> strengthens demand forecast accuracy and inventory optimization.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify"><strong>Virtual Twin</strong> and virtual simulation support error-free execution and rapid innovation.</p></li>
  <li style="text-align: justify"><p style="text-align: justify">Integrated and secure digital ecosystems enable fast decision-making and agility.</p></li>
</ul>
